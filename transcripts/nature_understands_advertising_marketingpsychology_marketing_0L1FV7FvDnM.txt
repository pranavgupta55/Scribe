That actually, other than surviving, the most important thing you can do is attract a mate of equivalent or higher genetic fitness to yourself. And so proxies are used to actually determine precisely who's desirable. The peacock's tail is, of course, the most extreme manifestation. Female peacocks or peahens more or less have a heuristic, which is, when in doubt, you go for the one with the larger tail. And so nature has a very large advertising budget. In fact, more interestingly, by a process called ex-adaption, much of the progress that took place in evolution is believed to be advertising-led.
