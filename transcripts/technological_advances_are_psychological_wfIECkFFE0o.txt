A simple point to make, which is just worth making before I say anything else, which is that psychology is technology. Although we think of them as completely separate things, all technological advance comes from the understanding of the property of something, whether it be silicon or stainless steel or nitrogen or whatever. It comes from understanding of property. As you get better at understanding these properties, technology gets better. And the properties of human behaviour and the human brain should not be excluded from that quest.
