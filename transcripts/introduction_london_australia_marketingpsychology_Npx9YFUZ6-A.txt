My name is Rory Sutherland. I'm the vice chairman of Ogilvy & Mather in the UK and we're here based in sea containers on the South Bank in London. I also write a weekly column for The Spectator called The WikiMan. My job is to persuade people in all walks of life that quite often the best way to solve a problem is by changing the way people think rather than changing physical reality. My greatest life lesson is really that whatever you do in life, try and be good at two things that are perhaps interconnected.
