# Sales Process Structure

> Salesperson has massive advantage knowing how conversation should flow since they've done it hundreds of times

## Key Takeaway
- Rep: 100+ sales conversations (1 flow)
- Prospect: only had conversation once (doesn't know how it goes)
- Lean into this asymmetric advantage

## Connections
- Better process design reduces required skill level and produces consistent results. _(connects to: Sales Training Process)_
- Simplified sales scripts unlock junior talent when consultative processes require specialized expertise; script mastery compounds through repetition. _(connects to: Sales Process Design)_
- Salesperson advantage from knowing conversation flow develops through reps; teaching concrete behaviors beats abstract qualities. _(connects to: Sales Training And Behavioral Language)_
- Sales process is the constraint; must prioritize closing rate over top-of-funnel volume, leveraging conversation flow advantage. _(connects to: Sales Process Optimization)_
