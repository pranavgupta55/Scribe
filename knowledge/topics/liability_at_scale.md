# Liability at Scale

> Founder becomes liable for outcomes they don't directly control

## Framework
- You sell the offer, but partners/subcontractors deliver it
- If partners fail, customers blame and sue you, not them
- You're liable for promises you can't guarantee across 50 partners

## Key Takeaway
- This liability issue prevents scaling beyond certain point

## Claims
- Done-for-you at scale becomes unmanageable because you can't control the 50+ partners' outputs, quality, and delivery—you're liable for promises you didn't fulfill — [the_real_problem_with_done_for_you_businesses_iL5v-37Vm48.txt]
