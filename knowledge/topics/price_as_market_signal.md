# Price as Market Signal

> Pricing communicates to the market what you're about; people use price as a proxy for quality and fit.

## Key Takeaway
- High price signals: this is for people who value quality, made by noteworthy people, fast, easy, guaranteed
- Low price signals: this is commodity, interchangeable with competitor, cost-focused
- People will interpret high price as high value (it's a good enough rule of thumb in general)

## Examples
- Rich people shop price-high-to-low (most expensive first), looking for best quality
- They don't want to save money; they want better stuff, higher quality, skip the line, faster
- They pay premium for noteworthy creators, better ingredients, easier process
