# Leader as Standard Holder

> The highest job in a company is holding standards; people like Jobs and Elon matter because they refuse mediocrity.

## Framework
- Every role should be run by person with highest standards
- You can tell how good entrepreneur will be by standards they keep
- They push back: why can't it be faster, easier, better

## Why It Works
- Standard-setting compounds over time
- 1% better standard leads to exponentially better outcomes
- Removes most variability and mediocrity from organization
