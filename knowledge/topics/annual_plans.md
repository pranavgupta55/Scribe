# Annual Plans

> Annual plans have dramatically lower churn than monthly; people decide once rather than 12 times.

## Key Numbers
- 16-17% discount standard (buy 10 months, get 2 free)
- Annual buyers churn less due to: lower rate, more commitment, more qualified, fewer decision points

## How-To / Method
- Implement annual option immediately
- Announce as new offering
- Expect meaningful % of customers to upgrade
