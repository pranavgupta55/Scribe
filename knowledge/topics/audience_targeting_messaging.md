# Audience Targeting & Messaging

> Define your ideal customer precisely; narrow topics; avoid reading just the positive comments—track behavior changes and conversions instead.

## Method
- Define ideal customer (business owners, age range, income level, etc.)
- Focus all content on solving problems for that specific person
- Expect to lose audiences who want different content; that's okay if you gain more ideal customers
- Don't follow comments; follow conversion metrics

## Example
- Alex's ideal = business owners who think about making money; avoid lifestyle, college, food, relationships content even if it gets views
- A successful mutual friend consuming content for years suddenly stopped—comments alone missed this insight
- Relationship content attracts people wanting relationship advice, not business owners; audience segmentation is real

## Common Mistakes
- Trying to serve everyone instead of your ideal customer
- Following comments instead of tracking actual conversions
- Expanding into adjacent topics that dilute brand focus
