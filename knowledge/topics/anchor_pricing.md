# Anchor Pricing

> 10x upsell anchors the entire offer and improves base conversions.

## Framework
- Upsell: 5-10x base price
- Don't worry if most don't buy it
- It helps sell base offer at higher rate

## Key Takeaway
- Expect 1 in 5 to 1 in 10 to say yes to premium
