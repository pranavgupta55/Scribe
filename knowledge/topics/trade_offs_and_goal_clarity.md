# Trade-offs and Goal Clarity

> Regret comes from imagining upsides without their costs; growth requires sacrificing something concrete.

## Framework
- Regrets come when we imagine the upside we missed without accounting for the downside we avoided by not pursuing it
- Trade-offs are not right or wrong, just preferences; satisfying life requires either wanting less or trading more
- Founders have ~4-5 big productive seasons remaining in their 30-40 year career window—cannot pursue unlimited paths

## Implications
- If you want $100M without more work, you must sacrifice short-term profit to hire premium talent (who are expensive)
- If you want both $100M revenue and current work hours, you will experience unsatisfaction; one preference must dominate
- Opportunity cost: choosing one path means surrendering skills and value created from other paths, even if those paths seemed valuable
