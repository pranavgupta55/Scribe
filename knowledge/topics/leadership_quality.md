# Leadership Quality

> Store culture is dictated by the leader's standard for acceptable effort—declining store has a leadership problem, not a market problem.

## Key Takeaway
- Two stores same market geography; one thriving (owner there 5 days/week), one dying (owner 1 day/week)
- Conclusion: owner quality > market conditions

## Examples
- Original location: 36 years strong until key man left
- Beach location: newer (5 years), owner-led, growing fast
