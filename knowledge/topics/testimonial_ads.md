# Testimonial Ads

> Real user voices and results are one of the highest-converting formats because they overcome skepticism through proof.

## Examples
- Podcast-style clipped from recording sessions
- Parade of proof: multiple quick testimonials stacked
- Raw iPhone testimonials with minimal production
- Walk-n-talk rants showing results at actual location
- Lifecycle ads showing customer journey
- Group testimonials with many people in frame

- Testimonial ad variations: user-generated content, founder direct-to-camera, podcast style, raw iPhone testimonials, walk-n-talk rants, parade of proof group testimonials, lifecycle ads, influencer collabs — [100m_playbook_goated_ads.txt]
