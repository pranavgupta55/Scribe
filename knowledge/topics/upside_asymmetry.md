# Upside Asymmetry

> All upside in life is on the other side of uncertainty; the gap between downside and upside is where arbitrage lives.

## Framework
- Stock can only go to zero but infinitely high
- Buying at $10 means max loss is $10 but could become $1,000
- All of upside in your life is on other side of uncertainty and delay

## Why It Works
- Arbitrage lives in uncertain and delayed
- Everyone else uncertain too but few take the bet
- Those who do get outsized returns
