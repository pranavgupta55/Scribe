# Separation of Feeling and Action

> Core maturity skill; breaking link between emotion and behavior; you can feel angry, sad, or scared while acting normally

## Key Takeaway
- Just because you feel bad doesn't mean you act bad
- Goal is to return to baseline behavior as fast as possible

## How-To / Method
- Recognize feeling without acting on it
- Behave as you would normally despite emotional state
- Over time, behavior change becomes smaller until imperceptible

## Common Mistakes
- Confusing this with numbing emotions; you still feel them, just don't act them out
- Thinking this invalidates feelings; it separates feelings from behavior only
