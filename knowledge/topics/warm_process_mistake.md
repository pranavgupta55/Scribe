# Warm Process Mistake

> Can't use low-friction warm process on cold traffic; must structure for strangers

## Key Takeaway
- Warm process on cold leads = won't sell (only sell warm leads, the easy 20%)
- Cold process works on both cold AND warm leads

## Examples
- Book launch: webinar structured for people who don't know you
- People who knew you weren't offended
- 2/3 of sales from actual selling, 1/3 from email

## Claims
- Using warm process (low friction sales) on cold leads doesn't work; must use cold process on cold leads, warm process works on cold leads too — [built_a_1_6m_practice_now_it_s_my_biggest_problem_G3NUEoTJ0Ec.txt]
- Book launch webinar structured for people who don't know you; people who knew you weren't offended; 2/3 of sales came from actual selling, not just email — [built_a_1_6m_practice_now_it_s_my_biggest_problem_G3NUEoTJ0Ec.txt]

## Connections
- Warm and cold sales require different processes; applying warm to cold leads fails because cold audiences need proof. _(connects to: Warm Vs Cold Sales Process Mismatch)_
- Warm organic and cold paid traffic convert differently; funnels must restructure for stranger trust-building vs. existing relationship leverage. _(connects to: Sales Motion Changes With Cold Traffic)_
