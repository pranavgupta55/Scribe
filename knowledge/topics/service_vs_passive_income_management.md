# Service vs. Passive Income Management

> Keep passive investments (REITs, real estate funds) separate from active business; if passive generates distractions (constant monitoring), it incurs opportunity cost.

## Key Takeaway
- Active and passive income should be kept apart; only passive income you can hands-off is worth the distraction trade-off

## How-To / Method
- If real estate or passive investments require constant monitoring, decision-making, or tinkering, convert to REITs/funds with good partners who manage it
- If passive investment improves returns by 5% but eats 20 hours per month, abandon it—active business will generate better returns with that time

## Caveats
- Passive investing should not change daily behavior; if founder checks real estate deals daily, it's become a distraction
