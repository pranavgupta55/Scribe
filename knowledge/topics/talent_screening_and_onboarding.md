# Talent Screening and Onboarding

> Multi-stage funnel screens for coachability and discipline before hire

## How-To / Method
- Pre-interview: send script, request 60-second video of candidate
- Group call: drill script, give feedback, observe how they take it
- Filter aggressively: only advance candidates who are coachable and prepared

## Key Numbers
- From 10 candidates, select 2-3 for job offer
- High attrition (equal hiring and leaving) means screening is too loose
