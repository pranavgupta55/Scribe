# High-Value Customer Service

> High-value customers are less demanding per dollar; $100K is 1% of their net worth vs. $100 = 10% of poor customer's savings.

## Why It Works
- Start high to build top-down brand (Rolls Royce light easier than Honda pretending)
- Serve fewer high-value customers than many low-value ones
- High-value customers less demanding because smaller % of net worth
