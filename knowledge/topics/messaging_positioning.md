# Messaging & Positioning

> Prospects arrive without understanding what you offer, killing sales efficiency despite strong demand

## Key Takeaway
- Define exactly what you sell; prospects book calls without knowing how you execute
- This creates friction in sales because they arrive thinking "do you come into my restaurant?"

## How-To / Method
- Use a video sales letter (VSL) or qualification process before calls
- Pre-frame expectations so reps can skip 70% of discovery on the call
