# Top-Down Business Strategy

> Start high, work your way down rather than the reverse.

## Framework
- Tesla began with $250K Roadster (limited production), then Model S, then Model 3/Y (mass market)
- Each step down made the product more accessible while maintaining brand premium

## Why It Works
- Creates positive brand narrative: cheap version feels like a gift, not a demotion
- Reverse approach (cheap to expensive) feels inauthentic
