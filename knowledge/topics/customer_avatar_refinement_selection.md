# Customer Avatar Refinement & Selection

> Filter on three dimensions: demographics (who), quantifiables (business size/ability to pay), behaviors (engagement patterns); ruthlessly exclude low-value avatars

## Framework
- Demographics: age, location, background
- Quantifiables: revenue, net worth, business size
- Behaviors: which types engage, activate, and succeed

## Example
- Gym Launch: excluded personal trainers with <10 clients (value-destructive)
- Financial advisors: filter to those with demonstrated sales ability and capital

## Implications
- Some customers are profit-negative; exclude them to improve overall model ROI
- Better to serve 100 great customers than 1000 mediocre ones
