# Fulfillment Bottleneck

> Selling faster than you can deliver destroys business model

## Key Numbers
- 6 clients at $50K offer = $300K per client revenue
- Want 50 clients = $2.5M revenue potential
- But can only fulfill on small fraction of that

## Why It Works
- You're selling the dream, not the delivery
- Customers see the gap between promise and reality

## Claims
- High-ticket done-for-you business with 80% of $5M revenue and 6 clients selling $50K offers—founders want to scale to 50 clients but fulfillment and operational control becomes impossible — [the_real_problem_with_done_for_you_businesses_iL5v-37Vm48.txt]

## Connections
- Balance sales pace with delivery capacity; over-deliver first to prove concept, then systematize. _(connects to: Sales To Fulfillment Continuum)_
- Selling faster than fulfillment capacity drives cancellations; gap between promises and delivery destroys unit economics. _(connects to: Sales And Fulfillment Misalignment)_
- Freeing delivery time via hiring lets founder focus 100% on promotion; promotion is the real business lever. _(connects to: Marketing Vs Delivery Time Split)_
