# Sales Conviction

> Conviction—how much you believe in the product—is the primary driver of sales success

## Key Takeaway
- Conviction transfers through tone of voice when you truly believe
- Can train yourself into right tone OR trick yourself by building actual belief

## Framework
- Conviction is spectrum not binary
- Requires two components: trust + belief
- Natural salespeople typically show aptitude from day one

## Claims
- Top predictor of good sales is conviction; requires two elements: trust (through expertise/rapport) and personal conviction in product — [how_to_get_good_at_sales_n_6TZGmck3k.txt]
- Conviction is spectrum not binary; ask 'to what extent do I believe?' not 'do I believe?' — [how_to_get_good_at_sales_n_6TZGmck3k.txt]
- Salespeople naturally good at it typically started strong day one; short failure-at-sales cycle (first week indicates potential) — [how_to_get_good_at_sales_n_6TZGmck3k.txt]
