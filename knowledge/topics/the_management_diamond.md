# The Management Diamond

> Diagnose why people (or you) aren't executing using the 5-step diamond; most blame 'doesn't want to' when it's usually one of the first 4.

## Framework
- 1. Didn't know they needed to do it (most common)
- 2. Didn't know how to do it
- 3. Didn't know when/deadline
- 4. Something is blocking them
- 5. Didn't want to do it (rarely true; most people want to keep their job)

## How-To / Method
- Ask the question directly: 'Why didn't you do that thing?' and follow the diamond
- Apply to yourself: if not doing more work, diagnose which box is the real blocker
- Example: if customer service issues take 80% of time but cost $60K/year and profit is $400K, it's worth paying $60K to reclaim time for constraint-solving
