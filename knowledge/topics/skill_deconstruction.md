# Skill Deconstruction

> All skills must be broken down into smaller behavioral components.

## Framework
- Bucket down: business → marketing → copywriting → email subject lines
- Specific skills don't generalize; general skills (hand-eye coordination) do
- Keep chunking down until behavior is quantifiable and changeable

## Examples
- Basketball is not a skill; shooting, dribbling, passing are sub-skills
- Marketing is not a skill; you must specify copywriting, ads, email, etc.

## Claims
- Skills are chains of adapted behaviors - multiple same-condition-new-behavior instances linked together. — [how_to_outlearn_everyone_q9qBqnhdWKw.txt]
- Breaking down skills to quantifiable behavioral components is essential - ask what specific actions demonstrate mastery, not vague objectives like "get good at basketball." — [how_to_outlearn_everyone_q9qBqnhdWKw.txt]
