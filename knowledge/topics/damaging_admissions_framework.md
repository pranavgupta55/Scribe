# Damaging Admissions Framework

> Front-load all objections and deal-breakers to self-qualify leads and build credibility.

## How-To / Method
- Before selling benefits, list every reason offer won't work
- "If you want only June in Italy, won't work. If want top-shelf luxury only, won't work."
- After listing trade-offs, mention benefits; people now believe them

## Why It Works
- Wind out sails; leads self-disqualify if deal-breakers apply
- Qualified leads still want benefits despite trade-offs
- Removes "too good to be true" objection by front-loading reality
