# Pricing & Trial Structure

> Free trial without penalty removes urgency; charge upfront with rebate contingent on use

## Common Mistakes
- Free trial with credit card = no penalty, no urgency to activate
- Users sign up passively with zero intention to engage

## How-To / Method
- Charge $199 upfront
- Offer: full rebate if user sells fence 2-3 times in 10 days OR make month 2 free
- Messaging: we only charge if you don't use it, because we know you'll stick if you do

## Claims
- Free trial without penalty provides zero incentive to use the tool; users opt in but never activate because there's no cost to leaving — [i_built_an_app_and_no_one_s_using_it_-Le_gCaW_5c.txt]
