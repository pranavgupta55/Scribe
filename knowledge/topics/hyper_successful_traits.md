# Hyper Successful Traits

> Three traits: grandiose vision, crippling insecurity, impulse control.

## Framework
- Grandiose vision: inflated superiority complex (thinks they're better than everyone)
- Crippling insecurity: feels like they'll never be good enough
- Impulse control: stays focused for extended periods

## Why It Works
- Grandiose vision drives ambitious goals
- Crippling insecurity prevents complacency
- Impulse control ensures follow-through
