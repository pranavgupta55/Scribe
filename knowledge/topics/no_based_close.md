# No-Based Close

> Frame close as permission request, not assumption, for natural compliance

## Examples
- Instead: 'you're going to do this'
- Ask: 'would you mind if we...?' or 'can I ask you...'

## Why It Works
- Natural communication dynamic most influencers do instinctively
- Phrased as question, person less likely to resist
