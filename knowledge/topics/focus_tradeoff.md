# Focus Tradeoff

> Can't operate both businesses at highest level; must choose focus and risk one suffering

## Key Takeaway
- Marketing agency: less passion, more profitable, operationally scalable but leaving money on table
- Coaching: high passion, hard to scale, requires constant effort
- Can't do both well simultaneously

## Common Mistakes
- Trying to build courses while main business lacks operational systems
- Divided attention kills both businesses
