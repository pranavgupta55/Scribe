# High-Ticket Sales Pricing

> 90-95% close rate signals massive underpricing; raise price, accept lower close rate, net more revenue.

## Framework
- Setup/Airbnb service: $10K offer with 90-95% close = left 3-4X on table
- Raising to $20K: might drop to 35% close rate, but 35% × 4X = 140% of prior revenue
- Rule: if closing above 80%, you have pricing power sitting on the table

## Why It Works
- High close rate = too many yes-es = offer is too valuable at that price
- Raising price filters to only those who truly need it (higher-quality clients)
- Revenue per deal goes up more than deal count goes down
