# Pricing Tiers and Jumps

> Jump price 5x, not 50%, to create distinct market tiers.

## Framework
- Core product: $1K
- Next tier (wrong): $1.5K — buyer doesn't see differentiation
- Next tier (right): $5K or $10K — buyer sees clear upgrade

## Why It Works
- Large jumps signal premium positioning and justify service/product differences

## Claims
- People misprice by incrementing ($1K → $1.5K), but the buyer's next tier is 5K or 10K from the $1K product; that's the next rung on the ladder. — [why_you_should_charge_5x_more_not_50_more_CTJRsVA89Oc.txt]
