# Future-Casting Your Narrative

> Construct a larger story you'll tell about this hard period to sustain yourself through it.

## Method
- Screenshot your lowest moments (bank account, sleeping on gym floor) as proof for future telling
- Say to yourself: I will fucking tell this story
- When you lose everything, capture evidence that feeds into your larger narrative of comeback

## Why It Works
- A large narrative gives vision and direction during low periods
- Hard things you overcome feed into the larger story you'll someday tell
- It transforms the hard period from punishment into character development for your hero's arc
