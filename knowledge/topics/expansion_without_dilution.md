# Expansion Without Dilution

> Grow revenue by expanding your core strength, not by adding services that dilute focus.

## Method
- Step 1: Drop secondary services (Airbnb setup, management); free up 50%+ of your time
- Step 2: Use freed time to recruit 40 CPAs instead of 4
- Step 3: Assume 3/4 close rate instead of 100% (more CPAs = broader pipeline)
- Step 4: 10x lead flow + 75% close rate = 8x business

## Why It Works
- You're not inventing new products; you're selling more of what works
- All freed time goes to your highest-leverage activity (CPA recruitment and relationships)
- Business scales while ops complexity decreases
