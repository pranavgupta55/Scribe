# Dual-Sided Revenue Models

> Monetize both sides of supply/demand curve

## Framework
- Test prep: paid by student (proven)
- Career placement: paid by university (new revenue stream)
- If certification holders are high-demand (instant jobs), pre-negotiate placements at higher earning levels

## Why It Works
- Captures value from both buyer (university) and end-user (student)
- Creates competitive moat by hoarding scarce resource (qualified candidates)
