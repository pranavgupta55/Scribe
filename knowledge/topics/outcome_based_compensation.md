# Outcome-Based Compensation

> Get paid when X happens, divorcing compensation from time investment and making it purely about skills and ability to deliver outcomes.

## Examples
- Rev share and profit share arrangements
- Top 3 Google Maps rankings bonus
- Fitness coach paid if client loses 20 pounds
- Project bonuses based on finishing on time or getting ads profitable

## Why It Works
- Compensation is predicated on skills not hours
- You can achieve the same outcome in five seconds or five months and get paid the same
- Agencies charge percentage of spend; consultants get percentage of profit or revenue
