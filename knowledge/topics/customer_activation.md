# Customer Activation

> Customers get the blueprint but won't act; tie incentives and enforcement to promised outcomes

## Key Takeaway
- Biggest problem: customers won't take action even after seeing results

## Method
- Use bonuses, discounts, guarantees contingent on specific QC actions
- Enforce upfront; state facts and tell truth in contracts
- Structure initial offer to tie fast cash to required customer behaviors
