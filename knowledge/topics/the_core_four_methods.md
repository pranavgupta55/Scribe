# The Core Four Methods

> Exhaustive categorization of all lead-generation methods: combining warm/cold audiences with one-to-one/one-to-many communication

## Framework
- Warm Outreach (1-to-1 to people who know you)
- Post Content (1-to-many to people who know you)
- Cold Outreach (1-to-1 to strangers)
- Paid Ads (1-to-many to strangers)

## Key Takeaway
- All advertising falls into one of these four; mastering one funds mastery of the next; they compound together

## Claims
- The Core Four are the only ways one person can advertise: warm outreach, posting content, cold outreach, and paid ads; they are exhaustive ways to tell people about your stuff — [100m_leads.txt]
