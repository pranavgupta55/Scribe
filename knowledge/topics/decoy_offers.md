# Decoy Offers

> Advertise cheap/free basic version; present premium alternative nearby to highlight value difference

## Framework
- Advertise lesser version freely
- When leads ask, present both options side-by-side emphasizing premium
- Make contrast huge between options (remove guarantees from decoy, add all benefits to premium)

## How-To / Method
- Strip decoy to essentials; maximize premium features
- Present premium first if possible; if forced to present decoy, ask 'Are you here for free stuff or lasting results?'
- Advertise benefits not features; discount offers in four ways (% off, absolute $, free portion, total package)

## Key Numbers
- 8 out of 10 take $399 premium when presented against free option in gym example
- Discount offers perform similarly across all four communication methods

## Why It Works
- Leads want 'lasting results' more than free stuff when framed correctly
- Value perception comes from contrast, not absolute pricing

## Claims
- Decoy Offers present a basic free or discounted version alongside a premium option, getting 8 out of 10 people to take the $399 premium option in the described gym example. — [100m_money_models.txt]
