# Trust Framework

> Trust is a behavioral bet where you make yourself punishable by another person

## Framework
- Type 1: You at risk, them punishing (sharing secrets with someone who can use them against you)
- Type 2: Them at risk, you punishing (someone gives you a knife to their back and bets you won't stab)
- Type 3: You at risk, environment punishing (asking someone to pick up your kid—they could kidnap or just not show up)
- Type 4: Advice-based trust (you follow someone's advice and bad outcomes make you trust them less)

## Key Takeaway
- All trust fundamentally involves risk and punishment, regardless of whether it's secrets, word-keeping, or advice
