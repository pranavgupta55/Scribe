# Long-Term Thinking In AI

> Historical pattern: humans plus superior tech always beat inferior tech; AI is the next tech shift.

## Examples
- Stone Age to Bronze Age, Bronze to Iron, Iron to modern: superior tech wins
- Chess, Go: machines beat humans despite skepticism
- Airplane autopilot: initially resisted, now standard

## Key Takeaway
- Humans plus tools beat humans with other tools; still competing with humans
- The day you try to beat machine alone, you lose
- As long as it's humans plus AI versus humans plus inferior tools, confidence is warranted
