# Profit Share Pool Structure

> Distribute 10-20% of profits to leaders; percentage stays fixed as pie grows, aligning incentives.

## Framework
- Define profit pool at 10-15% of net profit
- Distribute among leaders; as business scales, percentage constant but dollar value increases
- Creates owner mentality: they want to bring other leaders because it expands pie

## Caveats
- For geographically constrained (multi-location local), use static %, not growth % to avoid demotivation when growth slows
