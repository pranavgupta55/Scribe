# Expectation Setting

> Retrain audience to use calendar/resources instead of founder's cell; position change as integrity-based, not cost-cutting

## How-To / Method
- Learn script: "Route to resource or next calendar slot available"
- Stop rewarding quick outreach with quick responses; train new behavior instead

## Framework
- Integrity frame: "I made a promise to help X people, but I'm breaking it. So I'm restructuring to stay in integrity."
- Flip pitch: "You get even more, just different" + 17 benefits
- "I can reinvest in things you care about that I've been falling short on"

## Why It Works
- Positions founder as moral agent, not as penny-pincher
- Makes pushback sound like questioning founder's integrity (disarms objections)
