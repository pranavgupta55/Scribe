# Service Business Reinvestment Strategy

> Reinvest profit into talent and brand; virtuous cycle of better work → reputation → higher pricing → better talent.

## Key Takeaway
- Two channels: hiring/talent development and brand spending (aspirational moments)
- Service business pricing should always increase; if not, you're not better than competitors
- Virtuous cycle: good work + scarcity = higher price = better talent = better work = higher reputation

## How-To / Method
- Over time, service providers improve and should charge more; reputation should drive price increases
- Reinvest extra cash into acquiring better talent
- Use remaining profit for big brand moments (not direct response) that build aspirational value

## Examples
- Red Bull space jump: unclear ROI, but huge word-of-mouth, increases brand perception and consumption
- Service firm upleveling: hire better lawyers/consultants; charge higher rates as quality increases
