# Warren Buffett Pricing Power

> Ability to raise prices without losing customers indicates strong brand and good business.

## Key Takeaway
- If you have pricing power (raise prices without losing business), you have very good business
- If you need prayer before 10% price increase, you have terrible business

## Example
- Strong brand: unbranded $5 t-shirt raised to $60 (12x) without losing sales
- Weak brand: would lose all sales at 10% increase
