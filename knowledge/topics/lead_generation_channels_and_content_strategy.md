# Lead Generation Channels and Content Strategy

> Local markets benefit from trust; go multi-channel with paid ads + long-term content for brand expansion

## Key Takeaway
- Local trust means you can one-call or two-conversation close; don't need complex funnels
- Content (4 long-form + shorts/week) is 2+ year play to expand geographic radius and attract premium pricing

## Examples
- Chiropractor at $2.4M, 2 months into content creation: still too early, long-term play
- Amen Clinic (national brand) gets premium pricing and national clientele due to long-term thought leadership

## Framework
- Short-term: paid ads to saturate local market (low complexity, high trust)
- Long-term: content cadence to become thought leader, expand radius, sustain growth
