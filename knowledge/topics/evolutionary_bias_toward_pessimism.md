# Evolutionary Bias Toward Pessimism

> Pessimism was a survival advantage; we still carry those genes despite living in abundance.

## Framework
- Genes that made people cautious proliferated; optimist genes died when they took unknown risks
- World has changed but genetics haven't - we still overweight downside

## Implications
- Must make conscious decisions to counteract innate pessimism bias
- Recognizing the bias is the first step to capturing upside

## Claims
- Humans underestimate upside and overestimate downside due to evolutionary survival bias - pessimistic genes proliferated because optimists who ate unknown berries died. — [humans_overestimate_the_downside_I8YZLBYYMag.txt]
