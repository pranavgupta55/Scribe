# AI Training Methodology

> Train AI like a new employee with clear definitions, samples, and iterative feedback loops.

## Framework
- Humans learn through reinforcement: outcome feedback, repeat successful patterns
- Computers recognize patterns even better than humans
- Same training mechanics work for both

## How-To / Method
- Provide 12 clear rules and 16 writing/behavior samples
- Define what good looks like explicitly before requesting output
- Remove emotional/intangible language; use observable behaviors
- Repeat feedback loops; 100 cycles take 100 minutes with AI vs. 1.5 years with humans

## Common Mistakes
- Expecting AI to succeed without training, then giving up after one bad output
- Not defining observable outputs; expecting agent to guess
