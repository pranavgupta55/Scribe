# Compounding Growth Through One Channel

> Dominate a single lead source fully before adding others; concentration enables negotiating power.

## Framework
- Currently: 4 CPAs = 60-70% of revenue = $500-750K per CPA per year in commissions
- Goal: 40 CPAs = same commission per CPA, but 10x lead flow
- Even if close rate drops from 100% to 75%, 10x lead flow = 8x business

## Why It Works
- With 40 CPAs, you can turn down deals that require secondary services
- You have negotiating power because you're not dependent on any one CPA
- Repeat customers come back (they've already bought 3-4-5 properties with you)
