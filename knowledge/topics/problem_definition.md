# Problem Definition

> Define problems operationally with evidence and business impact to diagnose correctly

## Framework
- What does X mean? (operational definition)
- How do you know? (quantitative evidence)
- Why do I care? (business impact)

## Example
- 'Messaging is off' → people book but don't understand offer
- Evidence: unqualified calls or lack of clarity on proposal
- Impact: harder sales process, lower close rate
