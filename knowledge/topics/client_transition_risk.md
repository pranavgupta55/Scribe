# Client Transition Risk

> Losing 30% of customers during transition + new marketing spend can flip profitable business to zero margin

## Framework
- Current: $1.6M revenue, 70% margin, zero acquisition cost
- Risk scenario: -30% revenue = $1.12M, new marketing costs, margins compress sharply
- Could flip from 70% to near-zero margin

## Implications
- Cannot hand off 100% of clients to new practitioner without acquisition flywheel
- Must build paid acquisition confidence first
