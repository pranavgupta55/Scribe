# Lead Generation & Marketing

> Create endless ad variations by scrambling headline types, copy angles, images, and video styles instead of hiring expensive creative agencies.

## Framework
- Headline options: duration, gender, benefit, status, curiosity, fear-based, negative, challenge
- Copy options: short, long, free offer, reason why, benefits, status, description, scarcity, urgency, implied authority
- Image options: contrast, B&W, banners, diagonals, group pics, selfies, sweat shots, memes, flashcards, scenery
- Video options: iPhone quality, subtitles, pitching, facility tour, whiteboard, testimonials, group exercise, client testimonials
- Test: Headline 1 + Image 8 + Video 16, then reshuffle and test Headline 3 + Copy 2 + Image 12; collect winners

## Key Numbers
- Test ~150 ad combinations across 100+ markets to identify ~5 home runs (winners everywhere) and ~15 triples (winners in 2/3 of markets)
- Spend $100k+ on testing to deliver proven ads to top clients; eliminates guesswork for them
