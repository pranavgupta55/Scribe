# Pricing Psychology

> Higher prices can signal quality and improve conversion.

## Examples
- Health company 50% price increase led to higher close rates
- People didn't believe it worked because it was too cheap

## Claims
- Price increases sometimes improve close rates - company raised price 50% and close rates went up — [stop_selling_from_your_own_wallet_yEmM9JygeEo.txt]

## Connections
- Both concepts relate to prices and higher. _(connects to: Premium Pricing)_
- Both concepts relate to quality. _(connects to: Price As Market Signal)_
- High pricing signals quality to wealthy buyers; poor customers see only the price tag, not ROI. _(connects to: Value Perception And Pricing Psychology)_
- Higher prices trigger virtuous cycle; signal quality to attract better customers. _(connects to: Pricing Leverage)_
- Higher price triggers cycle: margins → talent → service → reputation → demand → higher price. _(connects to: The Pricing Virtuous Cycle)_
- Niching enables 100x higher pricing for same product; higher prices signal quality and improve conversion. _(connects to: Niching)_
- First high-ticket sale breaks belief barrier; then higher prices signal quality and improve conversion. _(connects to: Upselling To High Ticket Customers)_
- Value-based pricing with high anchoring works because higher prices signal quality and improve conversion versus revealing cost math. _(connects to: Cost Plus Vs Value Based Pricing)_
