# Performance-Based Business Models

> Zero capital means you're selling commissions or split deals, not managing overhead.

## Framework
- Commission-based: get % of deal without upfront cost
- Split deal: negotiate vendor discount, sell higher, keep spread
- No ad spend = no leverage; business owner demands more of deal
