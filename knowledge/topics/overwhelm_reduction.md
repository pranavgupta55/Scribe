# Overwhelm Reduction

> Too many features cause churn; Planet Fitness won by offering only 10-15% of competitors' facilities at lower price.

## Key Takeaway
- When you use 1 of 5 things, you feel 20% value; when you use 1 of 1 thing, you feel 100% value
- More features increase likelihood of churn, not retention

## Examples
- Community deleted content, churn dropped 30% → 5% (6x LTV increase)
- Planet Fitness $10/month focused on cardio vs. $59 competitors with all amenities

## How-To / Method
- Form: 'If I delete everything, what one thing would you fight for?'
- Inverse: 'If I keep everything, which ONE thing would you not mind me removing?'
- Chart responses; most valuable things are usually outsized
