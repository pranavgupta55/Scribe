# Paid Media Arbitrage Scaling Limits

> Pure paid media e-commerce hits hard ceiling around $10-12M revenue; beyond requires brand moat, not just optimization

## Key Numbers
- $3M current run rate (4 months in)
- $10-12M ceiling from media arbitrage optimization
- Additional 2x possible with better funnel/ops (to ~$6-7M), then wall approaches

## Why It Works
- CAC rises as competitor bids increase at scale
- Margins compress as CAC eats into gross profit
- Optimization hits diminishing returns (email, upsell, retargeting only push so far)

## Implications
- Continuing past $12M without brand moat leads to direct response doom loop
- Must transition from media arbitrage to brand-driven sales before hitting ceiling
