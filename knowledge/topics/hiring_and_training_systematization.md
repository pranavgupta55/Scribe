# Hiring and Training Systematization

> Turn sales intuition into documented system for replication

## How-To / Method
- Codify all founder behaviors: what do you do when you walk into meeting?
- Script every customer interaction
- Document successful sales process step-by-step
- Train new agents on system, not on 'just do what I do'

## Why It Works
- Michael Jordan was better player than coach—can't just copy genius
- But codified behaviors can be taught and replicated
- Real Brokerage president: spent all time recruiting and training, not selling
