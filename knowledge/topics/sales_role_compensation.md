# Sales Role Compensation

> Sales roles should be commission-based and proven before joining leadership; avoid equity mistakes early.

## Key Takeaway
- Commission on sales is already a strong incentive; don't add equity on top

## Method
- Make metrics clear: e.g., need to create 5 deals per month to reach leadership table
- Measure before promoting to group with profit share

- Giving equity to a sales guy is a day-one mistake; make compensation quantifiable with concrete milestones like creating 5 deals per week before leadership table — [never_give_real_equity_do_this_instead_lqKx0GDHFX8.txt]
