# Black Box Thinking

> Stop trying to understand why things work; focus on observable inputs and outputs.

## Key Takeaway
- People rarely know why they succeed - they've been reinforced for actions
- Asking why usually gets a narrative to satisfy psychology, not truth
- Observable reality: did they buy? Did they smile? That's all that matters.

## Common Mistakes
- Obsessing over psychology and neuro-linguistic programming instead of behavior
- Believing expert explanations when the expert hasn't consciously analyzed their own actions

## Claims
- Ignore psychological narratives about why things work and focus on observable inputs and outputs - watch what successful people do, not what they say. — [how_to_outlearn_everyone_q9qBqnhdWKw.txt]
