These systems automate follow-ups, reducing human intervention and boosting conversion rates by 400% compared to waiting 42 hours, while also handling thousands of messages efficiently without significant revenue gains. Contradictions arise between the efficiency gains of AI and the necessity of human judgment in complex tasks like deal closure. While AI excels in follow-up and lead generation, businesses often fail to capitalize on existing lead databases, leading to client dissatisfaction.
