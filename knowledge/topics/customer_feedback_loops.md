# Customer Feedback Loops

> Structure cancellation feedback to identify patterns; pair with best-customer feedback to build your core retention engine.

## How-To / Method
- DM canceling customers: 'Why are you canceling?'
- Create tag sheet: price, overwhelm, not using, missing feature, not what I expected
- Review every 20 cancellations, fix top 1-2 reasons
- Ask best members: 'What made you stay? What's the #1 value?'

## Why It Works
- Head of customer support sits next to leadership for tight feedback loop
- Turns gut feeling into data-driven priorities
