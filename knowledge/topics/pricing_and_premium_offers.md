# Pricing and Premium Offers

> Guarantee-backed pricing (5-10x higher) works because competitors lack confidence to promise execution; premium pricing filters for better customers.

## Framework
- Service guarantee: "Deliver on time, on budget, satisfactory—or refund my profit" allows 20-40% price increases because competitors won't guarantee
- Customers always want cheaper, faster, guaranteed—that will never change; ignore price complaints and focus on offer (faster, reliable, guaranteed)

## How-To / Method
- Most competitors are not confident in on-time/on-budget delivery, so they won't offer teeth to a guarantee
- You can say: "I promise on-time, on-budget delivery with profit refund if I fail. Others won't, which tells you they're not confident."
- This removes price sensitivity and shifts conversation to confidence and proof of execution
