# Diminishing Returns

> Dollar-to-value ratio peaks at $150k/year; flattens beyond $650-700k/year

## Key Numbers
- Peak QoL-per-dollar ratio: ~$150k/year income
- Diminishing returns plateau: $650k-700k/year
- Potential decline: $1M+/year (stress, relationships, management burden)

## Why It Works
- Can't consume more stuff at extreme income levels
- Extra money creates coordination problems
