# Attendance Tracking & Follow-up

> Flag members with <2 workouts/week by Wednesday each week; these are cancellation-bound and need immediate, aggressive outreach to recover.

## Key Numbers
- Red-flag threshold: 2 or fewer visits per week
- No-response within 24 hours: Major escalation signal; likely to cancel within 7 days
- Impact: Early intervention saves 25-50% of would-be cancellations, translating to 33% LTV increase

## How-To / Method
- Generate attendance report every Wednesday
- Flag members <2 visits in past 7 days
- Assign to high-priority outreach: 2-3 contact attempts (text, call, in-gym pull-aside) within 24-48 hours
- Ask discovery: schedule obstacle? injury? life stress? gym dissatisfaction?
- Offer solution: free schedule adjustment, pause membership, 30-day trial with friend, etc.
