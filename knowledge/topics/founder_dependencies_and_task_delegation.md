# Founder Dependencies and Task Delegation

> Junk drawer of founder-only tasks is solved by documenting, training contractors, and managing delegation.

## Key Takeaway
- Tedious, sporadic work = contractor/VA pool (Fiverr, etc.)
- Niche tasks that only founder does = training opportunity, not permanent founder job
- Goal: trade 20 hours founder time for 2 hours managing contractor

## How-To / Method
- List all tasks only you're doing
- Assess if it's truly high-leverage or just high-context
- Document the task; hire contractor to learn it
- Do 1-2 rounds yourself while contractor watches, then flip roles

## Examples
- Editing 300 ads for launch (not ongoing) = hire 15 contractor editors + 1 QA, then scale down
- Shopify-specific fixes = hire Shopify contractor for 20 hours/month at commodity rates
- Video editing = contractor bench that turns on/off based on campaign cycles

- Shopify contractors are abundant, not expensive, and good; if you need 20 hours of specialized Shopify work per week, there's a contractor pool ready to serve you. — [helping_e_commerce_business_owners_scale_8C_6qojTA78.txt]
