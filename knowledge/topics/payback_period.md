# Payback Period

> Recover CAC within 30 days to leverage interest-free credit card financing and enable infinite growth.

## Key Numbers
- 30 days: interest-free credit card window
- Can repeat infinitely: get customer, recover cost, acquire next customer

## Framework
- Charge prices and structure payment (prepay, splits, layaway) to recover CAC by day 30
- Alternative: 90-day payback is acceptable if growing; adjust over time as scale adds overhead

## Implications
- Bootstrapped businesses must achieve 30-day payback to self-fund growth
- Investor-backed: 30-day payback attracts investor froth; shows no need for capital
