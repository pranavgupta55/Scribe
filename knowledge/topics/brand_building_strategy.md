# Brand Building Strategy

> Build defensible brand via customer belief and ambassador evangelism, not just legal patents or arbitrage edges

## Framework
- Promise → Associations → Purchase → Product Experience → Brand Reinforcement (loop)
- Virtuous cycle: strong brand, loyal customers, influencer believers → sustainable exit value

## How-To / Method
- Pick one product you genuinely believe in
- Recruit influencers who believe in it (brand ambassadors, not mercenary affiliates)
- Invest in product quality and customer experience to reinforce brand promise

## Key Takeaway
- Brand enables $500M exit; pure product + media arbitrage caps at $50M

## Why It Works
- Brand creates customer loyalty; dupes can't replicate belief and associations
- PE buyers acquire brands, not products; brand moat justifies premium valuation
