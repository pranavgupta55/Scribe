# Marketing Distraction

> Don't build products/courses when core business is supply-constrained

## Common Mistakes
- Has Tax-Free Millionaire book, Fire CPA course, content library but no marketing plan
- Courses are fundamentally hard: customers expect everything, high churn
- Pulling focus from real problem (supply capacity)

- Cannot scale course/book products because they're fundamentally hard (high churn, customer expectations, sunk effort) — [there_s_no_easy_business_NadXwJQjBiY.txt]
