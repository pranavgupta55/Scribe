# Paid Partnerships & Collaborations

> Partner with creators/people ahead of you to shortcut your learning curve; pay to compress years of learning into months.

## Method
- Identify people already successful in the space you want to enter
- Pay them to teach you (courses, consulting, mentorship)
- This compresses learning time from years to months

## Example
- Started with less than 10,000 followers and knew nothing about YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter
- Used paid partnerships to learn platform-specific skills
- Reached top 1% creator status in 24 months using this method

## Key Takeaway
- Time vs money trade-off: deploy money to save time when entering new spaces
- This method is rarely talked about but highly effective for rapid growth
