# Reach-Out Communication Systems

> Systematize but never automate member communication via Google Sheets + trainers to ensure every contact feels personal despite scale.

## How-To / Method
- Divide members into groups by trainer (e.g., 30 per trainer if 200 EFTs total)
- Assign each trainer a day/week to do their group's 14-day reach-outs
- Track wins from daily huddles in shared notes so trainer can reference client news when contacting them
- Use text/messenger (not email); respond to no-show within 24 hours with escalation protocol

## Handwritten Card Referral System
- Step 1: Divide members into A/B/C/D groups; export to Excel with addresses
- Step 2: Each trainer gets one column; divide their list into 4 weeks
- Step 3: Trainer writes card with P.S. 'Text photo of this card to a friend who should be here; their first month is free'
- Step 4: When member comes to gym, trainer asks 'Who did you send my card to?' (they forgot)
- Step 5: Trainer has member record 20-second video together on member's phone to send to prospect
- Step 6: Trainer texts video + own phone number from member's phone; follows up to close appointment
