# Lead Gen to Coaching Transition

> Shifted from pure lead gen to coaching because advisors can't close; now building hybrid model

## Key Takeaway
- Lead gen alone doesn't work if buyer can't execute
- Coaching ensures execution + stickiness

## Examples
- Gym Launch licensed creative monthly to gym owners, didn't build 100-rep army
- Gym owners already knew how to run local ads; just needed fresh creative

## Why It Works
- Separates skill-building (one-time) from consumables (ongoing)
- Creates repeatable model without massive ops team
