# Scaling Media Buying and Keywords

> Saturate keyword markets by testing, not just spending more; market has far more capacity than founders think.

## Key Takeaway
- At $20k/month spend on Google Ads, a luxury gaming table company hasn't saturated; likely capacity up to $2M/month
- Problem is wrong keywords, not budget limits; requires burning capital on bad keywords to find winners
- Shift mindset from entrepreneur (save profit) to investor (deploy capital for returns)

## How-To / Method
- See experimentation budget as investing in finding keyword money-printing machines
- Once a keyword works, it prints revenue; keyword discovery is capital expenditure to unlock recurring channels
- Test 2-3 keywords deeply; expect to burn $200k-$300k to find 6 keywords that each do $100k/month

## Common Mistakes
- Founders psychologically avoid scaling spend due to fear of waste
- Treating experimentation as loss rather than investment
- Not understanding that media buying is a skill/discipline to master
