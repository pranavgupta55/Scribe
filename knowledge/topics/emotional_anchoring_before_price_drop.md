# Emotional Anchoring Before Price Drop

> Prepare the listener psychologically before the price to ensure proper reception regardless of buyer type.

## Tactic 1
- Say before price: 'it's super expensive'
- Rich person thinks their own high number, then hears yours, usually finds it reasonable
- Poor person braces for something big, makes the anchor feel less shocking

## Tactic 2
- Write price on paper, slide it across table
- Use calculator, turn screen to show them
- Prevents you from choking on the price (nervous delivery kills the sale)
