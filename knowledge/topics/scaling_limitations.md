# Scaling Limitations

> Done-for-you model doesn't work at 50-customer scale

## Key Numbers
- 6 current customers, want 50
- At 50, only 4 customers = 70% of revenue

## Why It Works
- You lose control of delivery and quality across 50 partners
- You're liable for promises you can't guarantee
- Chasing volume prevents deep partnership with real winners
