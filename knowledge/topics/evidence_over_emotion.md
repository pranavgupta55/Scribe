# Evidence Over Emotion

> You are what you do, not what you feel - evidence determines identity, not emotion.

## Key Takeaway
- Test loyalty: would they jump if a 10 appeared? If yes, they're not loyal
- Test honesty: do they act honestly when opportunity for dishonesty exists?
- North star: give yourself proof through behavior

## Claims
- A person isn't 'honest' or 'loyal' based on how they feel - they're honest/loyal only if evidence shows they acted that way when tested. — [reflecting_on_why_i_was_wrong_about_myself_patience_self_jud_ezN8B1d1RVw.txt]
- Building traits means giving yourself evidence through repeated behavior, not waiting to feel the trait first. — [reflecting_on_why_i_was_wrong_about_myself_patience_self_jud_ezN8B1d1RVw.txt]

## Connections
- Identity is defined by actions and evidence, not feelings or self-concept. _(connects to: Identity Through Actions)_
- Both show that identity comes from observable actions, not feelings; you are what you do, and space between feeling and acting determines behavior. _(connects to: Feelings Vs Actions)_
