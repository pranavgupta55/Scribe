# Investing Path

> Deploy accumulated capital into pieces of other people's companies for diversified returns.

## Key Takeaway
- Slowest wealth path; almost always secondary to active income generation
- Best suited for established wealthy individuals with capital to deploy

## Examples
- Public stocks, real estate, private businesses, bonds

## Caveats
- Diversification decreases upside; top investors are concentrated (5-8 major bets)
- Requires long time horizon (Warren Buffett at 60 was unknown; made majority wealth 80-95)
