# AI Implementation Pitfalls

> Half-built AI fails when compared to fully optimized human processes; requires equal training time.

## Common Mistakes
- Installing half-built AI and comparing to 6-20 year optimized human process
- Expecting drag-and-drop AI sales deployment without sales motion training
- Not matching human training effort and SOPs on AI side

## How-To / Method
- Apply same methodology to training AI as was applied to training humans
- Measure AI against 6-month or 1-year human performance, not day-one human competence
