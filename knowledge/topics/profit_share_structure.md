# Profit Share Structure

> 10-20% pool split among leaders creates ownership thinking without upside concentration.

## Framework
- Allocate 10-20% of annual profit to leaders pool
- Leaders each get a slice of that pool
- Company retains 80-90% for growth and future talent

## Key Takeaway
- Aligns incentives: leaders think like owners because they see direct profit impact
- Capping mechanism: limits exposure while maintaining motivation
