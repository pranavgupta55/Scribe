# Trauma & Permanent Behavior Change

> Permanent behavior shift resulting from aversive stimulus; can be positive (traumatized into strength) or negative (traumatized into weakness)

## Framework
- Positive trauma: learned better behavior from bad event, aligned with goals
- Negative trauma: learned worse behavior from bad event, against goals
- Both require permanent behavior shift to qualify as trauma

## Key Takeaway
- Trauma is not inherently bad; trauma into strength is still trauma but serves growth
- Cannot claim trauma if behavior didn't permanently change from event
