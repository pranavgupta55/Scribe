# Subscription vs. One-Time Model

> Emergency services are easy to sell during pain; prevention services require different sales motion and lower success.

## Key Takeaway
- One-time sale during emergency: high intent, easy close
- Recurring sale during normal times: low intent, requires more selling

## Examples
- HVAC breakdowns in Vegas summer = emergency, easy to sell
- Preventive HVAC membership = low pain, hard to sell
