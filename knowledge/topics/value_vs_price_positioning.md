# Value vs Price Positioning

> Shift mindset from commodity price competitor to value provider

## Why It Works
- Clients want best return on investment, not cheapest option
- Company with 95%+ retention and global network operates completely differently from competitors
- Speed and risk management are actual value dimensions that client cares about

## Implications
- Add consulting rider at 25% discount to create additional revenue stream during contract
- Foot in door allows you to cross-sell other services and expand scope

## Claims
- Employment medical evaluations business has 95%+ customer retention and can operate in 50+ countries with distributed networks—perceived as commodity but operates completely differently — [how_to_decommoditize_a_service_business_m8m7jbuIP4U.txt]
- Add consulting rider to RFP offering: 25% discount on one-time consulting during contract term—creates foot in door for ongoing scope expansion and additional budget approvals — [how_to_decommoditize_a_service_business_m8m7jbuIP4U.txt]
