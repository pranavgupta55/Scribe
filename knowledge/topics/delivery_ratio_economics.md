# Delivery Ratio Economics

> Change group size from 1-on-1 to 1-on-4 quarterly sessions to solve supply constraints without hiring or diluting quality.

## Framework
- Supply constraint solutions: (1) raise price, (2) change ratio, (3) productize, (4) hire
- 1-on-4 ratio preserves coach X-factor while expanding capacity 4x
- Group cap: 7 couples max before losing intimate feel; anything larger requires different format (1-to-many)

## Capacity Math
- Current: 10 hours delivery per couple/year at 42 clients = 420 total hours
- New 1-on-4 model: four 90-min sessions/week × 12 weeks = 24 couples/week, 288/year = 2,880 total hours per coach
- If batching all calls one day/week (one person): four 90-min sessions + admin = 6-7 hours/day, 60+ total capacity
- If two people each do weekly sessions, 120+ capacity
- For 150-client target: need 2 people doing one day/week each, or one person doing ~2 days/week

## Why It Works
- Couples hear others' solutions, reducing need for daily 1-on-1 coach contact
- Quarterly (vs. monthly/weekly) forces clients to prioritize and build self-reliance
- Coach improves over time through repetition; slight dilution improves gradually
