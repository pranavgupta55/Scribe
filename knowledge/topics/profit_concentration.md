# Profit Concentration

> Most profit is at the high end; accept low conversion rates because the margin is large.

## Key Takeaway
- Small percentage of customers buying at 10x price = more profit than large percentage at 1x price

## Why It Works
- Math: 10 customers @ $10K > 100 customers @ $1K (same revenue, lower operational load)
