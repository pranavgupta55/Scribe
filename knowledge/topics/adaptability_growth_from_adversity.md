# Adaptability & Growth From Adversity

> Fourth component; whether you stabilize above, at, or below your original baseline after bad event; permanent behavior trajectory shift

## Framework
- High adaptability: stabilize above baseline (got better from hardship)
- Medium adaptability: stabilize at baseline (didn't let it affect you)
- Low adaptability: stabilize below baseline (permanently worse off)

## How-To / Method
- Ask: how can this bad thing serve me?
- Think like movie protagonist: what does character do to move toward happy ending?
- Let hard times strengthen, not weaken you

## Key Takeaway
- Moments of greatest growth typically follow biggest upsets
- Duration of mourning unrelated to depth of love; behave powerfully while grieving
