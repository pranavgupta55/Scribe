# Audience Assumption Errors

> Never assume viewers know who you are - introduce yourself and credibility in every video.

## Common Mistakes
- Titles like 'Alex Ramosi Guide to Haters' (assumes fame)
- Inside jokes for existing community (excludes new viewers)
- Acting like you're already friends (confuses cold audiences)

## How-To
- Introduce yourself: 'I'm Alex Ramosi, I run companies doing $250M/year'
- Explain why to listen: credentials, track record, or proof
- Fully explain all references and avoid inside jokes

## Key Takeaway
- Warm audiences (who know you) don't mind re-introductions
- Cold audiences (don't know you) need introductions
