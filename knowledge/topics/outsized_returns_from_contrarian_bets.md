# Outsized Returns from Contrarian Bets

> Outsized returns come from betting against conventional wisdom with asymmetric payoff structures.

## Framework
- A stock can only go to zero but can go infinitely high
- In business unlike baseball, a single connection can produce thousand-run outcomes
- Long-tail distribution means big winners pay for many failed experiments

## Key Numbers
- 10% chance of 100x payoff should be taken even if you're wrong 9 out of 10 times
- Peter Lynch achieved 30% compounded returns for 15+ years by understanding asymmetry
