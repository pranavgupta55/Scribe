# Close Rate Analysis and Price Optimization

> Close rate reveals optimal pricing; 80% = 3-4X increase available; 30-40% = appropriately priced; below 30% = improve sales/qualification not price.

## Framework
- 80% close rate: 3-4X price increase available (might drop to 35% but 35% × 4X = more revenue than 80% at 1X)
- 60-80% close rate: 2-3X increase available
- 50-60% close rate: 1.5-2X increase available
- 40-50% close rate: 1.25-1.5X increase available
- 30-40% close rate: appropriately priced
- Below 30% close rate: improve selling or avatar qualification, not pricing

## How-To / Method
- Track close rates; if dropping, raise price and observe if rate drops less than proportional (meaning revenue increases)
- Example: 80% close rate at $1,000 = 8 sales = $8K; drop to 35% at $4,000 = 3.5 sales = $14K; 75% more revenue

## Key Numbers
- If currently at 80%: 2-3X pricing power sitting there; might seem absurd until you model out revenue
