# Accountability Systems

> Three-tier accountability structure addresses the core reason people fail: not consistency, but actually doing it.

## Framework
- Peer-to-peer: suffer alongside someone at same stage, don't feel alone
- Alumni: one step ahead, cheering (e.g., finished 40 lbs, now toning)
- Expert: solved this 100+ times, breaks through plateaus, measures progress multiple ways

## Key Numbers
- 75% of value in weight loss was accountability, not fitness knowledge
