# Session Duration & Structure

> Run 45-minute back-to-back sessions to split peak capacity times; frame as 'more convenience' and 'better results' not as less time.

## How-To / Method
- Old: 5:00 am (60 min), 6:15 am break, 7:30 am, 9:00 am, 5:00 pm, 6:15 pm break, 7:30 pm = packed 5am & 5pm, empty 9am
- New: 5:00 am, 5:45 am, 6:30 am, 8:45 am, 4:00 pm, 4:45 pm, 5:30 pm, 6:15 pm = same payroll, 33% more capacity
- Sell as: 'We added more times so you don't fight for parking' or 'More focus, less dilly-dallying in between'
- For small facilities (≤15 cap): Use 30-minute sessions instead; capacity jumps to 24 people/hour from 16
