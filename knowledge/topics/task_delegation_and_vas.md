# Task Delegation and VAs

> Delegate one-time work to contractor benches; recurring tedious work to VAs/Fiverr; trade hours for hours

## How-To / Method
- One-time spike: spin up contractor bench (e.g., 15 editors for launch), don't hire full-time
- Recurring tedious: use Fiverr, VAs, Shopify contractors for piecemeal work
- List all regular tasks; outsource most; keep high-leverage decision-making only

## Key Numbers
- Shopify contractors: inexpensive, many high-quality, available for few hours/week
- Trade 20 hours of low-value work for 2 hours of management

## Framework
- Categorize: one-time (contractors), recurring-tedious (VAs), recurring-strategic (keep)

## Common Mistakes
- Refusing to delegate because tasks are 'niche-specific'
- Hiring full-time for temporary or sporadic work
