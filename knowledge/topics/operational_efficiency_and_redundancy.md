# Operational Efficiency and Redundancy

> Team redundancy prevents business disruption; one absent person should not halt operations; pricing bumps fund redundancy hiring.

## Framework
- If one technician absence stops all work, business is fragile; need 2-3 backups for every critical role
- Creating redundancy requires cashflow; raise prices (20-40%) first to fund additional hires

## How-To / Method
- Multi-step solution: (1) raise price to create margin, (2) use margin to hire extra person, (3) create redundancy so Tim's absence doesn't halt crew
