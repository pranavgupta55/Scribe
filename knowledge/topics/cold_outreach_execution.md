# Cold Outreach Execution

> One-to-one contact with strangers via email, phone, DM, or direct mail to generate engaged leads

## Key Numbers
- 100 cold emails/day with 3% engagement rate → 3 engaged leads/day
- Expect 20% phone pickup rate → 25% of pickups accept offer = ~5% conversion to engaged lead
- Timeline: 0 sales month 1, 2 sales month 2, growth each month until month 5+ when scaling kicks in

## How-To / Method
- Step 1: Build list—scrape with software, buy from broker, or manually build from groups
- Step 2: Personalize with 1-3 facts about prospect before pitching
- Step 3: Deliver big fast value (lead magnet or offer) in under 30 seconds
- Step 4: Automate delivery (voicemail drops, email blasts) not communication quality
- Step 5: Distribute at scale; follow up multiple times, multiple ways
- Step 6: Retry list after 3-6 months; circumstances change, timing matters

## Framework
- Three problems to solve: getting contact info, knowing what to say, compensating for low response rate with volume
- Personalization can 50% increase email response rates when written at 3rd-grade reading level

## Examples
- One VA sending 2,000 emails/day across automated software = 40 engaged leads/day for $1M+/month business
