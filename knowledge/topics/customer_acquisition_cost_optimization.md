# Customer Acquisition Cost Optimization

> Current CAC is unsustainably high ($815/attendee, 5.25% show-up rate); reduce via better offer + better creative rather than channel diversification.

## Key Numbers
- 511 registrants → 26 attendees (5.25% show-up) → 12 sales calls booked → 6 closes per 6-challenge cycle
- Cost per attendee: $815; attendee-to-sales-call conversion: 49%
- 90-95% of sales come from challenges; podcast guesting, social outreach, events = 5-10%

## Why Current Model Fails
- 5-day format dilutes teaching across low-engagement days; Day 1→Day 2 drop: 9% → 4-5% (lose 50%)
- Offer (Accelerator at $25K with unlimited Slack) signals low scarcity; unclear differentiation from $5K tier
- Best-performing content (couple's relationship insight: masculinity/femininity) exists but not repurposed

## Solution
- Test 4-hour date night event (replaces 5-day challenge) with same content compressed into one flow state
- Expect 2x attendees + higher conversion due to date-night positioning and continuous presence
- Keep CAC metric, not lead-gen metric, as north star
