# Revenue Model Transition

> If new model is mathematically superior, transition by replacing, not adding

## How-To / Method
- Confirm: higher conversion rate? Yes
- Confirm: higher lifetime gross profit? Yes
- Confirm: more people want it? Yes
- Then: keep selling licensing on front end until it surpasses done-for-you revenue and profit
- Finally: stop replacing done-for-you customers, let them attrition naturally

## Key Takeaway
- Never flip the model simultaneously—always transition by replacement, not addition
