# Audience Building

> Build attention/audience before product; this provides leverage multiplier for any future launch

## Key Numbers
- Pre-existing audience can enable $100M+ product launch
- Audience built with no sales track record still has high credibility if grown through consistent effort

## How-To / Method
- Document effort: show 6 months of prep work for first competition, volume of learning visible
- Epic proof vs epic effort: either show results or show hustle; people follow both
- Build waitlist: people paying with time now (waiting) are more likely to pay with money later

## Why It Works
- Attention = leverage; audience enables 10x outcome on any product or service
- People believe in process and effort, not just results
