# Referral-Based Sales

Referral-based sales leverage pre-sold business opportunities from franchise owners, enabling rapid customer acquisition without additional ad spending. Franchise referrals generate significant results through strategic partnerships, as evidenced by the example of over 26 referrals adding 30,000 monthly to an agency. The system emphasizes minimal effort, with franchise owners already predisposed to refer, and relies on the reputation manager to generate reviews and referrals from active customers. However, some sources note the system’s ineffectiveness in generating reviews and referrals, creating tension with claims of high success rates. Strategic targeting of franchise owners with identical businesses in different markets and leveraging AI-generated ads (via GBT) further enhance this model. Cold outreach is ineffective, underscoring the importance of pre-sold relationships, while compliance ensures non-offer mentions in feedback processes. The process is designed to be easy, as franchise owners are pre-sold and already aligned with the business’s goals.
