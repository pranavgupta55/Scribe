# Corporate Client Leverage

> Corporate buyers can be reframed to justify premium pricing by quantifying convenience value vs buyer's time cost

## How-To / Method
- Calculate buyer's hourly rate ($150k salary = $75/hour)
- Add switching costs: time on calls, reminders, coordination across 50 vendors
- Frame premium price as time savings: cost to buyer if they manage coordination internally

## Framework
- Pitch: 'You make $75/hour. Each vendor coordination costs you that. Our platform removes all that waste. Net ROI is lower price than self-managing vendors.'

## Why It Works
- Reframes conversation from balloon cost to business cost of operations
- Justifies premium through time arbitrage logic
