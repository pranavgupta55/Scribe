# Pricing Strategy & Value Stacking

> Higher prices filter better customers; discounting to compete erodes profitability without reducing churn; $100k+ offers exist in local markets of $2.5M coaching businesses

## Key Numbers
- Real estate coaching: $15-25k done-with-you vs $100k turnkey
- 9% of Americans have $1M net worth; local market of 2k+ people has 180+ qualified buyers
- Can close 10-15 mixed-tier customers from 100-person room at high ticket

## Common Mistakes
- Assuming small sample = market capacity; 200 people from 2000 shows market limits, not market inability
- Reducing price to compete when competitor undercuts; zero churn improvement, full revenue erosion

## Key Takeaway
- Price increase filters to better customers who value and consume offerings
- Price decrease does not improve retention if psychological threshold already crossed ($3k vs $2.5k feels same)
