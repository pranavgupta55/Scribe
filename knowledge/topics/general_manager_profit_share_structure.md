# General Manager Profit Share Structure

> Structure incentivizes location performance and founder departure

## Framework
- Base salary + profit share
- 5% base, +1% per year to cap at 10%
- KPI-based milestones

## Benchmarks
- On-target earnings (OTE) range for privately held manager:
- 75K if underperforming
- 100K on-target
- 120-130K if crushing
