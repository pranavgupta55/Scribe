# Character and Vision in Team Building

> Founders attract talent through consistency, vision, and character; being a hothead or unreliable repels top performers

## Key Takeaway
- Talent gravitates toward founders who don't blow up on bad days, maintain consistency, have clear vision
- Small business founders can't compete on salary; must compete on character and growth opportunity

## Framework
- Master: not being a hothead, staying consistent through challenges, maintaining calm
- Vision: big enough that people believe their aspirations fit, not just a job

## Implications
- Getting out of your way (letting talent do their job) is as important as finding talent
- Founder personal development is business development; character attracts team quality
