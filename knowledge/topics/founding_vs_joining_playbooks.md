# Founding vs Joining Playbooks

> Join scaling company (15-100 headcount) before starting own thing

## Framework
- Spend 3-4 years at company going from $10M to $100M+
- Learn what scaling looks like when done right
- Then peel off with acquired skills and patterns

## Why It Works
- Wildly undervalued to get that scaling education while someone else takes the risk
- If joining $200K bottleneck, you're learning by fixing problems, not scaling

## Claims
- Better path: join $10M+ company for 3-4 years to learn how scaling works right, then peel off with skills, instead of joining $200K bottleneck — [should_i_take_5_to_help_grow_his_business_LGbS0GOZBNE.txt]

## Connections
- Swapping sketchy partners for legitimate ones before scaling beats dependency trap; joining scaling company teaches playbook. _(connects to: Founder Dependency)_
