# Content at Scale vs. Capturing Reality

> Create volume by capturing existing business activity, not by sitting in front of camera making stuff up.

## Framework
- Active recording: sit down for 50 pieces = nothing but camera time = miserable
- Capturing: do what you love (Q&As, calls, events), transcribe, extract interesting moments via AI, share

## Examples
- Speaker does Q&As with business owners, loves it, captures all → infinite content
- Takes calls from million-dollar+ community members, documents → proof + content
- Holds events, if calendar blank, does office Q&As → proof in real time
