# Observational Learning

> Observe differences between winners and others through a detached, scientific lens.

## Method
- Watch top 1% first, identify what they do (not what they say)
- Ask: what specific micro-steps do they do differently?
- List actions: hand position, tone, eye contact, posture, timing

## Why It Works
- You lack complete context of all micro-steps without observing details
- Comparing good vs. bad examples reveals actionable differences
- An autistic, emotionless observation lens cuts through narrative clutter
