# Engaged Leads Definition

> Distinction between raw leads (contact info) and engaged leads (those showing interest in your specific offer)

## Key Takeaway
- Leads alone don't matter; you need engaged leads—people who show interest in your solution
- Raw leads from lists or databases are cheap but unqualified until they demonstrate interest

## Framework
- Engaged leads come from warm/cold audiences and public/private communication (2x2 matrix = Core Four)

## Claims
- Engaged leads are people who show interest in your stuff through responding to or acting on your offers; regular leads without engagement don't convert to customers — [100m_leads.txt]
