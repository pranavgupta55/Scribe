# Proof and Credibility

> Proof options: either achievement or effort-as-proof

## Framework
- Option A: Achievement (notable past wins)
- Option B: Effort (e.g., 2,000 videos on TikTok = you've learned something valuable)
- 11 ways to show proof without looking like a newbie (Leads book, page 61)
