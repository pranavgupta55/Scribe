# The Swamp (1-3M Revenue Range)

> The hardest scaling stage: you own 50% your income, bet 50% profit on one hire, both options terrible

## Key Numbers
- $1-3M revenue range is the swamp
- $250K revenue = $62.5K profit at 25% margins
- Staying at current level or investing to grow both suck equally
