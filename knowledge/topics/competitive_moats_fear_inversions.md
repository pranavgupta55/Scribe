# Competitive Moats & Fear Inversions

> Competitor copying unprofitable model is actually beneficial; focus on profitable offer.

## Key Takeaway
- Competitor will copy the broken model, not the profitable one
- Founder hopes competitor scales single-day tours; it will drain their cash
- Never go out of business if you focus on customer

## Examples
- Past gym launch: founder's competitor offered cheaper one-on-one coaching
- Founder cut own prices to compete; lost $6-7M annually in profit
- Business never recovered; taught $50M lesson about not competing on price

## Connections
- Brand is only real defense against commoditization; creates pricing power and talent attraction; competitor copying unprofitable models actually helps. _(connects to: Brand Building And Competitive Moats)_
