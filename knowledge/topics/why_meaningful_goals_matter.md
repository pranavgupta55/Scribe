# Why Meaningful Goals Matter

> Big enough goals justify suffering; meaningfulness is determined by what you're willing to sacrifice.

## Framework
- Love measured by what you're willing to give up to maintain it
- Man who loves goal will walk further than man who loves journey
- Man who walks to protect family will walk until competitor dies

## Examples
- Warriors fight for freedom, duty, protecting loved ones, not for abstract love
- People crawl through broken glass with smile if protecting family

## Key Takeaway
- Goal must be bigger than you or you can't overcome obstacles bigger than you
- Viktor Frankl: big enough why overcomes almost any how
