# Irresistible Offers

> Bundle your services into a defined-end program that feels rare, exclusive, and risk-free so prospects pay $500-600 on first visit.

## Framework
- Pre-frame: Authority (social proof, scarcity of trainer availability) before prospect arrives
- Price anchor: Show $799-999 regular price so $600 challenge feels like a steal
- Splinter stack: List all bonuses separately (meal plan, grocery list, recipes, accountability coach, online portal, super shake guide)
- Scarcity: 'Only X spots left this week'
- Urgency: 'Program starts tomorrow'
- Crazy guarantee: 'Get your money back if you don't lose weight or don't feel we overdelivered'
- Bribe: Free month of membership if they sign up for recurring EFT today
- Downsell upsell: Charge $600 for challenge, then $49/week recurring after—framed as massive discount

## Why It Works
- Makes your offer incomparable to competitors; eliminates price-shopping behavior
- Money-back guarantee builds trust and shows confidence in delivery; <1% historically claim refunds
- Bundling raises perceived value without raising actual cost to you
