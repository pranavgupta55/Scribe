# Tolerance & Emotional Threshold

> First component of mental toughness; how long you maintain intended behavior before disruption from hardship

## Framework
- Long fuse (high tolerance): takes much to throw you off
- Short fuse (low tolerance): small inconvenience derails you (bad weather, slow Wi-Fi, critical comment)

## Common Mistakes
- Confusing tolerance with ignoring pain; tolerance is maintaining intended behavior despite discomfort
- Thinking tolerance is fixed; it is trainable through practice
