# Personal Growth Philosophy

> Committing to not stopping is the only guarantee; growth comes from stretching between who you are and who must become.

## Framework
- Pain of growth is stretch between current and required self
- Can't have benefits of growth without accepting suffering cost
- Commitment to continue matters more than success timeline

## Examples
- Slept on gym floor 4-5 hours nightly with athlete's foot from LA Fitness for 6 months
- Worked under parking garage with cars waking me, no AC
- Opened gym 4:15am daily for 5am sessions; extreme conditions but committed

## Key Takeaway
- Every successful person shares hard path; only differentiator is not stopping
- Defining terms and seeing reality clearly separates winners from whiners
