# Group Role-Play Sales Training

> Daily team sales training rotating through five components (intro, discovery, offer, objections, looping) keeps scripts sharp and team aligned

## Framework
- Five parts: intro, discovery, offer, objections, looping
- Fifth day rotates based on team struggle or hot topic of week
- Run morning role plays as team, not one-on-one coaching

## Why It Works
- Repetition keeps team aligned on script
- Problem parts get focused practice
- Peer group reinforcement stronger than individual coaching
