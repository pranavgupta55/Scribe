# High-Ticket One-on-One Selling

> Unscalable one-on-one premium tier provides immediate cash flow, shifts belief about value, and funds aggressive growth.

## Examples
- Personal training: one client, $125/hr × 90 min × 5 days/week = $4-5k/month cash
- That cash funded gym growth without reinvesting business revenue

## Why It Works
- Working with high-value clients rewires belief about what people will pay
- Shifts entire psychology from scarcity to abundance
- Provides working capital without debt or VC

## Common Mistakes
- Believing 'selling time is what poor people do'
- Thinking you must scale before profiting
- Ignoring that everyone earns per hour (even Buffett)
