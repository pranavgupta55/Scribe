# Churn Prevention

> Monthly consumption anchors members to the community and product.

## Framework
- Cannot say 'I am communitied up' — community is consumable every month
- Adding things members don't use makes product less valuable, not more
- One thing people use + four things they don't = less valuable than one thing alone

## Implications
- Focus on making one consumable valuable rather than adding more stuff
- Consumable drives monthly touchpoint and retention

## Claims
- Membership churn is driven down when members consume something monthly, such as community interaction or fresh consumable inputs. — [your_business_needs_consumable_media_Pa1XNVGhzOI.txt]

## Connections
- Monthly consumption anchors members to community; community is recurring product people consume month-to-month. _(connects to: Community Value)_
