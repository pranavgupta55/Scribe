# Churn and Retention

> Target 80%+ annual retention (B2B); dramatically increases LTV and business value with same CAC.

## Key Numbers
- 50% annual retention: $100/year customer = ~$200 LTV
- 80% annual retention: $100/year customer = ~$500 LTV
- Same CAC, 2.5x customer value difference

## Framework
- Measure: of 100 customers on Jan 1, how many remain next Jan 1?
- Target: ≥80% (20% annual churn)

## Why It Works
- CAC is commoditized across industry; LTV is the differentiator
- High retention allows spending more on acquisition (arbitrage)
- Avoids leaky-bucket problem of constant customer replacement
