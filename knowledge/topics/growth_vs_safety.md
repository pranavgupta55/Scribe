# Growth vs. Safety

> Can't have both fast growth and zero debt in capital-intensive business; choose your trade-off consciously.

## Key Takeaway
- You want to grow faster AND avoid debt; one must give
- Slow growth without debt is valid; fast growth usually requires debt

## Implications
- If you plan to stay in business 20-30 years, slow and steady wins the race
- If debt covenants fail in a bad year, you go to zero while slow growers survive and buy assets cheap
