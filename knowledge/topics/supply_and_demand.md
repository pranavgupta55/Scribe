# Supply and Demand

> Desire only exists for things you cannot have; keeping supply below demand perpetually increases desire and price.

## Hormozi Law
- The longer you delay the ask, the bigger the ask you can make
- The longer the runway, the bigger the plane that can take off
- Keep supply below demand to maintain ravenous prospect base
- Satisfy zero demand = no money; satisfy all demand = kill golden goose

## Why It Works
- Desire is contract to be unhappy until getting what you want
- Once desire is satisfied, it disappears entirely
- Pent-up demand increases for subsequent promotions
- Scarcity increases perceived value without changing product
