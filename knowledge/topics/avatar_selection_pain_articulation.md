# Avatar Selection & Pain Articulation

> Pick right avatar first; articulate their pain better than they can; specific pain is stronger motivator than generic promise.

## How-To / Method
- Identify ideal customer avatar upfront
- Extract pain language from book reviews in your niche
- Mirror their exact language back: 'I know you're struggling with...'
- Specificity > vagueness; observable pain > abstract benefits

## Why It Works
- If you articulate problem better than they can, they believe you can solve it
- Accurate problem description = stronger persuasion than size of promise
- Pain and persuasion exist only in the specific, never the vague
