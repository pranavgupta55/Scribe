# Resilience & Recovery Time

> Third component; how long it takes to restabilize after behavior change from bad event; measures recovery speed

## Key Numbers
- High resilience: bounce back in 5 minutes
- Low resilience: funk lasts months or years

## How-To / Method
- Once you hit rock bottom, execute 180-degree turn immediately
- Return to normal behavior at full throttle
- Practice catching yourself spiraling and reversing faster each time

## Why It Works
- Bad thing only controls you as long as you allow it
- Recognizing moment enables faster recovery with repeated practice
