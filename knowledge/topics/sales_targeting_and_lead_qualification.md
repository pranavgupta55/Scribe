# Sales Targeting and Lead Qualification

> Lead scoring and qualification are critical; only qualified wealthy prospects will close on high-ticket offers; speaking to wrong avatar guarantees failure.

## Framework
- Assume everyone is rich in your pitch; design offers for wealthy customers; poor people self-select into lower tiers
- If targeting seven-figure business owners, close rate on $10K offer will be far higher than if speaking to broke people
- For every 10 prospects, expect 1 to be correct avatar (top 1%); may need to speak to 100 to find correct fit

## Common Mistakes
- Speaking to wrong avatar: if you talk to bottom 50% about $10K offer, you will get 0 yeses and mistakenly believe high-ticket doesn't work
- The reason it fails is not the price—it's that you're not speaking to people who have money
