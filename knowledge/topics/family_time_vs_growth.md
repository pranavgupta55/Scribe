# Family Time vs Growth

> Fear of losing family time is real but assumes a false dichotomy; the real trade is short-term profit vs long-term scale

## Common Mistakes
- Thinking you can scale to $100M without changes to current lifestyle
- Conflating time investment with family sacrifice

## Framework
- You can't make up family time long-term, but you can make up profit short-term
- Trade short-term profit (pay high-talent team) to preserve time later

## Implications
- If you want family time + $100M, accept lower profitability now to afford great people
