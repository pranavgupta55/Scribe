# Death Creates Clarity (Reverse Eulogy)

> Write your reverse eulogy to clarify priorities; most things you stress about are never mentioned.

## Key Takeaway
- Eulogy is 500-1,000 words covering your entire life; almost everything you stress about is excluded, almost everything you strive for isn't mentioned
- Eulogies focus on service and character, not Forbes covers or money made

## Framework
- Identify causes you want to serve and character traits needed to serve them at highest level
- Everything you do should be in service of your eulogy or obituary

## Why It Works
- Death creates clarity as forcing function for prioritization; reality check on what actually matters
