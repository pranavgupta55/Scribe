# Language as Behavior

> Words are actions that solicit responses; the response reinforces future use of the word.

## Framework
- Word/sound emitted → Response received → Reinforcement of word use
- Example: saying "hi" → others say "hi" back → more likely to say "hi" again

## Why It Works
- Behavioral reinforcement is mechanical; people don't consciously plan which words to repeat
