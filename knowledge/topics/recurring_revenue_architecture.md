# Recurring Revenue Architecture

> Build two-year+ LTV by closing renewals at in-person events with fast-action incentives (first-class flights, discounts, clear next-step positioning).

## Renewal at Events
- Highest close rate for renewals: in-person environment
- Trigger: 'You're coming to the event anyway; let's lock in next year while you're here'
- Offer first-class flight upgrades (total cost ~$6K) or $5K immediate-renewal discount

## Positioning for Renewal
- Don't apologize for quarterly cadence; frame as premium: 'Fewer meetings forces better prioritization and self-reliance'
- Couple's Insurance angle: 'Two getaways/year guaranteed. That's the marriage safety net you need.'
- New arrivals + renewals creates natural upsell moment

## Sunset Lower Tier
- Honor existing $5K commitments; deliver full value through end of term
- For renewal: 'We're discontinuing the lower tier. You can upgrade to new model at prorated price or move on.'
- Do NOT apologize; frame upgrade as better (group + events vs. calls + Slack)
