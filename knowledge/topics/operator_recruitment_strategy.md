# Operator Recruitment Strategy

> Target proven operators who can lead people; industry experience is a bonus, not the core requirement.

## How-To / Method
- LinkedIn outreach to candidates in 5-8M auto repair firms in similar ops roles
- Indeed ads as secondary channel; expect lower quality but occasional gems
- Reach out to ~500 candidates; offer relocation package if local talent scarce

## Why It Works
- Great operators can lead any business; fixing people > fixing cars
- Character and admiration matter more than domain knowledge
