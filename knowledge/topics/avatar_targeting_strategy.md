# Avatar Targeting Strategy

> Target advanced customers with problems they care about, not beginners

## Key Numbers
- Leads book (basic marketing): 2/3 attendees had no business
- Cash conversion cycles book (advanced): 65% attendees were business owners

## Framework
- Problem = avatar, so target by the problems higher-tier customers face
- Advanced barbers with money to spend have different problems than junior barbers
- Content and advertising must match the avatar's actual problems
