# Passion Myth

> Passion is not about loving what you do; it's about suffering for something meaningful worth the cost.

## Framework
- Latin 'passio' = suffering, from Passion of Christ (crucifixion)
- Bastardized into 'doing what you love' in modern culture
- Reality: success requires repeatedly doing what you don't want

## Common Mistakes
- Quitting job because you're not loving every second
- Believing following passion = enjoyable life
- Using lack of passion as excuse for not persisting

## Key Takeaway
- Suffering is required on all paths; pick path worth suffering for
- Young man quit job thinking he was doing something wrong; wasn't
