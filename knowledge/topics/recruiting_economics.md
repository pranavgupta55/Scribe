# Recruiting Economics

> Recruiting pays 20% of annual salary as commission; hunt for big salaries ($500K+), not VAs ($5K).

## Key Numbers
- $750K executive = $150K commission
- $5K VA placement = $1K commission
- 20% standard commission rate in recruiting
- Headhunting: target whales, not nets

## Why It Works
- You don't deliver the product (the person); you negotiate the deal
- No incremental cost per placement
- Scale by finding more high-paying roles and executives
