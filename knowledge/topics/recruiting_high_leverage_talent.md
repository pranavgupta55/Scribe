# Recruiting High-Leverage Talent

> Premium customers allow premium pricing, which funds premium talent recruitment; relocation packages, recruiting firms, and high starting offers overcome local talent shortages.

## Framework
- Premium customers (Yellowstone Club, celebrity properties) allow $300K+ profit margins per technician
- With high margins, founders can afford premium recruitment (recruiting firms, relocation packages, signing bonuses)

## How-To / Method
- For technical roles in limited local talent pools, run national ads offering generous relocation (e.g., $50K package over 6 months)
- Recruiting firms cost $50K+ but unlock national talent; cheaper than losing business due to understaffing
