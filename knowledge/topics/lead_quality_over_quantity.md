# Lead Quality Over Quantity

> Prioritize lead value over lead volume

## Examples
- Rather get $100 lead worth $1,000 than $10 lead worth $30
- Cost of high-quality leads may triple because there are fewer of them

## Why It Works
- The ratio between cost and value matters, not absolute spend
- Higher spend on better leads often results in lower CAC and higher profitability
