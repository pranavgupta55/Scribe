# Pricing Leverage

> Higher prices in service businesses trigger virtuous cycle (better margins, talent, customers).

## Virtuous Cycle (High Price)
- Raise price → higher gross margin → hire better talent → better service → better customers → better referrals/retention

## Vicious Cycle (Low Price)
- Lower price → lower margin → worse talent → poor service → worse customers → high churn → endless acquisition

## Action
- As volume increases, spin the pricing loop faster
- Progress = speed of pricing increases, not absolute number of customers
