# Communication Clarity

> Most communication fails because people don't define abstract terms; both parties say words neither understands.

## Key Takeaway
- Undefined terms = face noise; listeners interpret your words however they want
- No shared language = no real communication
- Most relationship and business problems stem from undefined expectations

## How-To / Method
- Define every abstract term before using it
- Make definition observable and behavioral
- Confirm both parties agree on definition

## Why It Works
- Good communicators transfer ideas efficiently by using language everyone understands
- With definition, teams collaborate; without, they attack each other
- Good communicators can observe things with their two eyes
