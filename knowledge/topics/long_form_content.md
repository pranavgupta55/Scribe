# Long-Form Content

> Structure long-form as 20 shorts sewn together on one theme; use pre-tested material (top tweets)

## Framework
- Pull top tweets from last 30-45 days
- Say them in sequence in long-form video
- Each quote is retention loop: 'wow that was good' → next quote → explanation
- Makes shorts inherently clippable
