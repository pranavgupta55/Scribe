# Strategic Spending

> Reinvest money in three buckets: tools, implementation help, and trial attempts.

## Framework
- Tools: software like CRM, landing page builder, school at $9/month
- Implementation: courses, communities, tutoring (underrated), one-on-one help
- Trial attempts: actual spending on ads, content editing, testing

## Why It Works
- Some investments are minor but give huge time leverage
- You can't reach $100K by saving alone; you must also invest
- These create asymmetric returns on your time
