# Conversion Rate Optimization

> Improve backend conversion through dialer, lead scoring, and sales messaging to increase LTV:CAC ratio.

## Framework
- More dials via parallel dialer = more conversations
- Lead scoring = only talk to best prospects
- Damaging admissions = self-qualify = higher close rate

## Key Takeaway
- Frontend (creative) and backend (sales) both drive down effective CAC
- Better creative and better sales both multiply profitability
