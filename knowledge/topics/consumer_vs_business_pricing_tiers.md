# Consumer vs. Business Pricing Tiers

> Price ranges differ dramatically between consumer and B2B; know the tier you're in.

## Consumer Pricing
- Impulse purchase: $500-600
- Higher-ticket purchase: $3,000-10,000 (typical for services)

## B2B Pricing
- Mid-tier business: $2,000-3,000/month
- Cheaper business offer: $400-800/month (e.g., $500/month considered cheap for business, super expensive for consumer)

## Why It Differs
- Businesses think ROI; individuals think absolute cost
- Same $500/month is tiny for business, massive for consumer
