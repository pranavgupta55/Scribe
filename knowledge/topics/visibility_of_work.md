# Visibility of Work

> Invisible effort is the price of real output; you won't get credit and you will get criticized

## Common Mistakes
- Expecting credit for effort (you won't get it)
- Expecting understanding (people will criticize and try to pull you away)

## Key Takeaway
- Do it for you
- Accept that work is invisible
- You get credit only for outcomes, not effort
- The work still needs doing

## Claims
- You have to do it for you because no one will see the amount of work; if you want credit for work you've already lost; you get credit for outcomes but never for effort — [the_5_30_am_parking_lot_test_SxS1vjwovEs.txt]
- You will never get credit for the work; you'll get criticized for it instead. People will say you work too much and try to pull you away. But the work needs doing. — [the_5_30_am_parking_lot_test_SxS1vjwovEs.txt]
