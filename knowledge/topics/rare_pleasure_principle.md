# Rare Pleasure Principle

> Things you love become unbearable if done daily due to hedonic adaptation; rarity preserves joy.

## Framework
- Love something once/twice yearly keeps it special
- Same thing daily causes adaptation and boredom
- Good thing done constantly becomes normalized and eventually despised

## Examples
- Love specific pizza place 1-2x/year; eat there every meal = misery
- Love meeting 10 entrepreneurs monthly; doing it daily = hate it
- Gym owner thought constant workout + food with friends = happiness; opposite happened

## Implications
- Can't build successful business around something you love doing constantly
- 95% of work will be supporting 5% passion; must accept this
