# Leadership by Example

> You cannot ask your team to do what you won't do

## Examples
- Owner says 'show up on time' but arrives late = team doesn't respect the standard
- Gandhi fasted for a month to model sobriety before telling a child to stop eating sugar = the child listened

## Key Takeaway
- Model what you want to see; it's the only way to establish credibility
