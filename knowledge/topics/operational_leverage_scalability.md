# Operational Leverage & Scalability

> Low operational complexity and low CapEx requirements enable rapid scaling without constant capital raises or dilution

## Framework
- Operational complexity: number of variables to manage for production growth
- CapEx: capital expenditure required to scale (excludes growth capital)

## How-To / Method
- Software and media scale with single unit economics
- Service chains require managing inventory, employees, leases, permitting

## Implications
- Low-CapEx businesses attract less external capital requirement, preserve founder equity
- High ROIC (return on invested capital) from reinvested cash makes business attractive to investors
