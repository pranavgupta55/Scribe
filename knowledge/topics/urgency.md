# Urgency

> Time-limited access or pricing creates action by decreasing the decision threshold; biggest sales occur in final 4 hours (50-60%).

## Four Methods
- Cohort-based: 'Start next week or wait until the following week'
- Seasonal: New Year Promo, Valentine's Special, Sexy By Spring
- Pricing/Bonus: 'This discount expires on X date'
- Exploding Opportunity: arbitrage opportunity decays with time

## Key Numbers
- Last 4 hours generate 50-60% of sales despite being only 3% of campaign time
- Fear of missing out stronger than desire for gain
- Waiting until last moment to decide is human nature, not flaw

## Caveats
- Urgency must be real; false deadlines destroy credibility
- Can combine with cohort model for ethical implementation
- Local businesses benefit most from high-frequency seasonal urgency
- Don't fear losing sales; last-minute action-takers generate most revenue
