# Power Through Say-Do

> Behavioral mechanism where proved prediction creates compliance with future requests

## Framework
- Say something → person acts → good thing happens → higher likelihood of compliance on next request
- Credibility from behavioral proof, not just claims

## Examples
- Stock picker: say 10 picks, they buy, all pop → has power for future requests
- Gym launcher: had authority because methods proved money-making across 100 gyms
