# Influence and Branding Framework (SPCL)

> Build brand through SPCL (Status, Power, Credibility, Likeness) to pre-program audience behavior.

## Framework
- Status: number of reinforcers (rewards) you control
- Power: say-do correspondence (when you say something, it works)
- Credibility: third-party proof that what you say is true
- Likeness: how similar they perceive you as being

## Example
- Trust fund kid: has status (money)
- Someone whose stock tips work: has power + credibility
- Your twin brother: has likeness
- Having all four = maximum influence

## How-To / Method
- Demonstrate status and credibility at start of videos ('I run $250M+ portfolio')
- Build power through quality of advice given
- Martha Stewart gave instructions (procedural) that worked, creating power and credibility, then sold everything

## Key Takeaway
- Branding = building history of positive reinforcement; strong brand allows you to offer with no promises: 'buy this, you'll like it'
- Many internet marketers get stuck looping: cherry-pick 10 good results from 1,000, use as testimonials, disappoint buyers, start over with new offer
