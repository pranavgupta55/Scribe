# Operational Leverage and Logistics

> Unless shipping is competitive advantage, outsource 3PL; redeploy founder and team to sales/marketing.

## Key Takeaway
- Value is in sales/distribution, not warehouse operations
- Shipping is commoditized (unless you're Amazon)
- In-house warehouse = founder/team distraction

## Why It Works
- 3PL specialists optimize logistics costs (cardboard, tape, peanuts)
- Founder should focus on high-leverage: hiring salespeople, sales training
- $5k-$7k profit per livestream show × more shows = $100k+/day revenue is better than $5k savings on logistics

## Common Mistakes
- Founder thinks logistics speed requires in-house control
- Live-selling business requires speed, but speed is people trained + logistics capability, not owner-managed warehouse

## How-To / Method
- List all logistics tasks your team does
- Calculate true cost + founder distraction + overhead
- Bid out to 2-3 3PLs
- Hire 2 more salespeople to get to 7+ shows/day (vs 1 show/day at capacity)

- For a luxury goods live-selling business ($6M/year selling designer bags and sunglasses on Whatnot with 110,000 followers, doing 5 hours/day and making $5k-$7k profit per show), the path to $38M is hiring 2 more camera salespeople and training them systematically. — [helping_e_commerce_business_owners_scale_8C_6qojTA78.txt]
