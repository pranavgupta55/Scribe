# Uncertainty as Fuel

> Certainty kills excitement; uncertainty is what makes worthwhile pursuits worthwhile

## Examples
- Rocky: 90-second montage = instant gratification; real life: years-long training with no guarantee
- Gods had certainty (immortality) so nothing mattered; mortals have chance, so everything matters

## Key Takeaway
- If you knew you'd succeed, you wouldn't bother
- Uncertainty is the ingredient that makes it worth doing
