# Decision Theory

> Sadness is perceived lack of options; fear exists only vague; play out specific costs to clarify.

## Framework
- When sad, ask: what trade am I unwilling to make?
- Always more options; deepest beliefs most invisible
- Fear only in vague not specific; play it out step-by-step

## How-To / Method
- Define terms carefully so you see reality clearly
- People getting what they want = better at making trades/bets
- Model their decision-making weights, not just behavior

## Implications
- Skill deficiency is seeing reality as you want not as it is
- Everything wanted is few trades away; trades embedded in belief system
