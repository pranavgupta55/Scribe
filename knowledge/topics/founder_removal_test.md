# Founder Removal Test

> Can business run without founder?

## Framework
- Give GM 3-6 months to run location alone
- Founder on 'vacation' doing back office and scouting
- Measure: location sustains or grows?
- If location dips, not ready to expand

## Why It Matters
- Founder thinks 'I'm out of day-to-day, business runs itself' but still working 16 hours
- True test: can location operate without any founder input?
