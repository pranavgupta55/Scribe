# Sales Process Design & Closing Mechanics

> Custom sales process: form → VSL with pricing → SMS BANT qualification → phone call with credit card ready; projected 50-60% close rate

## How-To / Method
- Post-opt-in: VSL explaining pricing ranges, materials, hooks, customization options
- VSL ends: "If you're not sure, that's why we have the call. It's an important decision."
- SMS after opt-in: BANT questions (budget, authority, need, timing)
- Call framed as buying decision, not consultation; $200 incentive to close same-day

## Key Numbers
- Expected close rate: 50-60% (vs prior 20%)
- Expected AOV increase: 2x+ (from customization and higher positioning)
- Combined impact: 4x business growth
