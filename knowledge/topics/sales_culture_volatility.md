# Sales Culture Volatility

> Feast-famine cycles indicate weak culture that celebrates big deals but doesn't sustain discipline

## Key Numbers
- $700k months crash to $200-250k
- Team bonuses of $20-30k incentivize sprint mentality, not consistency

## Common Mistakes
- Treating volatility as normal for the industry
- Not investigating culture and leadership as the root cause
