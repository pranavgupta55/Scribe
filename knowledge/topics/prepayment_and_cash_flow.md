# Prepayment and Cash Flow

> Use prepayment discounts, layaway, and ancillary benefits to pull cash forward and reduce financing risk.

## Discount Structures
- Minimum 10% for annual prepay (market return floor)
- "Buy 10 get 2 free" = 15-20% adoption rate
- Moderate discount + benefits (priority, concierge, guarantee) = 30-40% adoption

## How-To / Method
- Ask for half now, half in 30 days (not tied to delivery cycle)
- Align payments with customer paydays (salary, deposit cycles)
- Layaway: customer pays, then receives (faster payoff due to anticipation/incentive)

## Key Benefits
- Payback period acceleration (e.g., $25k deal: 50% down + 50% in 30 days)
- Zero delivery risk: they've paid before receiving
- Customer anticipation increases perceived value
- Third-party financing (e.g., Affirm) increases sales by 35%
