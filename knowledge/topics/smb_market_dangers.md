# SMB Market Dangers

> SMBs have internal volatility and operational chaos; serving them requires absorbing their dysfunction into your business.

## Key Takeaway
- SMB churn and volatility is systemic, not fixable by improving marketing
- Their problems are usually operator/sales skill issues, not marketing

## Implications
- 4-6 month average lifetime means you replace majority of revenue annually
- CAC rises as market becomes competitive
- You end up trying to fix their broken business instead of just doing marketing
