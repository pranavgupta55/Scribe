# Control Relinquishment

> The thing that made you successful (control) must be unlearned to scale

## Key Takeaway
- Quit job for control → must give up control to scale
- Many get stuck wanting to feel needed

## Common Mistakes
- Using business to fulfill personal needs for importance
- Refusing to delegate because you 'do it better'
