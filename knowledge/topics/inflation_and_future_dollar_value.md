# Inflation and Future Dollar Value

> Savings goals must account for inflation; $1M in 50 years buys what $170K buys today.

## Key Numbers
- $1 in 1975 = $6.02 today (50 years ago)
- $1M retirement goal needs $24M in future dollars
- $50K annual passive income from $1M only buys $8K stuff today

## Key Takeaway
- Traditional financial advice is 6x too conservative for inflation
- Adjust all goals upward to account for 50-year compounding
