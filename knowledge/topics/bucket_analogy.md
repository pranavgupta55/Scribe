# Bucket Analogy

> Businesses can be understood through the lens of a bucket with holes

## Key Takeaway
- A business is likened to a bucket of water, where holes represent potential problems or vulnerabilities
- The analogy emphasizes how systemic issues (like inefficiencies) can drain a business's capacity

## Why It Works
- Holes in the bucket symbolize challenges that can undermine a business's stability
- This metaphor highlights how unresolved problems (like resource gaps) can lead to gradual decline
