# Retention Benchmarks

> Platform average is 80% retention; best groups under 10% churn, weak groups below 70% retention.

## Key Numbers
- 80% monthly retention (20% churn) = School platform average
- <10% churn (>90% retention) = best performing groups
- <70% retention (>30% churn) = needs urgent focus
- If <70% retention, prioritize retention over acquisition

## Why It Works
- Small churn improvements = massive LTV multipliers (10% churn drop = 2x LTV)
- Serves as reality check on business health
