# Sales Dialer Efficiency

> Parallel dialer calling 10 numbers at once with 5% pickup enables team to maximize talk time, not waiting time.

## How-To / Method
- Dialer calls 10 numbers in parallel
- ~5% pickup rate = 2 connections across 10 dials
- System auto-routes first pickup to salesperson 1, second to salesperson 2
- No waiting between dials; constant talk time

## Key Numbers
- 300 prospects per day across all channels
- Need 6 salespeople with $300/day revenue KPI each
- Efficiency multiplies with bigger team and dialer
