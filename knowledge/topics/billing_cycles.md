# Billing Cycles

> Switch from monthly billing to 28-day or weekly billing to improve sellability, align payroll, and gain 7.7% instant profit boost.

## Key Numbers
- Monthly billing: 12 inflows per year but 13 payroll outflows (26 bi-weekly cycles) = one month of payroll out of pocket
- 28-day billing: 13 inflows per year at same gross revenue = aligns payroll; adds 7.7% margin
- Weekly billing: Most sellable ($49/week sounds cheaper than $200/month even though $49 × 4.3 = $210.70)
- Example: Raise from $118/month to $49/week ($210.70/month) → sounds cheaper but nets 64% profit increase with payment alignment

## How-To / Method
- Advertise rates as weekly ($39-49/week) for max sellability
- Settle up as 28-day cycles in backend for minimal admin work
- No freezes for absences under one month; reduces billing disputes and simplifies policy
