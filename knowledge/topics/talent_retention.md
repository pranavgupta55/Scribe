# Talent Retention

> Can't retain average talent; A players rocket up, leaving empty middle

## Key Takeaway
- Churn at lower levels is the real constraint to reaching $1B
- People say it's a hard job; need to make it easier to stay

## Common Mistakes
- No intermediate steps between entry and promotion
- When promoted, lose them to other companies or burnout
