# Content Strategy: Entertainment vs Education

> Educational content builds loyal audiences and customers; entertainment builds large but uncommitted audiences; all views are not created equal.

## Framework
- Entertainment content = entire point is to watch; succeeds if people consume
- Educational content = point is to change behavior; succeeds if person applies what they learned
- Edutainment = hybrid, but classified by dominant outcome
- Entertainment audiences want more entertainment; education audiences want more education—they don't funnel into each other

## Why It Matters
- Entertainment has larger followings because everyone gets value from entertainment
- Education has smaller followings because people already know different things; no one thing educates everyone
- Education audiences convert to customers, book buyers, email subscribers; entertainment audiences consume and leave

## Key Takeaway
- Don't follow entertainment tactics (they work for entertainment but not education)
- Choose one (education) if you want to build a profitable brand with repeat customers
- All views are not created equal; track who's actually buying
