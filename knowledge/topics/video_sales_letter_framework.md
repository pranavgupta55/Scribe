# Video Sales Letter Framework

> 5-7 minute VSL with hook, promise, proof, plan, pricing, objection pre-handling, and CTA to book call

## Framework
- Hook: "Changing railings transforms entire home look and ROI; railings cost fraction of home value added"
- Promise: "I'll show you how to choose perfect handrail for your home"
- Proof: Examples of before/after transformations from customers
- Plan: 4 steps to buying railing (materials, number, hooks, custom options)
- Pricing: Explain pricing and set expectations for delivery

## Objection Handling
- Address budget: "We have financing through Klarna, Affirm, Shopify options"
- Address authority: "Get spouse/decision maker on call"
- Address need: "High-intent if watching railing videos"
- Address timing: "How fast do you need it installed? Creates urgency"
