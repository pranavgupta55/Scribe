# Offer Naming

> M-A-G-I-C formula (Magnetic reason, Avatar, Goal, Interval, Container) creates compelling offer names that attract right buyers.

## Framework
- Magnetic: reason why running promotion (Free, 88% off, Spring Promo, New Year)
- Avatar: who offer is for, as specific as possible (not city but sub-market)
- Goal: dream outcome articulated vividly (Pain Free, Celebrity Smile, 7-Figure)
- Interval: duration to achieve (4-Hour, 21-Day, 6-Week, 12-Month)
- Container: bundle descriptor (Challenge, Blueprint, Bootcamp, Intensive, Summit)

## How-To / Method
- Use 3-5 components; not all 5 required
- Shorter and punchier names perform better than long ones
- Test multiple versions; some convert 10x better than others
- Use rhyming or alliteration for memorability (optional)

## Examples
- Free 6-Week Stress Release Challenge
- 12-Week Bikini Blueprint
- Lakeway Moms 90-Day Celebrity Smile Transformation
- Fill Your Gym in 30 Days (Free!)
- 14-Day Find Your Perfect Product Launch
