# Portfolio vs Single Brand

> Portfolio of 4-5 brands rarely succeeds; single strong brand with defensibility outperforms and is easier to manage

## Key Takeaway
- Common goal to build portfolio for exit; almost never achieved
- Results: margin compression, cash flow problems, co-founder friction, sense of wasted effort

## Why It Works
- Single brand allows brand-building investment and defensibility
- Portfolio forces founder to spread effort across losers and winners

## Common Mistakes
- Continuing to invest in losers to 'complete' portfolio
- Not recognizing which SKU is clear winner early enough
