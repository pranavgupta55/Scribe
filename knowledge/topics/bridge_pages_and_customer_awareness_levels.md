# Bridge Pages and Customer Awareness Levels

> Eugene Schwartz framework: cold market expansion requires bridge pages to guide unaware customers upmarket.

## Framework
- 5 awareness levels: unaware → problem-aware → solution-aware → product-aware → most-aware
- Each level has different hook and click journey
- Most-aware = existing customers; expansion goes upmarket to cold awareness levels

## How-To / Method
- Target broader keywords at lower awareness levels (e.g., home projects instead of gaming tables)
- Use advertorial bridge pages to bridge from broad keyword to sales page
- Clicks at lower awareness cost 10x less; ocean of untapped market

## Example
- Keyword: "home projects" or "room design" (problem-aware) → advertorial → sales page for gaming tables
- vs current: gaming table keywords (product-aware) → direct to sales page
