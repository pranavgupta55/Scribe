# Offers and Bonuses

> Offer is king; each bonus should be worth more than entire offer; single-issue buyers stay until they find the one unlock.

## Key Numbers
- Tested offer: pay shipping on 3 books, get all free; loses money, best metrics in career
- Guinness record: $106M in 3 days with bundled offers built over 2 years
- Obama 800K, Prince Harry 1.4M; strong offer drove record

## Framework
- Offer matters more than everything else
- Make offer so good people feel stupid saying no
- Each bonus worth more than entire offer
- Single-issue buyers: stay until one key unlock, one light switch, one reason to say yes

## Key Takeaway
- Best marketing move often is better offer, not better ads or copy
