# Before/After Proof and Social Proof

> Before/afters are the most scalable proof engine for service businesses

## Examples
- Turkish hair clinics post before/afters on Instagram and TikTok all day—this is their inbound demand engine

## How-To / Method
- Collect photos on day 0, day 30, day 60 of every customer
- Optionally give rebates for participation (usually not necessary)
- Create 30-second hook + before/after (can cover eyes or use pain-based hooks like 'struggling with XYZ')
- Source 2+ posts per day from existing customers
