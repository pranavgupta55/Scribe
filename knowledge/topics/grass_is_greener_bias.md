# Grass Is Greener Bias

> Competitors' problems are invisible; their buried bodies hidden; focus on fixing what you have.

## Framework
- When you see a shinier model, you see only upside
- You don't see operating costs, failed experiments, team churn, margin pressure

## Caveats
- Two $100M+ VC groups want your quality because they can't scale it; that's not grass being greener, that's you having the better model

- Grass is greener on the other side because it's fertilized with shit; competitors' problems and bodies are buried where you can't see them. — [how_to_say_no_to_the_wrong_opportunities_YJy7PL2apUo.txt]
