# Pay Then Work Model

> Get paid in full upfront; requires leverage and only works with strong positioning like surgeons or business owners.

## Framework
- Surgeons demand upfront payment before surgery
- Attorneys collect retainers and draw down as work is done
- Layaway model lets customers make payment plans before service starts

## Why It Works
- You take zero risk if they don't complete payments
- People will pay for speed through payment plan options
- Shifting risk shows your leverage in the relationship
