# Gross Margins

> Minimum 80% gross margin for services; net margin cannot exceed gross margin, must fund all operations.

## Framework
- Gross margin = (Price - Cost of Goods/Services) / Price
- 80% minimum for services: leaves 20% for all overhead, marketing, salaries, admin, insurance
- Service business math: can't have 50% net if only 50% gross (no room for CAC, overhead)

## Key Numbers
- $100 cost at 70% gross margin = $333 price; at 80% gross = $500 price
- Moving 70% → 90%: doubles actual margin dollars per customer

## Common Mistakes
- Underpricing because service feels easy (but customer pain/value is what matters)
- Thinking 'close to 80' is fine—each % difference has exponential impact
- Treating commoditized services as undifferentiated (rebrand, increase value perception)
