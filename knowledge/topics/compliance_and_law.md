# Compliance and Law

> Ignorance is not a legal defense; proactively learn applicable laws, review agreements, and understand constraints to gain strategic advantage.

## Key Takeaway
- Ignorance of law does not exclude you from consequences
- Must be educated on laws applying to your business, industry, partnerships
- Most laws written with good intent; following letter of law makes sense

## Method
- Read employment, partnership, contract agreements to understand tools and expectations
- Learn rules of the game you're playing (law = business rules)
- Know the variables in conflict; document actions; minimize legal bandwidth drain

## Implications
- Legal battles consume mental bandwidth (shower, dinner, driving time); reduce via knowledge
- Understanding conflicts allows strategy instead of stress
- Enables you to play with more freedom within rules (know what to avoid)
