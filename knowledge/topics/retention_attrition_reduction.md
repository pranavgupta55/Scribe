# Retention & Attrition Reduction

> Reduce churn from industry average 9-10% to 3-4% within 3 months via five simple systems: reach-outs, attendance tracking, cards, events, exit interviews.

## Key Takeaway
- Churn reduction from 9% to 3% = 300% increase in customer lifetime value
- No gym earning <3% monthly churn fails to make money; it's the highest-leverage metric to track

## Framework
- 1. Reach-out: Every member touched every 14 days via text/messenger (praise + problem-solve)
- 2. Attendance: Weekly reports by Wednesday; <2 visits/week signals cancellation risk; escalate to high priority
- 3. Handwritten cards: New member welcome card day 1, referral request card month 3-6, milestone thank-you cards
- 4. Events: Every 21 days (BBQ, bowling, hike, etc.); members-only for internal plays to drive FOMO
- 5. Exit interview: Before cancellation, use C-L-O-S-E-R framework to uncover true obstacle and offer creative solution (freeze, schedule shift, price adjustment)
