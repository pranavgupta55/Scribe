# Event-Based Revenue Model Optimization

> Bottom 40 of 140 single-day events generated minimal ROI (<$1k each); three 5-day camps each netted >$100k; shift focus to high-margin events and let competitors chase low-margin volume

## Key Numbers
- Single-day event: $300 ticket (underpriced)
- 5-day camp: $1,200 ticket (also underpriced relative to delivery)
- Bottom 40 of 140 events: net negative or <$1k ROI each

## Strategy
- Focus on 5-day events; increase from 3 to 25 planned for year
- Scale down single-day events to own space without operational drag
- Raise pricing on remaining events

## Competitive Moat
- Competitor copying low-margin single-day model will not profitably copy 5-day camps
- Focus on customer and defensible economics; competitor will fail on bad unit economics
