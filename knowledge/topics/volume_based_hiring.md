# Volume-Based Hiring

> Hiring is a volume game at scale. Spend massively on recruiting when ROI is clear.

## Key Takeaway
- Portfolio company: $1M+/day recruiting spend for 12 weeks
- Hiring 90 people in 12 weeks
- Productivity ramp needed post-hire, but volume matters for speed

## Claims
- Portfolio company model: hired 90 people in 12 weeks via 15 recruiting firms; cost millions/day in spend but hit hiring goal — [helping_a_15m_law_firm_solve_its_1_bottleneck_w435ckDcfy4.txt]
