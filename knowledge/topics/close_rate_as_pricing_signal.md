# Close Rate as Pricing Signal

> Your close rate on a given offer directly reveals how much pricing power you're leaving on the table.

## Framework
- 80%+ close rate: 3-4X pricing available
- 60-80% close rate: 2-3X pricing available
- 50-60% close rate: 1.5-2X pricing available
- 40-50% close rate: 1.25-1.5X pricing available
- 30-40% close rate: appropriately priced
- Below 30% close rate: get better at selling (offer or qualification)

## Why It Works
- High close % = too many yeses = underpriced
- Raising price 3-4X drops close to 35%, but 35% of people paying 4X = 140% revenue
- You also get higher-quality customers and better margins
