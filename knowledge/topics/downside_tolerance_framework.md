# Downside Tolerance Framework

> Don't say yes based on upside alone; evaluate if you can live with and tolerate the downside.

## Framework
- Q1: What's the upside?
- Q2: What's the downside?
- Q3: Can I actually live with the downside?
- Q4 (optional): How likely is the downside?

## Why It Works
- Prevents over-optimism bias by forcing honest assessment of failure scenarios
- Filters out opportunities that look good upside but have catastrophic downside
