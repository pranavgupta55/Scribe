# Alumni Earnings Documentation

> Document graduate earnings to create credible value proof

## How-To
- Get bank statements from 6 months post-graduation
- Offer rebate or free alumni job board access in exchange for earnings data
- Calculate and publish median earnings like Harvard publishes graduate median income

## Why It Works
- Transforms abstract value into concrete, publishable numbers
- Allows price much higher when you have demonstrated ROI
- Include disclosures: 10% don't get jobs, but most triple income
