# Affiliate Partnership Model

> Offer partners high-margin, low-cost service to resell; they keep 100% while you upsell core service.

## Framework
- Current model: Corey sends 4-5 leads to partner (they do $2k replacements), partner sends 20 leads (Corey does $150-500 cleaning)
- New offer: Partner charges $175 (dry vent cleaning), keeps 100%, Corey does work + upsells
- Partner incentive: $175 with zero effort vs. promise to refer (which many break)

## Key Numbers
- Current top partner: sends 30k/month in leads; Corey sends back 40-45k
- Cost of delivery: ~$100 per $175 service
- Upsell conversion: 50-75% into core HVAC ($1,650+)
- CAC if 1-of-3 convert: $75 (exceptional)
- HOA event outbound: 55 inspections booked per event

## How-To / Method
- Two outbound strategies: (1) HOA calendar approach (1-2/week, calendar-driven), (2) direct partner offer with financial incentive
- Lead with affiliate offer (not vague referral promises)
- Train technicians to pitch upsell during service delivery

## Common Mistakes
- Affiliate programs fail when there's no compelling financial offer
- Trying both strategies (HOA + direct partner) overstretches; pick one initially
