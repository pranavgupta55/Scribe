# Business Partnerships and Legal

> Handshake deals work only after years of proven track record; put everything in writing upfront when stakes are low.

## Framework
- Rockefeller: friendship founded on business > business founded on friendship
- Handshake deals only after 5+ years and large data set of consistent behavior
- Trust = predictability (person acts like they have in past, across circumstances)

## Key Numbers
- 8 lawsuits in 2025 (mostly early year)
- All lawsuits involved friends or friendly relationships
- Business conflicts arise 6+ years after creation (contracts signed 6-7 years prior)

## How-To / Method
- Front-load discomfort: ask hard questions when stakes are low
- Ask: what if you don't want to partner? What if I don't? What if one dies? What if we disagree on money/sale?
- Approach: 'I want everything in writing so we have no misunderstanding'
- Better agreements now; best time was 20 years ago, second best is today

## Framework
- Sharon's 5 levels of friendship: past memories, gossip about others, ideas, executing together, money/making/losing money
- Money reveals values and how someone behaves around reinforcers
- Level 5 friendships rare; only do big deals with level 5 friends
