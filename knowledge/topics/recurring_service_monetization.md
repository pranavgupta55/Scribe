# Recurring Service Monetization

> For recurring services, combine deep first-month discount with setup fee, then full price recurring

## How-To / Method
- Set regular recurring price ($1,000/month example)
- Offer deep discount on first month ($100 = 90% off)
- Add setup fee ($500)
- Customer pays: $100 + $500 = $600 first month, then $1,000/month recurring

## Why It Works
- Low first month removes price objection
- Setup fee captures part of discount value back
- Ongoing revenue at full price continues indefinitely if churn is low
