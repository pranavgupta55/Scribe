# Customer Segmentation & Market Focus

> DIY customers (70% of business) are least price sensitive and most obsessed with product; contractors/designers are commoditized and price-shop frequently

## Key Numbers
- DIY: 70% of business
- Custom orders: 30% of business (later optimized to 50%)
- Average order value similar across segments (~$873 per order)

## Framework
- DIY: less price sensitive, product obsessed, high perceived value
- Contractors: price-sensitive, shop between suppliers, commoditized
- Strategy: optimize for customer who feels product is valuable, not volume

## Why It Works
- Gross margin can increase 2-3x by repositioning to less price-sensitive buyers
- Same sales volume with higher margins = better profitability
