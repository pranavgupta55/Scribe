# Price Multiplier Strategy

> Each tier should 5-10x price and deliver proportional value. Weak upsells (e.g., $100 to $129) don't work.

## Why It Works
- Weak upsell ($100→$129): same customer psychology
- Strong upsell ($100→$500): different customer segment
- Expect 20% of tier to uptake next tier

## Claims
- Rule of thumb: 5-10x price increase per tier; expect 20% of each tier to uptake next tier — [the_pricing_rule_that_5x_d_my_profit_overnight_yBrRSS3Vsxk.txt]
