# Community Building Asset

> Build a gated community of target avatars to own your market permanently

## Framework
- Podcast guest becomes automatic community member (gated by broker approval)
- Broker controls scarce resource—access to all other high-net-worth owners in county
- Status flip: broker becomes curator/connector, not transactional agent

## Key Numbers
- 55 people in first day of community launch (multifamily owners + brokers)
- Current podcast: 75 episodes, goal to 500

## Implications
- Creates business asset you can sell (working CRM of all multifamily owners)
- Competitors cannot enter market if you control the community resource
- Transforms from deal-by-deal broker to ecosystem operator
