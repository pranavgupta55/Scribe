# Program-Based Pricing

> For defined-length programs (12 weeks, 6 weeks), use deep first-month discount plus setup fee structure

## Example
- 12-week physical therapy program: $1,000/month × 3 = $3,000 total
- Offer: 88% off first month ($120) + $1,000 setup fee
- Customer pays: $120 + $1,000 = $1,120 first month, then $1,000 × 2 months for total $3,120

## How-To / Method
- Calculate total program cost (months × monthly price)
- Offer deep discount on first month only
- Add setup fee separate to remaining months
