# Insurance Industry Economics

> Traditional broker commissions incentivize keeping clients overpaying, not saving them money

## Key Takeaway
- Buffett identified health insurance as the most broken industry model
- Brokers make 5-10% for convincing clients to overpay

## Framework
- Nonprofit takes 20% cut on savings generated instead of percentage of client costs
- Aligns incentives so company profits when clients save
