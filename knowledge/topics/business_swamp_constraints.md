# Business Swamp Constraints

> At 1-3M revenue with low margins, you're trapped: must either overwork or risk hiring an operator who takes 50%+ of profit.

## Framework
- At $400K-$800K annual profit, hiring an operator costs $200K-$400K, leaving minimal gain
- Options: (1) work double-time to next level, (2) take massive risk on operator hire, (3) fix margins first

## Key Takeaway
- Fixing sales and pricing gets you out of the swamp before hiring
