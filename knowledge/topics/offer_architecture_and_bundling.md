# Offer Architecture and Bundling

> Combine separate products into one compelling offer with a single core value stack and eliminate underutilized features.

## Framework
- Trim and Stack process: list all deliverables, then cut and recombine to keep only what customers will use and love
- Goal: offer few things people really want, not everything possible
- Each bonus should be worth the price of the whole offer on its own

## Current State
- Rise Together ($5K/yr): weekly group calls, Facebook group community, quarterly date night challenges
- Couplepreneur Accelerator ($25K/yr): 2-on-2 quarterly calls, weekly group calls, Slack 24/7, modules, deep-dive onboarding (4-6 hrs)

## Redesigned Offer
- Single $25K-$30K offer (payment plan $2,500-$3K/month or $9K down, then installments)
- Includes: one deep-dive onboarding, quarterly 2-on-2 strategy calls, two in-person events/year, optional group community
- Removes: Slack, weekly group calls, modules (low-engagement burnout features)
- Date night challenges repositioned as core value, not bonus

## Why It Works
- Quarterly date night challenges were #1 customer delight; now positioned front-and-center
- Customers feel less guilt about unused resources when fewer items are offered
- Simpler delivery = simpler marketing, selling, and operations
