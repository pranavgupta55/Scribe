# Identifying Your True Strength

> Direct feedback to quit something you're not good at, then go crush a niche where you're naturally gifted.

## Key Takeaway
- Lead-gen agency was a mismatch; after quitting, $125M in real estate sales in 4 years
- The shift came from feedback: 'Have you worked your own leads and seen if they're actually good?'
- Partner fell off, founder tried the work himself, discovered he was much better at sales than operations

## Implications
- Being told to quit something by a mentor can unlock your actual strength
- The door stays open; you can revisit the failed business once you've proven yourself elsewhere
- Building mastery in one domain first creates the foundation for future pivots
