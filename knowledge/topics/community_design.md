# Community Design

> Build belonging via introduction to 10 regulars; employees with one work friend stay 5x longer.

## How-To / Method
- Identify top 10 model citizens
- DM them about goals, invite to contribute (not just watch)
- Publicly spotlight and recognize behavior
- Introduce new members to these regulars via matchmaking
- Lead with: 'This is John. John, meet Tina, Jesse, Trish. John is great at X. Trish is great at Y.'

## Why It Works
- One real friend outside work = 5x longer tenure
- Prevents in-group/out-group feeling
- 10 regulars alone can anchor entire community engagement
