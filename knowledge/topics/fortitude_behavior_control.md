# Fortitude & Behavior Control

> Second component; intensity of behavior change once tolerance threshold exceeded; the depth and magnitude of deviation from normal

## Key Takeaway
- Low fortitude: tiny comment creates imperceptible reaction
- High fortitude: massive setback causes barely noticeable behavior change

## How-To / Method
- Recognize when you've acted out against your goals
- Admit the weakness to yourself
- Give yourself chance to demonstrate strength by stopping the behavior

## Implications
- Hardest component to improve because requires admitting initial weakness
- Once recognized, reversal reverses momentum of bad decision
