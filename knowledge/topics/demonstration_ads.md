# Demonstration Ads

> Show the product or service in action to build credibility and set expectations for the full customer experience.

## Examples
- Product demo: show lead magnet while explaining it
- High production hero ads: fast-paced, scene switches, product and results throughout
- Service demo: show results (e.g., packed gym) rather than just explaining service
