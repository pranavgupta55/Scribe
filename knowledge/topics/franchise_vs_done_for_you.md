# Franchise vs Done-For-You

> Franchises offer legal protection, equity, and enterprise value that done-for-you lacks

## Key Numbers
- Franchise sale: $150K upfront, 10-year contract
- Equity stake: 10-30% in franchisor parent company
- Franchise sales rep friends: given 30% equity away for 9-figure exits

## Why It Works
- Franchises have earnings disclosures and typical returns (legitimate)
- Done-for-you offers unrealistic promises with high risk of delivery failure
- Franchise buyers get what they sign up for; done-for-you buyers often don't
