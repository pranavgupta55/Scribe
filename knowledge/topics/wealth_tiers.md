# Wealth Tiers

> Wealth impact on happiness follows distinct income plateaus with different ROI profiles

## Key Numbers
- $70k/year: basic happiness threshold
- $150k/year: peak dollar-to-value ratio
- $650k-700k/year: diminishing returns flatten
- $1M+/year: potential quality-of-life decline
