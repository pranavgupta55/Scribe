# Marketing Clarity

> Clear beats clever in all things; repeat until death; conversion percentages tiny; simplicity and clarity are maximally effective.

## Key Numbers
- Made 5,000 ads saying same thing; still most audience didn't know, didn't attend
- 100 people register: <50% show up
- 1,000 see ad: <2% click
- Higher converting than anything in career despite simplicity

## Method
- Repeat message until you're sick of it; you'll never repeat it enough
- One big benefit with 10 reasons beats 10 benefits with one reason each
- Simplify copy down until elegant; emphasize clarity over complexity

## Examples
- Marketing friend: complex 10-thing campaign; simplified to one benefit + 10 reasons; highest converting campaign in company history
- Book launch: 5,000 nearly identical ads still broke record

## Key Takeaway
- Leonardo: curse of winner is aiming too low and achieving; aim big
- Cleverness is ridiculousness when conversion percentages are tiny
