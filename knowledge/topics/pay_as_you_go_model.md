# Pay As You Go Model

> Front-load some payment but face 5x faster turnover than employees due to how quickly vendors get fired.

## How-To / Method
- Structure payments in parallel with work: half now, half later, or milestone-based
- Typical for contractors and independent vendors

## Key Numbers
- 3-12 month average contractor engagement
- 5x annual turnover for vendors vs employees
