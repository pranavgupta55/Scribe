# Customer Retention Through Transparency

> Honesty about timeline + clear incentive = commitment.

## Example
- "If I can lower your electric bill, will you say yes?" → Customer commits
- "It's going to take four months. You'll be the first to get the lower rate." → Customer accepts

## Claims
- The fix: Tell customers upfront "It's going to take four months. You don't have to do anything. Just sign here, and you'll be the first one to get the lower rate." This sets correct expectations and positions the wait as worth it. — [fixing_a_sales_agency_with_a_40_cancellation_rate_2PfbKVGNgPM.txt]
