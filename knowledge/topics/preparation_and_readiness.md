# Preparation and Readiness

> Be ready for the fat pitch by practicing fundamentals daily before opportunity arrives

## How-To / Method
- Practice swing, sprints, coordination drills, gym training before competition
- Build skills, money, network, audience before opportunity appears

## Why It Works
- Prepared people capitalize on opportunities; unprepared people miss them or fail
- Baseball analogy: practice happens before at-bats

## Common Mistakes
- Wait for opportunity before starting preparation
- Get lapped by people who prepared first
