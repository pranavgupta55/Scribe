# Risk vs. Reward Distribution

> Equity provides four levers; give only the ones the person needs and can handle.

## Framework
- Equity provides: cash flow, sale bonus, risk, and control
- If person doesn't want risk, don't give control either
- Leaves only cash flow and sale upside as tools

- Equity provides four things: cash flow, sale bonus dollars, risk, and control; most people seeking incentives don't want risk or control — [never_give_real_equity_do_this_instead_lqKx0GDHFX8.txt]
