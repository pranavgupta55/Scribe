# Comfort as Constraint

> Founder has built a 2–3 hour/week business by hiring right; now comfort is stopping him, not capability

## Key Numbers
- Current revenue: $6M at 2–3 hours/week
- Target: $100M
- Constraint: willingness to go all-in again

## Key Takeaway
- Already proved he can scale (did it first 5 years); now comfortable and doesn't want to disturb it
