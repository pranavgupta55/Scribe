# Active vs Passive Assets

> Side businesses (real estate, etc.) are fine if passive; they become distracting only if you make them active

## How-To / Method
- Use REITs and funds for passive real estate, not active deal-hunting
- Keep active businesses separate from passive ones
- Don't blend skill sets (don't redesign roofs on your real estate because you're a roofer)

## Implications
- Passive money in S&P or rental funds doesn't distract from primary business
- Active involvement in side deals = time lost from main business
