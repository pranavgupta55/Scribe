# Capital Efficiency Mindset

> Constraints force creativity; capital encourages laziness

## Key Takeaway
- You have sufficient cash flow - don't need to raise
- Having capital makes you solve problems with money instead of creativity
- Keep constraint to force smart thinking

## Examples
- Well-funded competitors haven't won because money doesn't solve relationship problems
- Building community requires skill, not capital
