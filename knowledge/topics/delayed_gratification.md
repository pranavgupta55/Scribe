# Delayed Gratification

> Society incentivizes short-term thinking; those who delay gratification compound.

## Examples
- Governments give $100-200k loans to people with zero earning history; sets them up for failure
- Over 50% of high school seniors think millionaire by 25; delusion about required effort

## Implications
- Price of goodness at anything is saying no to everything else
- Effort to compound is length of time you say no to alternative paths
