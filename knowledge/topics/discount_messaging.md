# Discount Messaging

> Frame discounts multiple ways; same discount can be described as percentage off, dollar off, or new price

## Framework
- $1,000/month at 50% off can be: '50% off', '$500 off', or '$500 first month'
- $1,000/month at 20% off can be: '20% off', '$200 off', or '$800 first month'

## How-To / Method
- Test multiple framings in marketing (A/B test headlines)
- Use framing that resonates with target customer segment

## Key Takeaway
- Same discount converted three ways; different framings may have different conversion rates
