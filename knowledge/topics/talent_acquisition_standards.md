# Talent Acquisition Standards

> Hire to pull (want the person), not to push (escape pain). Best talent is always in the future.

## How-To / Method
- Before: hire from pain of understaffing
- After: hire because you really want this specific person
- Monitor this with a computer reminder to reframe hiring motivation
