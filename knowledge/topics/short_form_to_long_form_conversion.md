# Short-Form to Long-Form Conversion

> Shorts funnel people to longs; longs funnel to books

## Framework
- Shorts = ads to get facial recognition, funnel to deeper content
- Longer content = more persuasive, higher commitment
- Books = deepest content, highest commitment signal

## Key Takeaway
- Each layer increase consumer commitment and signal of intent
