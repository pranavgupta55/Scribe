# Value-Based vs Cost-Plus Pricing

> Cost-plus pricing leaves margin on table; shift to value-based (willingness to pay)

## Key Takeaway
- Cost-plus is worst pricing model for service businesses
- Value-based pricing asks: what will customer pay, not what did it cost

## Why It Works
- Decouples price from delivery cost; allows premium on convenience and scale
- In override models (subcontractor delivery), customer pays for operational efficiency, not labor hours
