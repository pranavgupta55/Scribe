# Income Diversification

> Shift from transaction-based to recurring consulting fees with transaction discounts attached

## How-To / Method
- Charge consulting fee annually (e.g., $8,000/year per client) to create recurring revenue
- Bundle with 90% discount on transaction commissions for all consulting members
- Package with free software access (Yardi or similar), creating switching cost

## Key Takeaway
- Commission is psychologically tied to transactions, but consulting fee + commission discount is psychologically different and easier to collect upfront
