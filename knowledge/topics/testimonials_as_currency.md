# Testimonials as Currency

> Proof from paying clients is stronger than proof from free clients, but free-client proof beats no proof.

## Framework
- Free client with testimonial → show to prospect → prospect doesn't compare to free (sees results only)
- Paying client testimonial → stronger signal but harder to earn early

## Implications
- If client won't pay you and won't testify, you're probably not actually good
- If client won't pay but will testify, you have assets for the next sale

## Claims
- If you're good, clients will want to pay because you're making them money; if they don't want to pay, you probably aren't that good—but even then, a testimonial from a free client is valuable. — [how_to_get_your_first_clients_with_zero_proof_QgvX_5km_Yo.txt]
- A testimonial from a free client is valuable to paying clients because paid clients don't have an anchor of free—they see proof of results, not discounted value. — [how_to_get_your_first_clients_with_zero_proof_QgvX_5km_Yo.txt]
