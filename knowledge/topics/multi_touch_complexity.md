# Multi-Touch Complexity

> Longer sales cycles and multiple touchpoints create ambiguity in attribution modeling

## Key Takeaway
- Customer watches for 3 years then converts in 1 week
- Did they come from first touch 3 years ago or most recent trigger?
- Answer: probably most recent, but nuance grows with time

## Framework
- Deeper you go into forensic attribution, more nuanced and harder to act on
- Risk: oversimplification creates wrong priorities
