# BYOA Business Model

> Individual contributors become valuable by bringing trained agents, scaling one-person departments.

## Framework
- BYOS/BYOA: bring your own software or agents
- One person can operate as an entire marketing department via trained agents

## Examples
- Anthropic: one person manages entire marketing function via agents
- Employee offers: "I am your entire marketing department" becomes viable value prop

## Implications
- Enormous earning power at employee level as BYOA contractor
- Can embed in company for equity, freelance as agency, or negotiate cash premium
