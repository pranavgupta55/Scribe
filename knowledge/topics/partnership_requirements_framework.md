# Partnership Requirements Framework

> Partnerships only make sense if partner brings something you don't have

## Framework
- Partner must have: money you don't, skills you don't, time you don't, or network/distribution you don't
- If both partners are the same, one is unnecessary
- Start with business need, not friendship

## Why It Matters
- Rockefeller: rare for great businesses from great friendships, common for friendships from great businesses
- Start business first, friendship follows more naturally
