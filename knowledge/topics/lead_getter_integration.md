# Lead Getter Integration

> Making lead getters (customers, employees, agencies, affiliates) permanent ongoing parts of business operations

## Framework
- Customer integration: referral rewards, repeat purchases, new upsells
- Employee integration: training on all core four methods, performance metrics, advancement paths
- Agency integration: transition from service provider to strategic advisor; team proves capability
- Affiliate integration: make product/incentive part of their core offering; recurring commission structure

## Claims
- Average active affiliate generates $10,000+ per month in sales; affiliates compounding effect: one super-affiliate recruiting 10 agencies recruiting 50 local businesses creating 2,500 leads monthly — [100m_leads.txt]

## Connections
- Affiliate Program Building with tiered payouts (25% tier 1, 50% tier 2, 100% tier 3) becomes a scalable Lead Getter Integration mechanism where one super-affiliate recruiting agencies creates 2,500+ leads monthly. _(connects to: Affiliate Program Building)_
