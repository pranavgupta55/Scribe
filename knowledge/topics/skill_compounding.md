# Skill Compounding

> Everyone learns with enough volume/reps; most underestimate repetitions required; compound through consistency.

## Framework
- In every human endeavor, significant volume required
- Everyone learns eventually if you don't stop
- People see polished result, think natural; didn't hear early bad work

## How-To / Method
- Create snowball of applied skills across domains
- Eventually reach feeling of inevitability: I will get it or die trying
- Stick one thing (don't change mind every 6 months)

## Examples
- Early videos: didn't sound good, no achievements, awkward
- Compounding effect of continued work over years
