# Sales Culture and Consistency

> Volatility is caused by inconsistent culture that rewards mercenary behavior instead of team discipline

## Key Numbers
- $700k peak months collapse to $200-250k
- $20-30k commissions after peaks create feast-famine cycle

## Why It Works
- Sales is a culture game; performance feedback loops are fast
- When culture is weak, one person's off-script deal becomes template for others

## Common Mistakes
- Treating sales team as mercenaries with no culture or team identity
- Not enforcing consequences when people go off script
