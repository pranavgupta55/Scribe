# Call-To-Action Best Practices

> Clear, specific CTAs with demonstrated next steps significantly increase action rates; clarity beats cleverness.

## Framework
- Tell them WHAT to do (click button, call number, reply YES, visit website, scan QR)
- Show them HOW to do it
- Tell them WHEN to do it (e.g., before expires)
- Tell them WHAT they GET (e.g., $1000 free stuff)
- Show WHAT HAPPENS NEXT (optional but powerful)

## How-To / Method
- Show visually what each step looks like on screen
- Walk through clicking the button, filling information, submitting
- Demonstrate the landing page or thank-you page they'll see
- Add urgency, scarcity, guarantees, or bonuses to increase action

## Key Takeaway
- Most ads fail to tell people what to do; do this and you outperform 80% of competition
- More people do what you want when you explicitly tell them to do it
