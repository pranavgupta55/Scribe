## Framework
- Start at premium tier; then launch lower tiers as 'more affordable version' of premium thing
- Starting low and going high doesn't work: 'Budget airline announces luxury service' doesn't land like 'Luxury airline launches budget option'
- Operationally, building for premium first allows you to learn complexity before scaling volume
