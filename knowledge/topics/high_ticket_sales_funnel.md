# High-Ticket Sales Funnel

> High-ticket requires higher friction; gate traffic through synchronous calls

## Framework
- Small traffic → live 1-on-1 call
- Medium traffic → webinar (group call + presentation + pitch 1-on-1)

## Claims
- Wedding photographer market is saturated and easy to generate leads in, but quality is bad; you need a higher friction sales process for $2K+ price points — [how_to_sell_a_2k_service_in_a_saturated_market_trAAFADIAZw.txt]
- For small traffic, bring leads to a live 1-on-1 call; for more traffic, use a webinar (send them a link to hop on a call with more people, present, then solicit 1-on-1 calls) — [how_to_sell_a_2k_service_in_a_saturated_market_trAAFADIAZw.txt]

## Connections
- High-ticket requires friction and synchronous sales; first sale breaks psychological barrier to higher pricing. _(connects to: Upselling To High Ticket Customers)_
- High-ticket sales have high turnover and require synchronous friction to gate low-quality leads. _(connects to: Churn And Cohort Dynamics)_
- High-ticket requires synchronous friction and gates; 90-95% close rates signal massive underpricing requiring raised prices. _(connects to: High Ticket Sales Pricing)_
- High-ticket sales require higher friction via synchronous calls; good friction removes bad leads and improves close efficiency. _(connects to: Sales Funnel Friction)_
