# Spending Skill Gap

> Most high earners never learn spending as a skill

## Key Takeaway
- People know to spend up to $100k/year for QoL
- Above that, spending is largely ineffective

## Why It Works
- Spending is learnable; time-buying is primary skill
- Alignment with purpose creates lasting value
