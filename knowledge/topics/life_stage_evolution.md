# Life Stage Evolution

> At higher business scales, quality of judgment (bets) matters more than quantity of work; prioritize sleep, travel, people to optimize decision-making.

## Key Numbers
- Biggest financial year in career (including exits)
- Achieved 'enough' for first time; bigger financial goals than most but now satisfied
- One deal (6.5 hours, one phone call, well-rested) worth more than annual revenue
- Best sleep score and HRV of entire life on day deal was structured

## Framework
- Crossed threshold: quality of judgment > quantity of work
- Model the rise, not the peak (fly private after you're rich, not to get rich)
- Shifting from working all day to planning personal life first, business fills cracks

## Method
- Maximize free/unplanned time to maximize CEO time allocation
- Prioritize better sleep hygiene (10-20% gains)
- Travel more to gain perspectives, filters, people input on decisions
- Unplug on weekends to let mind wander for out-of-box ideas
