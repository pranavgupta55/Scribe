# Margin Compression

> Scaling spend on arbitrage business compresses margins; eventually reaches breakeven

## Key Numbers
- Current: $10M revenue, 15% margins
- Problem: scaling spend increases CPA, which decreases margins

## Framework
- Each rung of scaling requires more spend to get same number of sales
- Performance marketing: same rung, but buy more traffic to get there

## Why It Happens
- Scaling ads hits less profitable audiences
- Eventually you're spending $1 to make $1
