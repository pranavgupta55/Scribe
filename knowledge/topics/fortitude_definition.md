# Fortitude Definition

> Measure of behavioral intensity after breaking point

## Framework
- How much do you change how you act once you've had enough and snap

## Claims
- Fortitude is the intensity of behavior change once your tolerance threshold has been surpassed — [how_to_build_unshakeable_fortitude_in_your_business_X4z_5m5_hig.txt]
- High fortitude means you snap/react, but the change in behavior is very small — [how_to_build_unshakeable_fortitude_in_your_business_X4z_5m5_hig.txt]

## Connections
- Fortitude measures behavioral intensity under stress; it is both definitional and measurable. _(connects to: Fortitude Behavior Control)_
- Fortitude measures behavioral intensity after the breaking point; mental toughness spans stimulus threshold, amplitude, rebound speed, and height. _(connects to: Mental Toughness)_
- Resilience and fortitude are independent; fortitude measures intensity after breaking point, not recovery speed. _(connects to: High Vs Low Resilience)_
- Fortitude measures behavioral intensity after breaking; tolerance—how much hardship before cracking—is measurable and improvable. _(connects to: Tolerance As A Mental Toughness Component)_
