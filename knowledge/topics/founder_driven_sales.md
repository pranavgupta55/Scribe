# Founder-Driven Sales

> Founder expertise-based sales doesn't scale; you become the bottleneck even as company grows.

## Key Takeaway
- You close because you have authority and deep industry knowledge
- Hiring someone to replicate that is expensive or impossible
- You cap out at $5M because only you and COO can close

## Implications
- To reach $100M, you need a sales process that doesn't require founder
- You become the constraint, not the solution

## Claims
- $17-18M real estate services company; founder and COO spend 20 hours per week closing, cap out at $5M external revenue because no one else can close — [why_you_re_still_the_only_closer_in_your_business_fECT6E27oi8.txt]
- Founder-driven sales is consultative and relies on authority/expertise, not scalable; can't find 22-year-olds with founder expertise or authority — [why_you_re_still_the_only_closer_in_your_business_fECT6E27oi8.txt]
