# Downside Capped Upside Uncapped

> Young person with time has capped downside (lose nothing) and uncapped upside (unlimited tries); recipe for opportunity.

## Framework
- If nothing to lose, have unlimited lottery tickets from life
- Chips replenish daily with time; game over when time runs out not coins
- Fear comes from having something to lose; fearlessness from knowing nothing belonged to you

- Young person's advantage: nothing to lose except time
