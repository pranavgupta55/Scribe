# Bootstrapped Business Model

> Fund your business from personal savings and reinvested profits with no outside investors.

## Examples
- Services, agencies, home service businesses
- Software (modern advantage vs. past)
- Education, e-commerce, local businesses

## Key Takeaway
- Best for first-time founders to learn without risking others' capital
- Slowest growth but maximum control

## Claims
- Four paths to mega wealth: your money/your business (bootstrap), other's money/your business (raise capital), your money/other's business (invest), other's money/other's business (fund management). — [the_4_proven_ways_to_build_wealth_in_2026_sL16tsGafcQ.txt]
- Bootstrapping is typically the slowest path because you must build the capital-generation machine while also building the capital-allocation machine simultaneously. — [the_4_proven_ways_to_build_wealth_in_2026_sL16tsGafcQ.txt]
