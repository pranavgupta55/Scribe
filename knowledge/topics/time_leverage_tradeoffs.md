# Time-Leverage Tradeoffs

> Calculate income per hour per activity, then ruthlessly delegate everything below threshold

## How-To / Method
- List all daily activities
- Ask: How much am I making from this activity?
- Ask: How many hours per day does it take?
- Peel off the 2-3 lowest ROI activities and delegate them
