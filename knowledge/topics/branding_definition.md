# Branding Definition

> Branding = deliberate pairing of product with outcomes to drive customer behavior.

## Framework
- Outcome (the feeling/benefit) is what creates the pairing
- Customer pairs outcome with product in memory
- Next time customer wants outcome, they reach for product

## Why It Works
- Human behavior is driven by association and repetition
- Pairing creates mental connection between product and desired feeling
