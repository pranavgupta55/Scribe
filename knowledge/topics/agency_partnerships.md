# Agency Partnerships

> Using external service providers to learn new platforms/methods with explicit endgame of team independence

## Key Takeaway
- Agencies teach skills your team keeps forever; don't become dependent

## How-To / Method
- Start with 6-month learning contract; agency teaches how/why behind decisions
- Set explicit goal: team matches agency performance within 6 months
- Pay for consulting afterward if needed, but with understanding you're transitioning to independence
- Pick agencies meeting 10 criteria: word-of-mouth, prominent client results, waiting list, clear process, realistic expectations, transparent needs, regular meetings, clear metrics, good offer (dream outcome/likelihood/time/effort), expensive

## Examples
- $750/hour agency taught Alex paid ads; $6,000 investment made back immediately in improved ROI
- Two-agency strategy: one 'good enough' to learn basics, one elite to maximize; graduated from both within 6-8 months

- Use agencies for 6-month learning sprints with explicit deadline to have your team match their performance, then transition to consulting-only; this prevents permanent dependency — [100m_leads.txt]
