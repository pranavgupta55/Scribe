# Offer Flexibility

> Same structure (discount + fee) can be adapted by waiving/charging different fees for different customer tiers

## How-To / Method
- High-value customers: charge discount + setup fee
- Medium customers: charge discount, waive setup fee
- Special tier: charge nothing upfront, higher recurring
- All use same underlying structure, just different combinations

## Why It Works
- Flexibility without devaluing core offer
- Salesperson can negotiate terms without changing core pricing
