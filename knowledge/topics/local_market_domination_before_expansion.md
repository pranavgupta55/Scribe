# Local Market Domination Before Expansion

> Conquer a city before a country, a country before the world; overexpansion causes collapse; Fayetteville-Raleigh market alone could reach $100M

## Key Takeaway
- Local dominance means maximized market share in defensible geography
- National expansion without local base is strategic overreach

## Example
- Home flipping business: 10-30 deals/month at $4M revenue could 8x to $32M by handling 20 deals/week within existing Fayetteville-Raleigh base

## Implications
- Focus on deepening market share, raising prices, and optimizing operations locally
- Content and brand scale nationally without requiring local operational complexity
