# Motivation Definition

> Motivation is the inverse of deprivation; you're most driven by what you lack relative to your reference point.

## Framework
- Physiological: sleep deprivation → sleep motivation; hunger → food motivation
- Psychological: money motivation = perceived money deficit relative to reference point (peer comparison)

## Example
- Billionaire peer group → 100M person feels deprived and hungry
- 5K/month peer group → 10K/month goal feels sufficient

## Claims
- Motivation equals deprivation; you're most motivated when most deprived. Motivation for sleep = sleep deprivation, motivation for food = hunger, motivation for money = perceived deficit relative to reference point. — [why_watching_podcasts_is_making_you_dumber_bpkxMfNiMbA.txt]
