# Tiered Offer Architecture

> Offer multiple tiers; high-margin premium funds low-margin volume; do the math to see where profit really comes from.

## Key Numbers
- 100 customers × $100 at 40% margin = $3,600 profit
- 10 customers × $1,000 at 100% margin = $10,000 profit
- 90% of customers generate 25% of profit; 10% generate 75%

## Why It Works
- High-margin tier subsidizes volume play
- Incrementally small volumes of expensive thing = big money

## How-To / Method
- If have $100 offering, add $1,000 tier
- If have community at $100/month, add $1,000 and $10,000 options
- Just make it available; >0% of people will buy it
