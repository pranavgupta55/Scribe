# Margin Protection

> Set fixed price and fixed discount upfront; never renegotiate to avoid haggling and credibility loss

## Framework
- Price is the price; discount is the discount; both set in advance and never change
- Present as: 'Today it's 50% off, tomorrow it won't be'

## Common Mistakes
- Quoting $1,000, customer says 'can you do $900?', you agree
- Loss of credibility: customer thinks 'you would have accepted $800 too'
- Opens haggling cycle: customer always tests lower price next time

## Why It Works
- Fixed pricing maintains professional frame
- Customer trusts you're not arbitrarily changing prices
- Removes incentive to haggle (price doesn't move)
