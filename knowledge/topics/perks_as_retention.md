# Perks as Retention

> Alternate title bumps with perks so people don't just take title and leave

## Examples
- Junior+ title + don't work Saturday nights
- Pest Deliverer title + pick your calendar
- Senior title + better truck
- Senior+ title + parking spot or other benefit

## Why It Works
- Title alone has limited value; perk increases perceived value of promotion
- Scatters rewards so each rung has something tangible
