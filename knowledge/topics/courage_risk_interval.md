# Courage & Risk Interval

> Courage = shortness of time interval between perceiving risk and taking action; longer interval = less courageous.

## Framework
- Risk perceived → [interval] → Action taken
- Short interval = high courage
- Long interval = low courage

## Claims
- Courage = the interval of time between perceiving something risky and taking action; shorter interval = more courageous — [how_to_see_reality_more_clearly_than_99_of_people_8YJEyMYYwnE.txt]

- Courage is short time between perceiving risk and taking action; fear dissolves when worst-case is detailed and quantified. _(connects to: Fear And Risk Taking)_
