# Passion vs Purpose

> Passion (your what) is external and fickle; purpose (your why/how) is internal and persistent.

## Framework
- What you do (carving ships, painting, gaming) = external, uncontrollable
- Why and how you do it = internal, persists over time
- What changes but why/how endures

## Key Takeaway
- Don't pursue passion for the what; pursue passion for the why
- Why and how get you through hard times; what is a treat not requirement
