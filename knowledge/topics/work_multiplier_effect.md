# Work Multiplier Effect

> Hiring contractors to handle volume work multiplies output; cost is minimal vs. revenue upside from scaling.

## Key Numbers
- Money Models: 15 editors total (5 in-house + 10 contractors) to hit 2,000 ads
- Cost to add editors << $105M revenue

## How-To
- Assess: what daily output can team produce?
- Calculate: how many staff needed for goal volume?
- Answer: almost always yes, do it
