# Self-Linking Business Loops

> Engineer your business to generate proof while serving customers; the loop feeds itself and your marketing.

## Method
- Hair extensions example: sweepstakes inside product → winners get in-person salon install → documents proof → feeds marketing → new customers buy
- B2B services: bring customers in, walk through campaigns/audits, document, share publicly
- For small agencies with 10 customers: run audits for all of them, document, package as marketing

## Why It Works
- Proof generation is baked into normal business operations, not extra work
- Each customer interaction becomes marketing asset
- Loop compounds: more customers → more proof → more trust → more customers
