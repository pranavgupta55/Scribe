# Mass Market Economics

> Mass-market low-price models require enormous capital and operational excellence.

## Example
- Netflix/Spotify charge $13; must create world-class entertainment across genres
- Both backed by outside capital to subsidize until scale achieved

## Implications
- Don't bootstrap into mass market; it's capital-intensive and requires automation
