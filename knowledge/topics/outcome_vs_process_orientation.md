# Outcome vs. Process Orientation

> Experts differ from novices in how they measure success

## Framework
- Experts are rewarded by the work itself, not just outcomes
- Judge yourself only by actions, not thoughts; this reduces self-disappointment

## Claims
- Experts at any skill become experts because they learn to be rewarded from the work itself, not just the outcome — [why_you_re_not_burnt_out_you_re_just_weak_85HElwKCAPY.txt]
- Judge yourself by your actions, not your thoughts; I became significantly less disappointed in myself when I stopped judging by thoughts and only by actions I took — [why_you_re_not_burnt_out_you_re_just_weak_85HElwKCAPY.txt]

## Connections
- Expert outcomes matter less than person-you-become from process; skill development creates external results as byproduct. _(connects to: Skills Over Outcomes)_
