# The Who Game and Leadership Levels

> Scaling from $6M to $100M requires transitioning from solo operations to hiring levels of talent; each level changes what's possible

## Key Takeaway
- Levels: $50K employee → $100K → $250K → $500K → multimillion-dollar talent
- Each hire unlocks new business scale; world-class talent is non-negotiable

## Examples
- Private equity founder at Gym Launch sale had visibly better team than founder team; stark difference drove outcomes
- Roofing founder can't reach $100M alone; must attract $250K–$500K talent with compelling vision

## Implications
- Small founders must win on character, consistency, and vision (not compensation)
- Vision must be big enough for A-players to see their aspirations fit within it
