# Speed & Latency as Leverage

> Speed (latency reduction) beats magnitude; time saved motivates more than money saved, especially for wealthy buyers.

## Key Takeaway
- Speed > money; as people get wealthy, time becomes more valuable
- How fast outcome happens > how big outcome is

## How-To / Method
- Cut delivery time in half or two-thirds
- Priority status for premium tier: first in line, first response, emergency pull
- Communicate speed explicitly in offer language
