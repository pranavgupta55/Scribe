# Close Rate vs Pricing

> Price and close rate form an inverse relationship—use close rate to identify pricing opportunity.

## Framework
- 80%+ close rate = 3-4x underpriced
- 60-80% close rate = 2-3x underpriced
- 50-60% close rate = 1.5-2x underpriced
- 40-50% close rate = 1.25-1.5x underpriced
- 30-40% close rate = appropriately priced (with proper sales motion)
- Below 30% = avatar/sales motion issue (not price issue)

## Exception
- SaaS with unlimited scale: balance price vs growth, lower price increases growth/decreases margin
- Service businesses (80% of market): unscalable, always raise price
