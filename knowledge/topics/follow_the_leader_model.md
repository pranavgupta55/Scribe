# Follow-the-Leader Model

> Competitors avoid the cancellation problem by batching commitments before fulfillment.

## Framework
- Get commitment and banking info upfront from sales
- Canvas neighborhood to hit a target percentage of signups
- Only then dispatch electrician teams for bulk installs
- Result: No surprise delays, no cancellations
