# AI-Driven Lead Generation Without Ads

> AI-Driven Lead Generation Can Be Efficient Without Ads

## Key Takeaway
- Generating leads from Facebook without leveraging the customer's database is inefficient.
- AI-driven lead generation can yield results without relying on existing customer data.

## Evidence & Examples
- A CrossFit gym in Alexandria, Virginia, generated $7,190 in sales from 26 appointments without paying for ads, using AI-driven lead generation.
- The example demonstrates that AI can replace traditional ad-based lead generation methods.
