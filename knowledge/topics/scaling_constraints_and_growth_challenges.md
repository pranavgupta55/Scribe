# Scaling Constraints and Growth Challenges

> Each business model hits specific breaking points; understanding them allows founder to solve bottlenecks proactively

## E-Commerce Constraints
- Cash constraints: profits must finance next inventory level; growth rate dictated by free cashflow generated
- Traffic/ad constraints: ad accounts stop performing past certain spend levels; distribution channels become saturated
- Supply chain: manufacturer capacity limits (1k units/day vs. 10k demand) causes flat growth until new manufacturer found
- Inventory management: lead times require forecasting demand 12+ weeks out; prediction errors cause stockouts or excess inventory

## Service Business Constraints
- Talent supply is always bottleneck; quality people harder to find as business scales up the skill tier
- Founder technical expertise dependency: if founder is best technician, they become bottleneck solving all complex problems
- Growth hard to scale YoY; doubling headcount while maintaining quality and reputation is difficult

## Education Business Constraints
- Retention is zero by design; always refilling customer base makes growth difficult past $1-3M
- Creates own competition: graduates can immediately teach what they learned at lower price
- Promotion skill bottleneck: most founders can't effectively market past initial launch; requires different skill set than education creation
- Scaling delivery with coaches dilutes reputation if coaches not as good as founder

## SaaS Constraints
- Pre-product-market-fit survival: 1-2 years of negative cash burn before revenue becomes material
- Monetization difficulty: low willingness to pay for software forces massive volume requirement to be profitable
- Retention critical: without retention, growth from new signups doesn't compound; churn outpaces new customer acquisition
