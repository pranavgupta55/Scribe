# Founder Optimization

> Highest-ROI founder investment is buying back founder time

## Key Numbers
- $50k/year spend gets founder 100 hours/month back
- For founder making $1M+/year, this is exceptional ROI

## Why It Works
- Founder time at high income level is most constrained resource
- Enables focus on highest-leverage activities

## Connections
- $20M-scaled founders outperform $3M exits; highest ROI comes from buying back founder time. _(connects to: Ambition Based Founder Screening)_
