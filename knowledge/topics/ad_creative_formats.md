# Ad Creative Formats

> Five proven ad formats work across all industries; mix and match based on what your best hooks require.

## Framework
- Demonstration: live use, unboxing, before-and-afters, hero ads (Old Spice style)
- Testimonial: user-generated, founder on camera, podcast style, raw testimonials, walk-n-talk rants, parade of proof, influencer collabs
- Education: whiteboard explainers, how-to listicles, tutorials, high-performing organic
- Story: narrative, lifestyle, emotional, humor, brand manifesto, problem-solution
- Faceless: screenshots, text-only, slideshows, animations, cartoons, visual effects

## Key Takeaway
- Format choice depends on hook and audience awareness level
- Mix and match formats to test different angles with same hook
