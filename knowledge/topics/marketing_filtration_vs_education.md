# Marketing Filtration vs Education

> Marketing finds buyers, it doesn't create them

## Key Takeaway
- The vast majority of the time, marketing is not an education system but a filtration system
- Exceptions exist (someone buying a book leading to a startup) but they're rare, not the rule
- This mindset shift changes how you build lead generation strategies
