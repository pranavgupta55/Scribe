# Trainer Motivation & Culture

> Extrinsically motivate via income paths; intrinsically motivate via significance (awards), contribution (solicit ideas), growth (pay for learning), certainty (on-time pay).

## Extrinsic Levers
- Revenue share on semi-privates (25% of gross)
- Supplement commissions per sale
- Internal play bonuses if trainer reaches enrollment targets

## Intrinsic Levers
- Significance: Read client wins in every team meeting; name 'Trainer of the Month' publicly
- Contribution: Ask 'What could we do better for clients?' in one-on-ones; implement their ideas
- Growth: Pay trainers $30/week to attend certifications or online courses during off-hours
- Certainty: Pay salaries on time, always; no emotional swings from leadership; ask questions before correcting
