# Margin Expansion

> Offshoring and AI can simultaneously increase operating leverage and expand margins

## Framework
- Offshore reduces cost per unit
- AI automation increases output per unit
- Both expand the margin function

## Connections
- Growth simultaneously compresses margins and enables expansion; AI workflows fix compression better than sales. _(connects to: Operations And Margin Compression)_
- Expanding before fixing margins compounds problems; offshoring and AI expand margins simultaneously. _(connects to: Margin Degradation)_
