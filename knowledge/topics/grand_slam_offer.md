# Grand Slam Offer

> An offer so compelling that prospects feel stupid saying no, requiring proper combination of pricing, value, guarantees, and positioning.

## Framework
- Offer = goods/services + payment method + terms of agreement
- Combines attractive promotion, unmatchable value proposition, premium price, unbeatable guarantee
- Allows selling in a category of one with no price comparison
- Gets more customers at higher prices for less money spent

## Key Numbers
- Can create 22.4x improvement in upfront cash collected
- Response rates increase 2.5x with compelling offer
- Conversion increases 2.5x due to better value perception
- Price increases 4x when differentiated from commodities
