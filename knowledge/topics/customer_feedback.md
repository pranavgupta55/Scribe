# Customer Feedback

Customer feedback is systematically collected through a combination of rating scales (1-5) and dedicated feedback forms, with incentives like raffles offering year-long memberships to encourage participation. Businesses leverage feedback to improve services, act on insights, and enhance their online reputation, while also using AI to reach out to past and current clients. However, the effectiveness of these strategies varies, with some systems failing to generate reviews or referrals, highlighting the importance of proper implementation.  

Contradictions arise in the balance between incentivized feedback and ethical practices. While some sources emphasize avoiding explicit offers in the feedback process, others describe raffles as a tool to boost engagement, raising concerns about whether such incentives undermine genuine customer satisfaction. Additionally, the system’s ability to generate reviews and referrals is inconsistent, with some sources noting its ineffectiveness despite the value of high response rates and organic visibility. This highlights the need for tailored strategies that align with both business goals and customer expectations.
