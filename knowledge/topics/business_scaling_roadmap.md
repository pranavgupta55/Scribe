# Business Scaling Roadmap

> Seven-level progression from first customers to $100M+ enterprise showing what comes next at each stage

## Framework
- Level 1: Friends know about it (warm outreach only)
- Level 2: Consistent pipeline (maximize personal work, one platform mastered)
- Level 3: Employees help (hire to scale beyond personal capacity)
- Level 4: Referrals flow (product delivers 25%+ of customers via word-of-mouth)
- Level 5: Multiple platforms/methods with team scaling (2+ core four activities, 2+ platforms)
- Level 6: Executive department heads (battle-hardened leaders own new advertising channels)
- Level 7: $100M+ enterprise (systems run without founder; all cylinders firing)

## Key Numbers
- 5-10 years to $100M even knowing exact steps; 12-24 month portfolio company average gains: 1.8x revenue and 3.01x profit year 1; 2.3x revenue and 4.7x profit by year 2

## Timeline
- Realistic expectations: 3-6 months minimum to crack any new lead source; most fail 9x before winning 1x
