# Tolerance as a Mental Toughness Component

> How much hardship can you endure before you crack is a measurable, improvable trait

## Framework
- Tolerance = length of the fuse before behavior changes
- High tolerance = long fuse = lots of stress before crack
- Low tolerance = short fuse = anything sets you off
