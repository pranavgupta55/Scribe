# Talent Retention Strategy

> Build loyalty to brand, not to individual provider

## How-To / Method
- Use phantom stock program: providers vest into schedule equity, don't get location equity
- Create career path for providers to stay
- Pay providers more even though margins are thin
- Ensure providers are loyal to brand first, not to customers

## Why It Works
- Top providers walk with customers when they leave
- Membership model (vs one-on-one provider relationship) starts shifting loyalty
- Phantom stock creates longer retention than equity-giving
