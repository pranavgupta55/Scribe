# Customer Data Ignored

> Agencies Ignore Customer Data, Leading to Frustration and Loss of Trust

## Key Takeaway
- Agencies use tested content (e.g., images, videos) but fail to proactively engage customers.
- Lack of proactive customer management leads to frustration and poor user experience.

## Why It Works
- The core issue is agencies' inability to balance customer-centric strategies with traditional marketing practices.

## Implications
- Customers feel neglected, leading to a desire for alternative solutions due to the absence of proactively managed interactions.
- Agencies failing to manage issues cause dissatisfaction and prompt customers to seek new partners.

## Evidence & Examples
- Customers feel neglected when their needs are not addressed, leading to a desire for alternative solutions.
