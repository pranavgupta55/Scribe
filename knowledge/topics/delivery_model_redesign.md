# Delivery Model Redesign

> Current cohort-scarcity model doesn't scale; founder must hire delivery coach or restructure without changing selling motion

## Key Takeaway
- 6 active cohorts of 20–25 companies, launch twice yearly, 90-min weekly sessions + daily office hours = unsustainable

## Method
- Don't change selling model (demand is abundant)
- Change delivery structure (not evergreen, but more repeatable)
- Hire second coach or restructure content into recorded/async where possible
