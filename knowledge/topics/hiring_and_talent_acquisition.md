# Hiring and Talent Acquisition

> Invest in world-class talent; $200K salary returning $1M lifetime gross profit is 5:1 return, better than S&P

## Key Numbers
- Fence company: each $200K salesman generates $1M gross profit lifetime = 5:1 return
- Roofing business: hire $250K–$500K+ talent to delegate, sacrifice short-term profit for growth

## Framework
- The Who game has levels: $50K hires (can read/write), $100K (transformative), $500K, $1M+
- Best talent is in the future, not behind you; you must punch above your weight to attract it

## Why It Works
- Top talent attracts more top talent; team quality is a business multiplier
- Hiring caps founder bottleneck; frees time to scale vision, not operations
