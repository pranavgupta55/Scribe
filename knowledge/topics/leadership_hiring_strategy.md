# Leadership Hiring Strategy

> Missing top layer is trapping founders in day-to-day; hire one role at a time by urgency

## Key Numbers
- $4.5M monthly, target $25M
- Founders doing everything because no C-suite layer

## Framework
- Identify 6-7 missing roles
- Prioritize by constraint (which role is most urgent)
- Hire that role first, then move to next
