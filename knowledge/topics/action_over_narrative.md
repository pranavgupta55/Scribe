# Action Over Narrative

> The story you tell yourself about motivation matters less than the actions you take.

## Framework
- You take action (event)
- You create narrative to explain it (often wrong)
- Narrative may be completely false but action is real

## Implications
- Stop worrying about the "why" and focus on the "what" (actions)
