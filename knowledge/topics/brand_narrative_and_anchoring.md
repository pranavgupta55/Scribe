# Brand Narrative and Anchoring

> High anchor makes affordable offerings feel like opportunities, not compromises.

## Framework
- Anchor high: "This amazing $250K car is out of reach for many"
- Then justify lower price: "So I made a similar car that's more affordable for you"
- Reverse fails: Budget brand claiming expensive product lacks credibility

## Claims
- Anchoring high with an expensive product creates a stronger brand narrative: positioning cheaper products as accessible alternatives works better than starting cheap and going expensive. — [why_you_should_always_build_a_business_top_down_Gr2A7wUoEwo.txt]

## Connections
- Avoid the middle; sell expensive to few or cheap to many; high anchor benefits entire brand perception. _(connects to: Pricing Strategy Anchoring)_
- High anchor makes affordable offerings feel like opportunities; downgrade from $100K offer by removing one feature to create compelling mid-tier. _(connects to: Offer Stacking Upsells)_
- 10x upsell anchors entire offer and improves base conversions; high anchor makes affordable offerings feel like opportunities, not compromises. _(connects to: Anchor Pricing)_
- High anchors make affordable offers feel like opportunities; branding enables premium pricing and competitive protection. _(connects to: Premium Positioning)_
