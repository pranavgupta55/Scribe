# Focus Through Subtraction

> Focus comes from removing distractions, not from adding more effort.

## Framework
- List everything you could do
- Remove everything that doesn't matter
- What's left is your focus

## Why It Works
- Prevents overwhelm from seeing too many opportunities
- Concentration on one thing beats scattered effort on many
