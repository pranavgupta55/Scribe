# Content Consumption Trap

> Listening to podcasts without behavior change is entertainment disguised as learning.

## Key Takeaway
- Thousands of podcasts, same life conditions, zero behavior change = wasted time

## How-To / Method
- Before consuming: commit to a behavior change you'll implement
- Without one: stop and do something else

## Claims
- People listen to thousands of podcasts in the exact same conditions but don't change behavior, which means they have not learned and are not intelligent (by definition). — [why_watching_podcasts_is_making_you_dumber_bpkxMfNiMbA.txt]
- Every time you consume content, ask: what behavior am I going to change? If the answer is nothing, you wasted your time and were entertaining yourself, not learning. — [why_watching_podcasts_is_making_you_dumber_bpkxMfNiMbA.txt]

## Connections
- Real learning requires behavior change through feedback loops, not just content consumption. _(connects to: Learning Through Iteration)_
- Podcasts alone aren't learning; true learning requires behavior change; only edutainment builds business. _(connects to: Three Content Buckets)_
