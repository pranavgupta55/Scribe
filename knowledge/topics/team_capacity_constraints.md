# Team Capacity Constraints

> If founder is required to train and systemize, you can't scale locations; fix that first before new models.

## Framework
- Current: founder + team integrated, founder required for training
- Needed: team can open location, run training, hit metrics without founder

## Why It Works
- Once that's true, you can pursue licensing without killing yourself
- At that point, you probably won't care anymore (more interesting problems)
