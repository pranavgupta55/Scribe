# Overhead Management

> Eliminate unprofitable sessions by targeting 80% service margin and cutting excess classes while raising trainer pay standards.

## Key Numbers
- Current gym: 163 sessions/month, $72/session revenue, $24.40/session trainer cost = 66% margin (below 80% threshold)
- Fixed: 99 sessions/month, $119/session revenue, $18.30/trainer cost = 84.6% margin
- Outcome: $3,741/month overhead savings → $25,932/year additional profit; doubles take-home for typical owner

## How-To / Method
- Calculate: Monthly revenue ÷ sessions/month = revenue per session
- Trainer cost: hourly rate × 1.22 (payroll tax) = fully-loaded cost per session
- Margin: (revenue per session − cost) ÷ revenue = % of each dollar left for rent, admin, profit
- Target 80%+ margin; if below, cut sessions or negotiate trainer pay to 'calibrate'
