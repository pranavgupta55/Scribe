# Proof-Promise-Plan Framework

> Educational video intros should include proof (credentials), promise (what you'll learn), and plan (structure).

## Framework
- Proof: establish authority (credentials, track record, past results)
- Promise: state specific outcome viewer will get
- Plan: explain video structure and what happens next

## Method
- Lead with whichever element is most important for that content
- For money/business content, lead with proof (answers 'why should I listen?')
- Example: 'I'm Alex, I built companies doing $250M/year revenue. In this video, I'll teach X.'
