# Intelligence as Learning Rate

> Intelligence = how fast you learn; if one person needs 10 examples and another 5, second person is more intelligent in that context.

## Framework
- Measurable as iterations to mastery
- Not fixed; context-dependent
- Directly influenceable by studying faster

## Why It Works
- Removes fixed 'you're smart or not' mindset
- Allows direct impact on own intelligence
- Learning rate is trainable
