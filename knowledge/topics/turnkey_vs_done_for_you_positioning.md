# Turnkey Vs. Done-For-You Positioning

> Clients prefer done-for-you (no friction) but it requires you to operationalize; consider pure acquisition play instead.

## Key Takeaway
- High-income earners want turnkey (no setup, no management work for them)
- Offering it requires you to manage operations at scale; kills margins and takes time
- Pure acquisition play: you find deal, introduce CPA for management, you done

## Implications
- Easier sale with all-in-one positioning, but impossible operations
- Alternative: keep relationship but introduce trusted partners; client stays with you, you don't operate
