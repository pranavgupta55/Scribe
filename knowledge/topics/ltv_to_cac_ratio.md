# LTV to CAC Ratio

> Adjust LTV to CAC targets based on automation level; human-in-loop requires higher ratio padding.

## Automation Levels
- 0 humans (SaaS): 3-to-1 minimum
- 1 human (e.g., ads → salesperson → automated delivery): 6-to-1
- 2 humans (ads → salesperson → service delivery): 9-to-1
- 3 humans (manual outreach → salesperson → delivery): 12-to-1

## Why It Works
- Humans create lumpiness/inconsistency; padding absorbs cost of new hires and performance ramp
- Example: Gym Launch first year hit 100-to-1 (spent $100k, made $10M)

## Two Paths to Scale
- Drive CAC to zero: build massive brand or viral product (Facebook model)
- Maximize LTV: land huge enterprise customers at high price (Salesforce model)
