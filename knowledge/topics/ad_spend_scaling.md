# Ad Spend Scaling

> Only scale spend after landing page and ad creative are optimized; maintain ROAS by keeping quality constant.

## Framework
- Order: optimize funnel → fix ads → scale spend (not simultaneously)
- Expected outcome: funnel/ads fixes may double leads without extra spend
- Constant ROAS × 2x spend = 2x leads, conversions, revenue

## Key Takeaway
- May not need to increase spend if funnel optimization already hits lead capacity (supply constraint emerges)
- Scale in increments; monitor ROAS at each spend level
