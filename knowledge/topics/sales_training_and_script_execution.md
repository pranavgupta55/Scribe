# Sales Training and Script Execution

> Daily training, role-play, script memorization, and process adherence trump raw talent.

## How-To / Method
- Memorize script: print two copies, read aloud, progressively black out words until reciting from fully blacked-out page (you say script as many times as there are words)
- Role-play: interrupt 50-60 times per script run to give real-time feedback and 'lock in' fixes by repeating them multiple times
- Train every day with daily role-play; weekly game tape reviews with managers on specific areas to improve

## Framework
- Process (adherence to script) is above everyone; a team of 8s on 10/10 process beats team with one 10 and no process
- One star who doesn't follow process is a cultural cancer long-term

## Key Takeaway
- Sales is highly trainable skill; given proper training, you can turn anyone into a top performer
