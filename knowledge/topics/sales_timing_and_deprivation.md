# Sales Timing and Deprivation

> Sell at point of greatest deprivation, not greatest value. Build perceived gap between current state and desired state.

## Example
- Steak restaurant: sell two steaks when person says they're starving, not after they're full from first steak
- Pitch to people who feel poor (around wealthier peers), not to people who are satisfied

## How-To / Method
- Agitate the pain and extend the gap between where they are and where they want to be
- Motivation comes from perceived deprivation (in business world), not physiological deprivation
