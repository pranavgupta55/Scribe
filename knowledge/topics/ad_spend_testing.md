# Ad Spend Testing

> Minimum spend must be 2x target CAC to gather sufficient feedback cadence

## Key Numbers
- $2k target CAC requires $4k monthly test budget minimum
- Feedback cycle too slow on lower budgets (2-month feedback on $2k spend)

## Why It Works
- Higher spend provides statistically valid data for campaign iteration
- Allows proper optimization before pausing underperforming campaigns
