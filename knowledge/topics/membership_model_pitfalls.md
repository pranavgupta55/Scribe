# Membership Model Pitfalls

> Test upsell membership after emergency service; it leverages existing pain and relationship without model change.

## Framework
- Respond to emergency calls as before
- After job completion: upsell prevention membership
- Gate model switch on ability to close these upsells

## Key Takeaway
- Most venture-backed home services membership plays have not succeeded profitably
- Successful version: HVAC service + membership upsell (works well)
