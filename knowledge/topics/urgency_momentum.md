# Urgency & Momentum

> Offer dual paths with urgency: higher commission if they launch webinar within 30 days.

## Framework
- Plan A: 10% ongoing (standard)
- Plan B: $3K upfront + 2% ongoing IF webinar launched within 30 days
- Urgency drives first action; momentum builds from there

## Why It Works
- Affiliates who act in first 30 days see fastest success, most likely to continue
