# Entrepreneurial Mindset and Opportunity Cost

> Belief breaks when you first sell at premium price; family and peers will call it crazy because they don't operate at that scale; embracing this is entrepreneurial maturity.

## Key Takeaway
- Everyone (spouse, parents, peers) will say you're crazy when raising prices to premium levels
- Wealthier people also raised prices and ignored the critics; that's why they're wealthier

## Why It Works
- Selling to wealthy people requires mindset shift from cost-thinking to value-thinking; most people never make this shift
- Immersion in wealthy customer base gradually elevates your thinking, network, and opportunities

## Implications
- Don't ask poor people how to make money; they don't know where it is and will always advise cost-cutting
- Choice: listen to broke friends → stay broke, or listen to evidence → get rich
