# Long-Tail Distribution in Business

> Business outcomes follow a long-tail distribution unlike baseball; a few huge wins pay for hundreds of experiments.

## Framework
- Baseball has truncated outcomes: max 4 runs per swing
- Business has unlimited upside: can score thousand runs
- This asymmetry makes boldness and failure important

## Why It Works
- You swing for the fences and strike out a lot but also hit home runs
- The difference in returns between success and failure is so massive it pays for all attempts
