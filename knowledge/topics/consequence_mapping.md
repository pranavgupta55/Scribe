# Consequence Mapping

> Think two steps ahead: what happens after your action, then what happens after their reaction.

## How-To / Method
- Before writing a message, ask: what do I want to happen after I send this?
- Map the reaction to your action
- Adjust action to increase likelihood of desired second-order outcome

## Example
- Ghosting = slight negative; lying = false positive short-term, damage long-term; honest refusal = respected no for future
