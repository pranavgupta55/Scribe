# Product Positioning

> No one buys vague lifestyle change directly; they buy defined endpoints, then realize they need continuity.

## Examples
- Gym: no one says I want a lifestyle; they buy 6-week challenge or 28-day detox
- Tutoring: no one says I want year-long tutoring; they buy prep for summer or for exam season

## Implications
- Frame entry around outcome (pass exam, improve score), not duration
- Once in, they see momentum and believe in longer journey
