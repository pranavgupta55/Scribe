# Sales Motion Architecture

> Implement VSL (video sales letter), pre-frame qualification, and sales training cadence to move cold close rate from 6% toward 20%+

## How-To / Method
- Step 1: VSL explaining value before call
- Step 2: tight qualification framework (yes/no questions)
- Step 3: pre-call framing (set expectations)
- Step 4: sales team training on framework
- Step 5: measure and iterate VSL copy weekly
