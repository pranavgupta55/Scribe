# Regulatory Compliance

> Done-for-you direct response is gray/illegal at scale, franchises are regulated

## Framework
- Small done-for-you: not enforced by FTC
- Large done-for-you: enforcement target (high CAC, declining trust, compliance risk)
- Franchises: FTC-regulated, legal structure, both parties protected

## Key Takeaway
- Same marketing skills work for franchises but with compliance and legitimacy
