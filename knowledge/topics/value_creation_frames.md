# Value Creation & Frames

> Three intellectual attack vectors to design high-value offers: 100X price question, word-of-mouth scenario, unscalable-but-valuable frame.

## How-To / Method
- Frame 1: 'If I charged 10-100X, what would I include?' (write crazy ideas, remove hard costs, price the rest)
- Frame 2: 'If word-of-mouth only, what must customer experience be?' (design around viral potential)
- Frame 3: 'If all unscalable but 10X value, how?' (creative bundling of high-touch elements)

## Key Takeaway
- Many ideas you have for premium don't actually cost much to deliver
- Cross out hard costs; remaining elements are doable at higher price
