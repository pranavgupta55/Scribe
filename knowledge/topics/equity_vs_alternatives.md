# Equity vs. Alternatives

> Profit share + sale bonus achieves alignment without actual equity transfer and its permanent risk.

## Key Takeaway
- Never give real shares if you don't plan to sell; only guaranteed outcome is you stay in business
- Equity ties people to business forever; most people want cash flow and potential upside, not risk

## Framework
- Profit share: recurring cash off bottom line, treated as income
- Sale bonus: lump sum if/when you sell, can be structured as capital gains
- If they leave early: they lose both

- Instead of equity, give profit share plus sale bonus (both taxed differently); profit share comes off bottom line, sale bonus is paid on sale day — [never_give_real_equity_do_this_instead_lqKx0GDHFX8.txt]
