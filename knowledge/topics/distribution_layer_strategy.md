# Distribution Layer Strategy

> Move from selling 20 customers/year with 80% churn to recruiting distributors that send monthly compounding customers.

## Framework
- Acquire 1 agency/producer per month as distributor node
- Each sends 1 customer/month to you
- Month 1: 1 producer → 1 customer; Month 12: 12 producers → 12 new customers/month

## How-To
- Outreach to agency owners / producers directly
- Show them how much more money they make bundling your service into their package
- Focus on CAC to acquire distributor node, not customer acquisition

## Key Takeaway
- Distributor churn becomes primary metric, not customer churn
- Compounding: distributors stay longer, creating stackable customer flow
