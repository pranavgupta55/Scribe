# Case Study Close

> Case studies close RFP deals when there's a small price delta

## Framework
- Everyone reads RFP and thinks 'price mode'
- When they then see case study, if price delta is not huge, you get the win
- Must show case study of most similar past client showing total value delivered

## Key Numbers
- Even with anonymized customer, whitewash with meta details (location, industry, company size) to approach reasonable believability

## Claims
- Instead of competing on price in RFPs, lead with case study that shows biggest win on dimensions client cares about (speed, risk management) with meta details like location/industry if customer identity is confidential — [how_to_decommoditize_a_service_business_m8m7jbuIP4U.txt]
