# Launch Strategies

> Three-phase promotional sequence building anticipation, revealing value, then driving immediate action

## Framework
- Whisper (4-6 weeks out, every 2-3 weeks): build curiosity via behind-the-scenes, hints, mysterious mentions
- Tease (30 days to 3 days out, 1x weekly to 2x weekly): reveal product, show value elements, explain benefits
- Shout (3 days out, every 30 min until launch): pounds audience with bonuses, scarcity, urgency, guarantees, CTAs

## Examples
- Movie releases: 5-sec trailer year out → 30-sec trailer 90 days out → full trailer week before
- Book launch: show work-in-progress, drafts, frameworks → explain contents and benefits → daily reminders last 72 hours with bonus incentives

## Claims
- Launches use whisper-tease-shout method: create curiosity early (whisper every 4-6 weeks), reveal product details and value (tease weekly), then drive urgency (shout daily until launch) — [100m_leads.txt]
