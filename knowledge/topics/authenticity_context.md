# Authenticity & Context

> Authenticity = how you behave with no risk of punishment; truly 100% authentic is impossible if you follow any rules.

## Framework
- 100% authentic = same behavior alone as public
- Requires zero inclinations outside societal/legal rules
- Most people have impulses outside rules → <100% authentic

## Examples
- Walk around naked alone, don't walk around naked public
- Slightly inauthentic when clothed; that's normal
- Authenticity = spectrum, not binary

## Key Takeaway
- Once defined, productive conversation happens
- Without definition, people argue about concept instead of behavior
