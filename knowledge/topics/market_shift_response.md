# Market Shift Response

> Before pivoting, explore whether the problem is systemic or if other customer segments still have old conditions.

## Key Takeaway
- Changing entire model is highest-risk move; explore incremental alternatives first
- Ask: Are ALL carriers changing policies (systemic) or just some in your area?

## Method
- Test if customers with full coverage still exist elsewhere in U.S.
- Test ability to close same-day service with cash upfront
- Measure: Can you close if you have first contact?
