# Facebook Ads Optimization

> Mobile-first creative with minimal text and direct offer dominates; test 40+ variations.

## How-To / Method
- Ads must be readable at phone size (~18pt font minimum)
- Lead with offer: 'Get Your Free 18-Point Inspection'
- Use customer photos (happy, near HVAC unit) not product-only imagery
- Minimize text; remove branding/logo unless critical
- Run 40+ variations: white bg, blue bg, different images, text sizes

## Key Takeaway
- Best-performing Corey ad: photo of him + wife + 'cleaning experts' text
- Organic Instagram posts can be repurposed as ads (append 5sec CTA)
- Algorithm pre-tests; good organic posts = good ad performers

## Caveats
- Do not design ads on desktop; text/elements size inaccurately
- CMO should text creative as phone image for QA before launch
