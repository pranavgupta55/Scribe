# Nail It Then Scale It Framework

> Perfect location 1 before opening location 2

## Framework
- Step 1: hire GM for current location
- Step 2: shadow for 3-6 months while owner does location scouting
- Step 3: owner fully removes self from day-to-day
- Step 4: measure if location sustains/grows without owner

## Why It Matters
- Extra 6 months now saves 4+ years fixing expansion mistakes later
- Overexpanding is harder to undo than scaling carefully
