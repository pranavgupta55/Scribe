# Framing Life as Happening For You

> You can live without regret by believing you had to go through that to get where you are now.

## Framework
- How could I have known that then? I had to go through it to know
- If I had known and been better off, I'd still be about as dissatisfied
- Everything that happened led to now; frame as necessary

## Why It Works
- Removes regret by reframing necessity
- Allows you to be present and grateful
- Stops comparing to counterfactual better timeline
