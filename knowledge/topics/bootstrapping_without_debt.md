# Bootstrapping Without Debt

> Build your first $100K entirely from cutting costs and reinvesting, avoiding debt entirely.

## Framework
- Step 1: Cut all costs to create cash flow
- Step 2: Save time to have capacity
- Step 3: Research what people pay for
- Step 4: Learn through iteration
- Step 5: Strategically reinvest money
- Step 6: Never increase lifestyle

## Why It Works
- No debt means no monthly obligations holding you back
- All cash flow can be reinvested not servicing debt
- Creates true financial flexibility at the $100K milestone

## Connections
- Both advocate building businesses from personal savings and reinvested profits without external capital or debt. _(connects to: Bootstrapped Business Model)_
