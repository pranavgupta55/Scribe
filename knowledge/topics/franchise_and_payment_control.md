# Franchise and Payment Control

> Better franchises control money flow, get paid first, then remit remainder to franchisees.

## Framework
- Control of money flow is a massive advantage
- Payment processors always get their slice first, then remit to merchants
- This ensures you get paid whether the franchisee succeeds or not

## Why It Works
- You're paid regardless of downstream outcomes
- Cash flow control equals leverage
