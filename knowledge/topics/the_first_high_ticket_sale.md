# The First High-Ticket Sale

> The moment you sell something you believed no one would buy at that price is the moment your business shifts.

## Key Numbers
- Usual price: $500 gym memberships (16 weeks, 3x/week)
- First high-ticket call: $6,000 (12x multiple vs. prior price)
- Expected reaction: he'll say no
- Actual reaction: 'done.'

## Examples
- First call: $6K → yes → $60K in morning across 8 calls
- Second call: $8K → yes
- Third call: $10K → yes
- By end of day: $60K collected with no product built yet

## Implications
- This one realization—that people would say yes to 12X price—changed the entire trajectory
- In one month, made more than 3.5 years of prior model
- The belief barrier was holding back all upside
