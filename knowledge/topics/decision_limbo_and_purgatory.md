# Decision Limbo and Purgatory

> Decision limbo is purgatory; you trade years of life without getting anything in return.

## Key Takeaway
- Many people sit on edge in decision limbo
- They trade the time they have but get nothing for it
- This is worse than consciously making bad trade

## Framework
- Get conscious of what you're trading
- Get conscious what you're trading it for
- Better to trade and get value than sit paralyzed
