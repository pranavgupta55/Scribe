# Landing Pages and CTAs

> Single-page conversion tool capturing contact info and driving specific next-step actions

## How-To / Method
- Match landing page visually and verbally to driving ad (consistency increases second action completion)
- Use simple, obvious design; test three templates (focused headline, long-form, multi-step)
- CTAs must be crystal clear and easy: short URLs, obvious buttons, simple language

## Framework
- Cialdini consistency principle: remind user of action they just took, show how second action aligns with it

## Claims
- Landing pages must match ads visually and verbally to maintain consistency from click to conversion; consistency thinking increases second-action completion rates — [100m_leads.txt]
