# Customer Behavior Change

> Strong branding directly changes what customers choose and how much they'll pay.

## Key Takeaway
- Two identical white t-shirts: one sells 6x more volume at 12x the price
- Difference is branding, not product quality

## Why It Works
- Branding drives preference formation
- Preference drives willingness to pay premium and loyalty

- Warren Buffett: It takes 20 years to build a reputation and 5 minutes to ruin it; if you think about that, you'll do things differently — [what_branding_actually_is_and_how_to_build_one_fast_Vt4D1qgo9ic.txt]
- Strong brand paired with ideal customer creates three cascading benefits: 10x or more price premium, 6x better advertising response, and customer lifetime value through repeated buying — [what_branding_actually_is_and_how_to_build_one_fast_Vt4D1qgo9ic.txt]

## Connections
- Strong branding changes customer choice and willingness to pay; translates directly to profit. _(connects to: Branding Economics Pricing Power)_
- Strong branding changes customer willingness to pay by commanding premium pricing and perceived value. _(connects to: Premium Positioning)_
- Branding works by deliberately pairing your offer with desired associations until customers internalize those connections as behavior-shaping beliefs. _(connects to: What Branding Is)_
