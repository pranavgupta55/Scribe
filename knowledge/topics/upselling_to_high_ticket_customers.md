# Upselling to High-Ticket Customers

> First high-ticket sale breaks belief that high prices don't work; price sets themselves, not the market; once you sell at high price, client expectations shift upward.

## Key Takeaway
- Founder's first $6K sale broke limiting belief that $500 was the max; had 8 calls after, yielding $60K in collected by day end
- Didn't have product built yet; client said yes because founder believed in price and projected confidence

## How-To / Method
- Set a price you expect rejection on; client surprise (saying yes) breaks your limiting belief
- Went from selling $500 weight loss packages to $6K gym launches (12X jump); next calls: $8K, $10K, $10K, etc.

## Examples
- Example: 8 gym launches in 8 calls = $6K + $8K + $10K + $10K + $12K + $10K + $10K + $12K = $88K collected
- Once you've sold at high price once, subsequent sales feel easier; framework is proven
