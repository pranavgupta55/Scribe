# Cost of Change Framework

> 20% guaranteed cost means only pursue changes with 50%+ upside

## Framework
- Every change causes 20% performance decrease initially
- This is a guaranteed cost of change, period
- Only accept 20% dip if you see 50% or double upside potential

## Why It Works
- Prevents constant tweaking that keeps business below potential
- Founder observes this pattern repeatedly across different businesses
- Allows for faster decision-making (both paths up the mountain will work, so pick one and move)

## Implications
- Explains why businesses stall between stages of growth—too many small changes with insufficient upside
- Most founders don't realize they're constantly incurring 20% costs with <20% upside
