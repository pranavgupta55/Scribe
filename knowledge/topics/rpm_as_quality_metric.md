# RPM as Quality Metric

> Revenue per 1,000 views (RPM) is superior to raw views for measuring business value.

## Framework
- RPM = (views × CPM) / 1,000
- Pairs quantity (views) with quality (audience spending power)
- Leading indicator of book sales, opt-ins, applications

## Key Numbers
- 90 days entertainment content: 2-3x views but 50% ad revenue drop
- High RPM month correlates with business application increase

## Why It Works
- Business audiences have high CPM (high purchase power)
- RPM captures both reach and audience quality simultaneously
