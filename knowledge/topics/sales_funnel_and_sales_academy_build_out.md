# Sales Funnel and Sales Academy Build-Out

> Scale home services by building parallel funnel for salespeople (app gen → nurture → interview → onboard → retain), matching demand gen structure

## Framework
- Demand funnel: lead gen → nurture → sales → onboard → retention/ascension
- Sales funnel (parallel): app gen → app nurture → interview → onboard → retention/ascension

## How-To / Method
- Step 1: Define what you measure in demand (CTR, opt-in rate, close rate, show rate)
- Step 2: Apply same metrics to sales recruiting (app rate, interview rate, offer rate, acceptance rate)

## Key Numbers
- Fence company (5 salespeople): each closes ~$100K gross profit; doubling to 10 adds $1M GPP
- Sales compensation: $200K for top-tier salespeople in home services at $20M scale
