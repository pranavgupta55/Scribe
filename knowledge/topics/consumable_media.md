# Consumable Media

> Build recurring revenue by providing monthly inputs customers need to use their education.

## Framework
- Consumable = something used and consumed repeatedly (not one-time education)
- Community, friendship, accountability are consumable by nature
- Pre-vetted ads, properties, trending products, accountability structures become valuable consumables

## Examples
- Gym franchise owners: tested ad pool refreshed monthly
- Wholesaling students: pre-vetted property list emailed each month
- Print-on-demand community: trending products list updated weekly/monthly

## Claims
- In gym advertising, the company tested 100 ads across representative markets, found the top 5 winners, and handed them to franchisees to run, eliminating testing costs and labor of ad creation. — [your_business_needs_consumable_media_Pa1XNVGhzOI.txt]
- In wholesaling education, the instructor had his team find, sort, and rate properties monthly, delivering a PDF of pre-vetted deal properties to students who only needed to visit and make offers. — [your_business_needs_consumable_media_Pa1XNVGhzOI.txt]

## Connections
- Provide recurring monthly inputs customers use; recurring consumption anchors members and enables recurring pricing. _(connects to: Consumable Products)_
