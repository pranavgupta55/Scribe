# Team & Personal Life

> Discretionary effort requires supportive home life; leaders enable rest and create conditions for team to achieve potential.

## Framework
- Unsupportive spouse/partner = diminished professional potential (widespread, not rare)
- Good home front = full brain access for 'the hunt'
- Unsupportive environment → cutting corners, avoiding bigger projects, smaller goals

## Key Takeaway
- Hard workers will run themselves into the ground; leader must visibly grant permission to rest
- Gauge when people are close to cracking; proactively tell them to take day/weekend/unplug
- Making history is rare; unlock discretionary effort by supporting team members' home environments

## Examples
- Told team during book launch: you will be able to say you made history when looking back
