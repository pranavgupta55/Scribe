# Centers of Influence

> Real customers are CPAs and financial advisors, not companies; they refer to companies

## Key Takeaway
- 170+ affiliate partners exist; only sending 1 customer/month total
- Most partners aren't active; need launch + integration strategy

## Framework
- Launch: get 10 referrals from partner to build internal case study
- Integration: plug into partner's onboarding so all new customers become referrals

## Method
- Reframe customer acquisition around centers of influence
- Build sales messaging for COIs (cost to acquire them, not cost per end customer)
- Provide warm handoffs; build integrations into their process

## Why It Works
- COIs already have customer relationships; founder just plugs in
- If all 170 send 1/month, that's $100M business
