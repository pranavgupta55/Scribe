# Upside in Uncertainty

> The biggest gains exist in territories everyone else avoids due to fear.

## Key Takeaway
- You can only go to zero - the downside is bounded
- Upside is unlimited in uncertain spaces where others won't compete

## Why It Works
- When everyone else is unwilling to bear uncertainty, you create arbitrage

## Claims
- The biggest gains come from the unknown upside; you can only go to zero, but the likelihood of going to zero as a regular person is almost zero. — [humans_overestimate_the_downside_I8YZLBYYMag.txt]
