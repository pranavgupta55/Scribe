# Brand Building

> Only way out of performance marketing trap is building brand to command premium and retain customers

## Key Takeaway
- 70% of advertising should be brand-driven, not direct response
- Brand is learnable: making positive associations with your product

## Method
- Rebranding work with branding agency
- Master product quality (founder must own, can't outsource)
- Build founder story, narrative, unique differentiation
- Top-of-funnel awareness tied to things audience cares about

## Why It Works
- Defensible (can't arbitrage your way into brand)
- Allows higher prices
- Increases repeat purchase rate
- Attracts better talent
