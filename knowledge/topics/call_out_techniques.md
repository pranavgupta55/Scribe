# Call-Out Techniques

> Attention-grabbing mechanisms that make audiences notice an advertisement in crowded environments

## Key Numbers
- First 5 seconds determine entire ad effectiveness (David Ogilvy: headline = 80% of ad dollar)
- Call-out improvements can 2-10x engagement

## Framework
- Verbal: labels, yes-questions, if-then, ridiculous results
- Nonverbal: contrast (bright colors, movement), likeness (people like you), scene-setting (familiar environment)

## How-To / Method
- Test 30+ call-out variations with 10 ad bodies = 300 creative combinations
- Use local area + person type for highest impact ('Clark County Moms,' 'Gym Owners,' etc.)
- Show spokesperson who looks, talks, acts familiar to target audience

## Claims
- Ad call-outs should harness the cocktail party effect using labels, yes-questions, if-then statements, ridiculous results, contrast, likeness, or scene-setting to cut through noise — [100m_leads.txt]
