# Resilience Building

> You're living proof every day that you've survived everything you've been through.

## Key Takeaway
- Most people don't give themselves credit for survival
- Frame: "I have not died; therefore I can handle this"

## Implications
- Day you can't stand it anymore, you won't have to—but proof exists that you can

## Claims
- You have only worked as hard as you've ever worked; the frame "I can stand it because I am not dead. I am living proof" gives evidence that you've survived worse. — [you_re_not_winning_you_re_just_getting_used_to_pain_IxqZEmO9WUQ.txt]

- Resilience measures recovery speed from hardship; survival is proof of capability. _(connects to: Resilience Definition)_
