# Owner Presence & Culture

> Daily presence from leadership dramatically increases retention; absence signals indifference and triggers churn.

## How-To / Method
- Set daily alarm to show up for customers
- Clear notifications, handle reports, respond to issues
- Ask daily: 'What am I doing today to show up for my customers?'
- Encourage good behavior (reviews, testimonials) publicly
- Delete bad behavior and remove 'cancers' from community

## Key Takeaway
- If member feels owner isn't active, they leave
- Never seen this rule fail
