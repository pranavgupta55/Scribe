# Email Reactivation Campaigns

> Monthly email themes (savings, allergies, case studies) with split-tested subject lines drive 20-30% revenue lift.

## How-To / Method
- 3-6 mini campaigns per month (2-3 emails per theme, not 1 email = 6 emails)
- Themes: (1) 'I owe you $175' upsell, (2) Allergy angle, (3) Savings angle, (4) Environment/health
- Follow-up sequence: stats email → case study email (e.g., 'Casey went from $600/mo to $150/mo')
- Split test subject lines ('I owe you' vs. standard); track open/response rates weekly

## Key Numbers
- Can drive 20-30% revenue lift from existing customer base
- Revenue from reactivation is all profit (no CAC)
- Compounding effect: once set up, runs indefinitely with revenue growth

## Key Takeaway
- Request customer utility bills (last 12 months) to calculate ROI (e.g., 'Pays for itself in 18 months')
- Highlight 'at no cost to you' in bold/underline (people scan email)
- Best angles: savings (quantified), allergy relief, health benefits
