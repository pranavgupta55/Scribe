# Patience as Behavior

> Patience is action taken despite impatience, not an emotional state.

## Key Takeaway
- You may want the outcome today or yesterday, but putting the penny on the scale is patience
- Feeling impatient while waiting patiently still counts as patience

## Claims
- Patience is not feeling patient; it's doing the patient thing despite not feeling patient - same with courage and other traits. — [reflecting_on_why_i_was_wrong_about_myself_patience_self_jud_ezN8B1d1RVw.txt]
