# AI as Cost Advantage Not Feature

> Keep AI efficiency secret, sell outcome not method

## Key Takeaway
- People don't care HOW ads are made, only if they get customers
- Consider AI a secret - sell $500K commercial quality at discount
- Don't lead with 'AI' angle, leads with quality/speed/price

## Example
- Company had DIY ad tool, struggled; rebranded as agency service with efficient model, won - because model mattered more than tooling
