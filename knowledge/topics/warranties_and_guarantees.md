# Warranties and Guarantees

> You can sell risk protection in any business by offering warranties for cost premiums.

## Examples
- AppleCare insurance program for cracked screens
- Home service warranties for delayed or difficult repairs
- Service business guarantees if something goes wrong or delayed

## Why It Works
- You inject elements of risk-taking into your business model
- Customers pay premium for peace of mind
- Apple gets paid repeatedly for essentially giving air
