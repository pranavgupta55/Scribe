# Discount Offers

> Massive discounts (50%+) generate lead volume while collecting payment upfront; most powerful for two-step sales via card-on-file upsells.

## Key Takeaway
- Discount offers are legally compliant in regulated markets where free is forbidden
- Four ways to display discounts; test which display format resonates with your market
- Best suited for well-understood services (dentists, gyms, chiropractors) where customers know price ranges

## Claims
- Discount offers eliminate 85-90% of no-shows by requiring payment upfront, critical for service businesses — [100m_leads_2_bonus_chapters.txt]
- Massive discounts (50%+ off) drive behavior; marginal discounts (5-25%) merely cut margin without generating real action — [100m_leads_2_bonus_chapters.txt]

## Connections
- Discount Offers and Free Offers sit at opposite ends of the lead-generation spectrum, with Free Offers generating 9x more responses than paid offers but Free Offers requiring higher subsequent pricing to justify CAC differences. _(connects to: Free Offers)_
- Attraction Offers reduce initial purchase friction through discounts or free offers to turn strangers into customers, with Discount Offers proving that massive (50%+) discounts generate volume while marginal ones just cut margin. _(connects to: Attraction Offers)_
