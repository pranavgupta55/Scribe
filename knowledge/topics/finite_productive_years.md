# Finite Productive Years

> You have ~4–5 big "seasons" of major business runs left in your career; use them intentionally

## Framework
- Warren Buffett punch-card analogy: 20 punches, can't sell them, pick carefully
- Same logic applies to entrepreneurs: ~4–5 major business runs possible

## Implications
- Can't do many things well; pick the one thing and commit
- Saying no to good opportunities is necessary
