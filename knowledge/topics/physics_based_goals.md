# Physics-Based Goals

> Work backwards from physics and math, not from industry norms or what's been done before.

## Framework
- Ask: what physical law prevents us from doing this
- If no physical law blocks it, treat constraints as mental handicaps
- Industry standards are gifts—let competitors use them

## Examples
- Steve Jobs: phone book on desk, has to fit in there; others said never done before
- Elon Musk: break bolts down to physics, why can't we do this on manufacturing line
- Book launch: if power is needed get generators; internet get backups; books get printing; shipping get 100s of people
