# Anchor Upsell

> Present expensive premium option first to anchor perception; offer reasonable alternative if customer balks

## Framework
- Three steps: present anchor (5-10x premium), get the gasp, offer rescue (main offer)
- Premium and main offers share primary features; premium has different secondary (luxury) features

## How-To / Method
- Anchor first, main second; this order gets more main sales than main-only
- Anchor must be legitimately offered, not fake; customer must genuinely consider it

## Key Numbers
- $16k suit to $2.2k suit example: 5x anchoring gets customer to spend 5x their $500 budget

## Claims
- Anchor Upsells present a premium offer at 5-10x the price first, expecting customer hesitation ('the gasp'), then offering a moderately-priced alternative that appears as a better deal. — [100m_money_models.txt]
