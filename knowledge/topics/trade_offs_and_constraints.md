# Trade-Offs and Constraints

> You cannot have it all; every life choice has trade-offs and you must consciously decide what to sacrifice.

## Examples
- House in ski town, near beach, secluded, walkable, cozy, spacious, quiet—impossible
- Married vs single, kids vs no kids, sacrifice 20s vs sacrifice 30s

## Framework
- Every choice is a trade; know cost and value
- 1% outcome requires 1% trade-off
- Trade inherently good or bad depends on person and context
