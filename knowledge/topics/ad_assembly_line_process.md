# Ad Assembly Line Process

> Chunk ad creation into separate production phases to enable rapid, scalable output of hundreds of variations weekly.

## Framework
- Phase 1: Write 50 hooks (80% of prep time)
- Phase 2: Record 3-5 meat variations (20% of prep time)
- Phase 3: Record 1-3 CTAs (~0% prep time)
- Math: 50 hooks × 3-5 meat × 1-3 CTAs = 150-750 ads per week

## How-To / Method
- Hooks take 1-2 hours to write
- Meat takes ~1 hour to record
- CTAs take ~5 minutes to record
- Rest of time devoted to preparation

## Key Numbers
- Portfolio founder scaled from 5 ads/month to 600 ads/month
- Revenue doubled in two quarters using this process
- One weight loss company saturated after 5-8k per day ad spend until increasing ad quantity
