# Partner Channel Model

> Sell to fitness coaches/mentors who have existing client bases to upsell to

## Framework
- Acquire fitness business coaches as resellers
- They maintain client relationships and sticky revenue
- Sell them stickier recurring model vs their current course-based model

## Key Numbers
- Agency CAC: $5k
- Agency onboarding fee: $25k
- Per-customer fee: $1k x ~50 customers = $50k
- Recurring: 30% of their top-line revenue

## Claims
- Target partner channels (fitness business coaches) rather than individual coaches to avoid lead generation dependency — [how_do_i_make_my_crm_sexy_on_the_front_end_IKRBA9ALc68.txt]
- Agencies as customers can acquire for $5k CAC, then onboard at $25k + $1k per customer (50 customer average), plus 30% recurring revenue — [how_do_i_make_my_crm_sexy_on_the_front_end_IKRBA9ALc68.txt]

## Connections
- Partnering with coaches who have client bases to upsell to mirrors affiliate dynamics of introducing for commission. _(connects to: Affiliate Model)_
