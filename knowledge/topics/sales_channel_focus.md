# Sales Channel Focus

> Pick one repeatable channel and do way more of it instead of spreading thin across four channels.

## Key Takeaway
- Owner tests: referrals, email, LinkedIn, social, BNI
- Referrals = no action item
- One of the four repeatable channels works; do more of it

## Claims
- Company does $1M/year, wants $10M; currently tests referrals, email cold outreach, LinkedIn, social DMs, BNI networking — [pick_one_sales_channel_or_stay_broke_Yjt_YfzF1kQ.txt]
- Referrals can't scale; no action item to do more. Pick one of the repeatable channels: email, LinkedIn, or social — [pick_one_sales_channel_or_stay_broke_Yjt_YfzF1kQ.txt]

## Connections
- Single-channel focus beats multi-channel chaos; choose channel matching team skill and separate inbound from outbound. _(connects to: Lead Generation And Sales Strategy)_
- Multi-channel local success requires paid ads short-term and content long-term; multi-channel at small scale spreads resources too thin. _(connects to: Multi Channel Acquisition Strategy)_
- Narrow content to business topics to attract owners; pick one repeatable channel and do way more. _(connects to: Business Focused Content Strategy)_
