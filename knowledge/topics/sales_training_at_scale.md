# Sales Training at Scale

> Daily group role-play by script stage keeps entire team sharp without requiring individual coaching

## How-To / Method
- Every morning, team separates into groups by stage
- Role-play the specific part of script that's hardest
- Rotate through 5 parts across the week, repeat hot topic

## Why It Works
- Group setting is cheaper than 1-on-1 coaching
- Keeps entire sales motion sharp, not just top performers
- Identifies weak spots in real time

## Framework
- Breaks sales into discrete, repeatable parts
- Each part has specific language/flow to master
