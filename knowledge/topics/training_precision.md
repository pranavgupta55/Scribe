# Training Precision

> Break amorphous traits into specific observable behaviors; train each micro-behavior vs. abstract trait.

## Examples
- Don't train 'be confident'; train eye contact, nodding, repeating back, taking calculated risks
- Don't say 'stop being a dick'; specify interrupting in meetings, telling people how to do jobs
- Don't say 'be lazy'; say 'respond within 5 minutes and prepare meeting notes'

## Why It Works
- Gives person something concrete to do/not do
- Behavior change is measurable
- Much higher success rate than abstract feedback
