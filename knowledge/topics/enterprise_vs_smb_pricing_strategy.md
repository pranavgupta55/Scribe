# Enterprise vs SMB Pricing Strategy

> Skip enterprise, target mid-market with volume model

## Framework
- Enterprise (500K/year): slow sales cycle, takes forever
- Mid-market ($100K+/month ad spend): fast, high-volume needs
- Qualifications: 1yr profitable ads, $1M/year spend, proven funnel

## Why It Works
- Mid-market has immediate pain (need 50-100 creative pieces)
- Enterprise single-ad thinking doesn't match high-volume reality
