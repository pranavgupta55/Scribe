# Branded Search Term Defense

> Bid on your brand name and variations to prevent competitors from scooping customers.

## Framework
- Own all branded terms: company name, owner name, branded variations (e.g., 'Hormozy', 'Alex Hormozy', 'Acquisition.com')
- Branded keyword ROAS is typically best in account
- Even with strong organic search, competitors can outbid you on your name

## Key Takeaway
- Prevents competitor ads on your branded terms
- Helps customers find you if they already know your name
- Profit per branded click is usually highest because high intent + zero brand education needed
