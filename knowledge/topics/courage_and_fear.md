# Courage and Fear

> Courage is doing the thing despite fear; cowardice is not doing despite wanting to.

## Framework
- Courage is unfakeable - either you do it afraid or you don't

## Claims
- Courage is unfakeable emotion per Jocko Willink - doing the thing despite fear is courage; not doing it despite wanting to is cowardice. — [reflecting_on_why_i_was_wrong_about_myself_patience_self_jud_ezN8B1d1RVw.txt]

## Connections
- Courage manifests as both willingness to act despite fear and speed of action when risk appears. _(connects to: Courage Risk Interval)_
- Courage acts despite fear; fear energy is wasted when you prove imagined scenarios wrong. _(connects to: Fear As Illusion)_
- Action comes before confidence; courage is doing despite fear while cowardice is not doing despite wanting to. _(connects to: Action Decoupled From Feeling)_
