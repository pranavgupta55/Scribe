# Cobblers Shoes Problem

> Agency too busy serving clients to market itself

## Key Numbers
- Current: $4.5M revenue
- Goal: $1M monthly ($12M+ annually)
- Margins: 18-20%
- Cash flow: ~$700K annually

## Why It Works
- Supply is the constraint, not demand
- Agency has more demand than capacity
- Founder personally doing marketing (ad sets, creative, phone work)
