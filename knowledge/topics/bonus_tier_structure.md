# Bonus Tier Structure

> Offer meaningful bonuses at three price rungs to incentivize higher purchase; no max price cap.

## Framework
- Three tiers (small $30, medium $67, large $99+) with clear operational differences
- Higher tiers require more staff effort (e.g., hand-buff, interior, guarantee)
- Keep high-operational-cost bonuses for paying tier; free tier low-touch only
