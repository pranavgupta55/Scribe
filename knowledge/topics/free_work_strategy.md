# Free Work Strategy

> Offer free calls as low-risk entry point; convert to paid after demonstrating value.

## Framework
- Pitch: five free one-on-one calls
- After fifth call: offer paid engagement at X dollars
- Lever to get visibility: tell people you work with them, offer free calls

## Why It Works
- Client has zero financial risk
- You have skin in the game (need to impress)

## Claims
- Offer five free one-on-one calls; at the end of the fifth, they can keep working with you for free or for X dollars—this is an easy way to get in front of people. — [how_to_get_your_first_clients_with_zero_proof_QgvX_5km_Yo.txt]
