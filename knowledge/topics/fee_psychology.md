# Fee Psychology

> Made-up fees work because they increase investment and commitment; transparent about invention creates trust

## Key Takeaway
- Invent high fee, then waive for all customers; they feel grateful for discount that never existed
- Transparent about reason builds credibility ('onboarding fee' vs hidden extraction)

## How-To / Method
- Invent fee with clear name (setup, onboarding, implementation, etc.)
- Explain reason to customer
- Can waive or discount; psychological benefit comes from existence + gesture of waiving

## Examples
- Pitch: 'Normally $1,000 setup fee, but I'm waiving it today—you just pay the discounted first month'
- Benefit: customer thinks they got $1,000 discount; you controlled the frame
