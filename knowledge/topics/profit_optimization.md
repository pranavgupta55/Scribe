# Profit Optimization

> Shift from top-line growth to bottom-line expansion is more valuable

## Key Takeaway
- $15M at 30% net = $4.5M owner benefit vs. $40M at 15% net = $6M
- Growth without margin improvement wastes resources and creates chaos

## Key Numbers
- Current: $15M × 14% = $2.1M net
- Target: $15M × 30% = $4.5M net (114% increase with no revenue growth)
