# Pricing and LTV-CAC Dynamics for SMBs

> Go extreme: either super cheap ($400/month, automated) or high-touch ($5K+, sophisticated customers); never the middle

## Framework
- Low model: $400/month, fully automated delivery, very high CAC recovery speed
- High model: $5K+, high-touch, serve businesses with proven metrics and sales ops

## Key Numbers
- Chiropractor at $2.4M, 30% margin, $600K profit should prioritize pricing restructure before ad scaling
- WAAS at $450/month × 10 sales/month = $100K/month revenue, then stalls without LTV or unit growth

## How-To / Method
- Step 1: Analyze unit economics (LTV, CAC, stick time); identify if churn zone
- Step 2: Decide: go cheap + automate, or go premium + narrow customer (sophistication requirement)
