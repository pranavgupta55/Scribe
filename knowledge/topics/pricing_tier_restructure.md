# Pricing Tier Restructure

> Replace fixed-quantity tiers with base + usage ($199 + $100/article); aligns cost with consumption and prevents churn in slow months

## How-To / Method
- Old: $1K for 10 OR $2.5K for unlimited
- New: $199/month base + $100 per article published
- Low enough agencies don't churn bad months; pays you for volume

## Why It Works
- Base tier absorbs volatility
- Usage tier captures upside
- Agencies stay because minimum cost is palatable

## Claims
- Current pricing: $1K month for 10 articles OR unlimited for $2.5K; one agency publishes 10/month, another 900/month—no usage component means margin inversions — [how_to_scale_a_white_label_agency_past_1_5m_0coMtm_i1UA.txt]
- Suggested fix: lower base tier ($199/mo) plus usage component ($100/article) so SMBs don't churn in bad months but pay more for volume — [how_to_scale_a_white_label_agency_past_1_5m_0coMtm_i1UA.txt]
