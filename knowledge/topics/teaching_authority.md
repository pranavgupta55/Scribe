# Teaching Authority

> Don't need to operate the perfect version of your business to teach others; only need advice that works

## Framework
- Customers evaluate: will this advice make me money?
- Not: is this guy currently running the best version?

## Examples
- Gym Launch: best gym was $650k/year, average was $600k; taught students to exceed both
- Hope everyone makes more money than founder did locally

- Customers only care if your advice will make them money, not whether you currently operate the best version of your own business — [the_real_reason_i_can_t_scale_two_businesses_at_once_kp68MDBTpWY.txt]
