# Customer Referral Programs

> Incentive structure and goodwill systems that turn happy customers into lead generators

## Key Numbers
- If referrals > churn: business grows without advertising
- If referrals = churn: needs other advertising to break even
- If referrals < churn: trapped paying high CAC
- Dropbox 39x growth in 15 months with free storage incentive; PayPal reached 100M users in 6 years

## Framework
- Seven asking methods: one-sided benefit ($CAC to referrer or friend), two-sided (PayPal/Dropbox model), ask at purchase, negotiation chip, referral events, ongoing program, unlockable bonuses

## How-To / Method
- Make goodwill via: better customer quality, lower expectations, faster results, improved product, new upsells
- Pay for referrals based on maximum CAC; three-tier structure optimizes payouts
- Match incentive to product (free tshirts for tshirt business, not IT services)

## Examples
- Three-way introduction with picture of gift card personalized with friend's name increases perception of value
- Prestige Labs: free products for affiliates selling 3+ per month creates 'athlete sponsorship' status

## Claims
- Referral programs work best with dual-sided incentives like Dropbox and PayPal used: reward both referrer and referred friend; offer 10-20% of annual affiliate revenue as training/certification cost — [100m_leads.txt]
