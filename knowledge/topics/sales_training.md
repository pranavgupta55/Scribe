# Sales Training

> Most businesses don't train daily; they hire and hope; actual training cadence is close to zero.

## Key Takeaway
- 97% of businesses don't know how to systematically train sales
- If you have the right script, you just reinforce saying right thing at right time

## Method
- Daily training: reinforce script, objection handling, discovery questions
- Role-play: have reps practice on each other
- Track: which questions get highest close rate; reinforce those
