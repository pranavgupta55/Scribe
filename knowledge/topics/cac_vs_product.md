# CAC vs Product

> Reframe premium spend as product marketing, not traditional CAC

## Key Takeaway
- Premium on ingredients, systems, and service IS the marketing
- CAC = cost to acquire through product quality

## Why It Works
- Word-of-mouth is driven by exceptional product, not ads
- Better to invest in product than acquisition spend
