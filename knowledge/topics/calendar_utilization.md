# Calendar Utilization

> Target 70% sales calendar utilization; too high kills conversion, too low kills morale.

## Framework
- 70% utilization (sweet spot): maximum conversion, pipeline work, convenience for prospects
- Don't exceed 85%: reduces show rates, prevents follow-up, kills close rates
- Don't drop below 60%: team loses momentum, morale suffers

## Why It Works
- Fully booked = inconvenient times, further-out appointments, lower show rates
- Empty calendar = no momentum, desperation in tone
- 70% balances conversions, pipeline development, and team morale

## Action
- When booked solid: hire more salespeople (never made less money doing this)
- When under-booked: train on pipeline/follow-up work, don't reduce team
