# Ai Ads

AI Ads are designed to generate targeted advertisements for chiropractors, leveraging generative AI models (GBTs) to create ad copy, headlines, and descriptions. The tool offers a free website and cloud platform, enabling users to optimize ad sets targeting Canada and America, with specific focus on 140 Anytime Fitnesses and three Basecamp Fitnesses. AI systems follow up with interested clients instantly, boosting conversion rates by 400% compared to waiting 42 hours, while reducing human intervention and workload. The AI’s outreach strategy, including budget optimization and cold outreach, has proven effective, generating low-cost leads (6.72 USD/month) and 341 leads for $1.86 through generated ads. However, the speaker emphasizes that AI cannot fully automate closing deals, requiring critical thinking, and that five AI workers can replace entire departments without time or money for updates.  

Contradictions arise between claims of AI’s effectiveness and limitations. While AI tools generate high response rates and lead generation, the speaker asserts that closing deals requires human judgment and cannot be fully automated. Additionally, the claim that AI can replace departments without updates conflicts with the note that sending thousands of messages does not lead to more customers. Despite these tensions, the AI’s follow-up process and ad set targeting demonstrate its potential to optimize revenue, even as challenges remain in fully automating complex sales tasks.
