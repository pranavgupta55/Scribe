# Value Equation

> Four levers determine perceived value: dream outcome and likelihood of achievement (increase), time delay and effort/sacrifice (decrease).

## Framework
- Value = (Dream Outcome + Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay + Effort & Sacrifice)
- If bottom equals zero, value approaches infinity
- Perception matters more than reality; prospects must perceive changes
- Focus on decreasing bottom drivers (hard stuff) rather than inflating top drivers

## Components
- Dream Outcome: gap between current reality and desired future
- Perceived Likelihood: conviction that the offer will work for them
- Time Delay: duration from purchase to benefit realization
- Effort & Sacrifice: tangible and intangible costs required
