# Founder Selection Bias

> Acquiring small businesses assumes you can easily scale them - usually wrong

## Common Mistakes
- Small businesses have 100 problems at once
- Founder got to that level for a reason - likely is the limiting factor
- Removing founder fails (PE buyout → business tanks), keeping founder fails (they won't scale)

## Implications
- Why private equity buys bigger, proven businesses instead of turnarounds
- Easier to find good manager for proven business than to build business from scratch
