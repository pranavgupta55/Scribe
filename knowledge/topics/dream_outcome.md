# Dream Outcome

> The desired end state or feeling a prospect wants; usually involves status increase within their social or professional group.

## Key Takeaway
- Everyone wants status increase; frame benefits in terms of how others will perceive them
- Multiple vehicles may deliver same dream outcome (fitness, surgery, supplements)
- When two offerings have same dream outcome, other value drivers determine price difference
- Status-driven desires (making money) typically valued more than appearance-driven ones

## Framework
- Identify the deeper desire (not just surface feature)
- Position your offer as the fastest, easiest, most status-elevating vehicle
- Use social proof and peer validation to reinforce status gain narrative
- Connect outcomes to how prospects will be perceived by others

## Claims
- The dream outcome is the gap between current reality and dreams; status-driven desires motivate purchases more than other desires. — [100m_offers.txt]
