# Generalization Across Domains

> Skill acquisition mechanisms transfer across completely different domains

## Examples
- Saxophone fingering drills (500 repetitions) = tennis forehands (500 repetitions) = content creation (500 tweets + 400 posts)
- Once you recognize the underlying pattern, you can apply the same persistence strategy to any domain

## Claims
- Learning how to try becomes a generalizable skill across domains; skills learned in saxophone transfer to tennis, which transfer to content creation (500 tweets and 400 posts work the same as 500 forehands and 500 backhands) — [how_learning_to_try_one_thing_beats_99_of_people_unkbSx2WZH0.txt]
