# Key Man Risk and Business Scaling

> Eliminate key man risk by breaking your activities into composite roles and accepting margin loss.

## Framework
- Every business relying on any person has key man risk; better you are, more key man you become (asset and liability)
- Scale zero: find minimum roles to replace 100% of your activities; hire accepting lower margins

## How-To / Method
- Do time study of your week, feed to GPT asking 'what are minimum roles to replace 100% of these?'
- Expect margin drop from 50% to 20% when hiring out; net result: business grows from $20M to $40M at lower margins but zero involvement

## Key Numbers
- $20M at 50% margin ($10M) with heavy involvement → $40M at 20% margin ($8M) with zero involvement shows business scaled

## Common Mistakes
- Thinking 'no one can do it as good as I can'—hire composites (rhino horn on horse body with fireflies) instead of unicorns
- Expecting two good hires on first attempt; expect 2 underperformers per 1 good hire, taking 6 months to recognize and replace
