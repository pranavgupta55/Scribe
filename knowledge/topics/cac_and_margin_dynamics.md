# CAC and Margin Dynamics

> CAC rises at scale; gross margins shrink; arbitrage edge evaporates

## Why It Works
- Increased bidding competition on paid channels raises cost per click
- Increased competition (dupes) forces price reductions to maintain volume
- Both squeeze net margin toward zero

## Connections
- Both concepts relate to margins and arbitrage. _(connects to: Margin Compression)_
- Rising CAC directly compresses margins; both forces cascade as SMB scaling occurs. _(connects to: Margins Compression)_
- CAC rises at scale, margins shrink, and arbitrage edges evaporate; fix margins before expanding or face compounding problems. _(connects to: Margin Degradation)_
