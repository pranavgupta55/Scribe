# Discipline vs. Liking What You Do

> Discipline is consistency with things you dislike; liking something requires zero discipline.

## Key Takeaway
- Fitness business owners who work out at 5 AM every day but avoid lead generation aren't disciplined—they're just doing something they enjoy
- Discipline must be trained as a skill by increasing your frustration tolerance (doing hard things without immediate reward)

## Framework
- Rule of 100: Do an action (e.g., 4 hours of outreach) for 100 straight days; most see transformation before day 100 if fully committed
- Build frustration tolerance by escalating volume: if 10 outreaches fail, do 20 tomorrow, then 30, 50, 100 to strengthen your capacity for unpleasant work
