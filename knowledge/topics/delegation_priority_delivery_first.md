# Delegation Priority (Delivery First)

> Hire for delivery/execution first, not sales; frees you to do promotion (marketing/deal flow), which drives revenue growth

## Framework
- Always delegate revenue last
- For services: hire delivery first
- For software: improve product first, then hire support
- Owner time = scarcest resource; protect it for revenue generation

## How-To / Method
- Audit time: what % is delivery vs. marketing/sales?
- Delivery likely consuming ~50% or more
- Hire person for delivery processes, free owner for promotion
