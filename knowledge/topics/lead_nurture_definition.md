# Lead Nurture Definition

> Lead nurture maximizes show rates between advertising and sales; occurs after lead expresses interest but before they buy.

## Key Takeaway
- If leads do not show, they cannot buy; show rate is one of the most important metrics in business
- 30-day show rate is the focus of the playbook; long-term nurture (emails, content, podcasts) covered separately
