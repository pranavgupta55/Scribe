# Hiring & Leverage

> At each scale level (SDR, manager, VP), you learn what excellence looks like through trial and error.

## Why It Works
- First business: you hire blindly, fail multiple times
- Second business: pattern recognition kicks in
- You can hire faster because you know what quality looks like at each role
