# Discount Plus Fee Structure

> Combine deep discount on first term with made-up one-time fee to acquire customers cheaply while monetizing and reducing churn

## Framework
- Customer pays: (discounted first term) + (setup fee) then full price recurring
- Achieves: acquisition appeal + margin protection + commitment increase

## How-To / Method
- Step 1: Decide depth of first-term discount (50%+ preferred)
- Step 2: Invent setup fee name and price
- Step 3: Clarify reason for fee to customer
- Step 4: Charge, discount, or waive per situation

## Why It Works
- Deep discount removes price objection at entry point
- Setup fee increases customer investment and sunk cost, lowering churn
- Discount surprises fewer people than 'free' (they expect to pay something)

## Examples
- Recurring: 95% off first month + $500 setup fee, then $1,000/month
- Program: 88% off first month ($120) on 12-week program ($3,000) + $1,000 setup fee
