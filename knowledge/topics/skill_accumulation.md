# Skill Accumulation

> Opportunity visibility increases with skill; at some point, so many opportunities exist that choosing becomes critical

## Framework
- Few skills = few opportunities (hard to see any)
- Many skills = many opportunities (hard to focus)
- Real opportunity isn't in chasing new things; it's in solving problems in front of you

## Claims
- Beginners see no opportunities (few skills); skilled people see so many opportunities they feel missing out; real opportunity is solving problems in front of you — [focus_is_a_daily_fight_not_a_one_time_win_-aX5ccYfVHU.txt]

## Connections
- Opportunity visibility increases with skill; find skills people already pay for rather than inventing cool ideas nobody needs. _(connects to: Skill Selection)_
- Complementary skills layer and multiply leverage; opportunity visibility increases with skills accumulated. _(connects to: Skill Stacking)_
