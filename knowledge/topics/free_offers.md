# Free Offers

> Free is the highest-volume, lowest-cost offer; generates 9x more responses than paid, works for massive companies (Facebook, YouTube, Netflix).

## Key Takeaway
- Free offers attract the most people and generate leads at the lowest cost per eyeball across all offer types
- If free doesn't work, it signals one of three problems: they don't want your thing, they don't believe you, or you're targeting wrong
- Free becomes problematic only if you can't operationally handle the volume

## How-To / Method
- Solve volume problems by adding friction: qualifications, application length, information requirements, forced consumption of sales material, ad length
- Sweet spot friction weeds out tire-kickers without losing qualified prospects who are lazy
- Test different friction levels to balance quality and volume

## Claims
- Free offers get 9x more responses than $0.01 offers per Dr. Dan Ariely's penny gap research — [100m_leads_2_bonus_chapters.txt]
- Free offers cost 5-10x less per lead than premium offers while maintaining same close rates per split tests across gyms — [100m_leads_2_bonus_chapters.txt]
