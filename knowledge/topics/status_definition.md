# Status Definition

> Status = controlling scarce resources people want; dependent on context and environment

## Key Takeaway
- Money alone gives status even if unearned
- Bartender has status in bar but not on street

## Examples
- Trust fund kid has status from money
- Bartender controls scarce resource (drinks/booze) in specific environment
