# Operational Scalability

> Start where you can handle the operational load; don't begin with mass-scale requirements you can't support.

## Key Takeaway
- Scaling Model 3 production is vastly harder than scaling Roadster production
- Better to prove the business model at high-end first, then build ops for volume

## Claims
- Starting at the high end is operationally smarter because the volume required for mass-market products (like Model 3) requires operational scale that wouldn't exist if you started with the cheap version. — [why_you_should_always_build_a_business_top_down_Gr2A7wUoEwo.txt]

## Connections
- Mass-market scaling requires capital whereas high-end is bootstrappable; start at your operational load level rather than overextending. _(connects to: Capital Requirements For Scale)_
- Start where operations can handle load; models scale via process and product not headcount. _(connects to: Operationalization)_
