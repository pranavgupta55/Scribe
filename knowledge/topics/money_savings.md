# Money Savings

> Aggressively save money in early career to buy optionality and recognize investment opportunities

## How-To / Method
- Food: buy discount grocers only, never eat out
- Clothing: reuse wardrobe for 2 years minimum; Goodwill backup only
- Housing: $300-400/month by bunking with 5 others, splitting bedroom
- Target: save as much as possible when future is uncertain

## Why It Works
- Money = time = optionality; money saved provides dry powder when fat pitch comes
- Early savings compound; early sacrifices have highest ROI

## Key Numbers
- Housing baseline: $300-400/month (6-person house, shared bedroom)
