# Warm Outreach Strategy

> Direct one-to-one contact with known contacts to convert them into engaged leads

## Key Numbers
- 100 warm outreach attempts → ~20 replies (1-in-5 engagement) → ~4 free customers (1-in-5 of replies) → ~1 paying customer (1-in-4 of free)
- 500 reach-outs/week = 5 customers/week × $400 offer = $2,000/week = $104,000/year (2x median US household income)

## How-To / Method
- Step 1: Compile contact list (phone, email, social media, past clients)
- Step 2: Pick highest-volume platform
- Step 3: Personalize with specific knowledge; use ACA framework (Acknowledge, Compliment, Ask)
- Step 4: Reach out 100/day, 3 touches per person
- Step 5: Use value equation to make offer; emphasize 'case study' style testimonials
- Step 6: Make it free initially
- Step 7-8: Stack yeses; once referrals flow, start charging with 20% increments every 5 customers
- Step 9: Keep list warm with periodic 9-word email probes ('Are you still looking to...?')

## Common Mistakes
- Not personalizing or doing minimal research on contact
- Stopping outreach too early; need 100+ days of effort minimum
- Asking for too much before building relationship
