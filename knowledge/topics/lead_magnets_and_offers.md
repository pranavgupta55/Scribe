# Lead Magnets and Offers

> Free or low-cost offers that provide value and reveal deeper problems your core offer solves

## Framework
- Three types: reveal problems, samples/trials, or one step of multi-step process
- Four delivery methods: software, information, services, physical products

## How-To / Method
- Seven steps: pick narrow problem → solve it → deliver it → test names → make easy to consume → make it excellent → make CTA clear
- Test headlines, images, subheadlines; small word changes (e.g., 'how to' vs. direct command) can 2-10x engagement

## Examples
- Dropbox free storage gave 500MB for each successful referral; PayPal $10 credit both ways
- Alex's case study video outperformed webinars by 3x because it demonstrated real results

## Claims
- A lead magnet is a valuable, narrow solution you give away free that creates problems your core offer solves; it should be so good customers feel stupid saying no — [100m_leads.txt]

## Connections
- Free or discounted offers reduce purchase friction and reveal deeper problems your premium core offer solves for qualified buyers. _(connects to: Attraction Offers)_
