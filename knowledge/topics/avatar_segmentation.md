# Avatar Segmentation

> Three-bucket avatar model: demographics, quantifiables (firm size, revenue), and behaviors

## Framework
- Demographics: what do successful customers look like?
- Quantifiables: what business size converts best?
- Behaviors: what did activated customers do vs non-activated?

## Why It Works
- Allows you to scan and exclude low-probability avatars
- Increases CAC but fixes stick because only good-fit customers enter

## Examples
- Gym Launch: excluded solo trainers with 10 clients (unprofitable to serve); only took firm owners with capacity to pay and scale
