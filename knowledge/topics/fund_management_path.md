# Fund Management Path

> Raise capital from LPs, deploy into other companies with debt leverage for maximum returns.

## Framework
- Raise $100M fund: contribute $5M (5%), raise $95M from LPs
- Buy $300M in assets using $200M debt leverage
- Returns after debt payback and LP hurdle rates accrue to GP (fund manager)

## Key Numbers
- $5M personal investment controls $300M in assets
- 20% annual return over 6 years: $300M becomes $900M
- After $200M debt + interest, $95M LP principal, $40M LP 6% hurdle: remaining splits ~50/50

## Common Mistakes
- Underestimating responsibility to multiple masters: LPs, regulators, entrepreneurs, customers
- Taking on riskier deals without proprietary edge or deal flow advantage
