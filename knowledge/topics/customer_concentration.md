# Customer Concentration

> Sticky, less price-conscious customers are higher quality

## Characteristics
- Homeowners: recurring, sticky, less price conscious
- Vacation rentals: price conscious, churn risk

## Claims
- Residential customers are sticky, less price conscious; vacation rentals are price conscious with lower LTV:CAC — [kill_the_customer_that_s_making_you_money_MKz81j4FsSM.txt]

## Connections
- DIY customers are least price-sensitive and most loyal; contractors are commoditized. _(connects to: Customer Segmentation Market Focus)_
- Concentrating sales in fewer high-end customers improves profitability versus chasing low-margin volume sales broadly. _(connects to: High End Pricing Strategy)_
- Sticky customers are higher quality and less price-conscious; rich buyers evaluate ratio not price tag. _(connects to: Value Perception And Pricing Psychology)_
