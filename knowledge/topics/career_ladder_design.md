# Career Ladder Design

> Expand 3-rung ladder to 9–18 rungs by adding Junior/Normal/Senior titles within each role

## Framework
- Original 3 rungs: Team Lead, Branch Manager, Regional Manager
- New structure: Junior, Normal (renamed), Senior, Senior+ for each role = 3 roles × 4 titles = 12 rungs minimum

## How-To / Method
- Example: Junior Pest Deliverer → (named) Pest Deliverer → Senior Pest Deliverer → Senior+ Pest Deliverer
- Repeat for next role

## Why It Works
- Keeps people 3–6 months away from next promotion always
- Prevents "I got promoted once and now I'm stuck" feeling
- Gives progression without needing to create whole new positions
