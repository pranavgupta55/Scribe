# Scaling Ad Production

> Move from monthly ad batches to weekly assembly-line production to maintain creative freshness and prevent audience saturation.

## Key Takeaway
- Designate one day per week (e.g., Friday) to full ad production
- Scale from 5 ads/month to 600 ads/month using same team
- Process compounds competitive advantage: copycats can't identify winners

## Implications
- Business won't shrink as audience scales; ad quality scales with spend
- Haven't saturated market; saturated current creative at that scale
- More ad spend profitably sustainable with better quality creative

## Claims
- Process prevents ad fatigue and confuses competition; running 600+ ads monthly makes it impossible for competitors to identify top-performing ads — [100m_playbook_goated_ads.txt]
