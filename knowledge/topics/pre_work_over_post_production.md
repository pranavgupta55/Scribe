# Pre-Work Over Post-Production

> Spend 4 weeks researching and planning, then minimal editing; don't spend 4 weeks editing.

## Method
- Front-load: research, scripting, structure design
- Back-end: minimal edits needed if planning is solid

## Key Numbers
- Pre-work saves 90-95% of post-production man-hours
- Enables 4x output: if editing took 4 weeks, now takes 1 week per 4 videos
