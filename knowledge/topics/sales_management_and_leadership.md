# Sales Management and Leadership

> Leader must set frame that discipline is non-negotiable; culture flows from top

## Framework
- 'This is how we do things. Either get in or get out. We win.'
- No one is above the process; process > player always
- Winners attract winners; losing teams repel talent

## Implications
- May need to fire half the team to rebuild culture
- Once frame is set, winners self-select in, losers self-select out
