# Skill Investment ROI

> Investing in income-earning skills yields the highest returns, often 100x+ on learning costs.

## Framework
- Skill lifts income permanently from $X to $Y annually
- Cost of skill investment amortizes across lifetime earnings increase
- Compare: $2K skill ($35K permanent annual lift) vs. $13K future value of $2K saved

## Key Numbers
- $2K one-time investment → $35K/year lift → $31M over 50 years
- $6K ads training → hundreds of millions lifetime value
- Highest ROI asset: skills that directly enable income generation

## Common Mistakes
- Avoiding skill investment due to fear of no return
- Not recognizing that one skill often locks multiple other skills
