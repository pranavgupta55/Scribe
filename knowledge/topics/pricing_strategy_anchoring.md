# Pricing Strategy & Anchoring

> Avoid the middle: sell extremely expensive to few or cheap to many; high anchor benefits entire brand perception.

## Key Takeaway
- Middle = where people die (insufficient margin, insufficient volume)
- Extremes = safe (high margin or high volume)
- High anchor transfers perceived value to lower tiers

## Examples
- Tesla: $250k Roadster → Model S → Model 3 ladder
- Charge $10k/hour; even if no takers, $100 tier feels more valuable

## How-To / Method
- List price upfront everywhere you sell
- Confront the high price directly: 'It's $10,000. Here's why.'
- Shut up and let prospect talk (talking = might say yes)
