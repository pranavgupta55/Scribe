## How-To / Method
- Order of ops: (1) Fix sales motion, (2) Change pricing, (3) Optimize operations
- Current: phone call → site visit → email quote (10% close)
- Better: phone call → site visit → collect deposit same day (target 35% close)

## Why It Works
- Email quote creates objection opportunity and price competition
- In-person closing captures energy and decision-making momentum
- 3x higher close rate at visit than email
