# Expansion Hooks for New Markets

> When capping out on profitable spend, write broader hooks that meet audiences where they are rather than constantly improving existing hooks.

## How-To / Method
- Shift from offer/proof focus to broader curiosity or pain-driven angles
- Use meme-like content to reach largest percentage of audience
- Test broader hooks on cold traffic to capture larger audience slices

## Caveats
- Broad offer-driven hooks only work if brand already has massive awareness
- Broad curiosity hooks can convert both warm fans and cold audiences simultaneously
