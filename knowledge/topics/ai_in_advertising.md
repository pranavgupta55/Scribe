# Ai In Advertising

AI in advertising is leveraged to generate targeted ads for chiropractors, with tools like the GBT model trained on ad revenue to optimize campaigns. The AI creates ad copy, headlines, and descriptions, while personalized outreach strategies, including follow-ups, drive revenue. A free trial and cloud AI tool are offered, enabling independent gym owners to generate low-cost leads (6.72 USD/month) through targeted campaigns. The AI’s outreach strategy boasts a high response rate, with five AI workers hired to handle tasks, reducing human intervention and workload. However, the speaker notes that AI cannot fully automate closing deals, requiring critical thinking, while optimizing lead generation through trial and error.  

Contradictions arise between claims about AI’s effectiveness and limitations. While the AI’s follow-up process increases conversion chances by 400% compared to waiting 42 hours, the speaker asserts that closing deals requires human critical thinking, suggesting AI cannot fully automate this. Additionally, the AI’s ability to replace entire departments with five workers is framed as a unique offer, contrasting with the general perception that AI tools are limited in scope. Sources highlight both the potential of AI to generate high-impact ads and the necessity of human oversight in closing deals, underscoring the balance between automation and strategic decision-making.
