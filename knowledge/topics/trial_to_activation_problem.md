# Trial-to-Activation Problem

> Users churn if they don't sell a fence in first 10 days; activation is the constraint, not the product

## Key Takeaway
- Once users place a fence and close a deal, they love it and rarely leave
- Lack of early activation is 100% the churn driver, not product quality

## Key Numbers
- 8% monthly churn is unsustainable
- Target: sub-2% monthly (80%+ annual)
- Activation window: first 10 days critical
