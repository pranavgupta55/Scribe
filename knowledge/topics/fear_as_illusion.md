# Fear as Illusion

> Fear creates suffering from imagined scenarios; when you prove fear wrong, you realize energy spent on it was wasted.

## Examples
- Germaphobe: hand washing, avoiding doorknobs, holding breath
- Five day camping trip with no hand washing and nothing happened
- Waited a week for maladies—none came; realized fear was illusion

## Framework
- Other people's opinions like germs—you think they kill you
- Then nothing happens and you realize they're just germs
- You didn't die and they don't even think about you
