# CAC vs. True CAC

> $130 reported CAC misleading; true CAC ~$500 after trial conversion math

## Framework
- Reported CAC: $130 per trial signup
- Facebook CPL (cost per lead): $1.50 at best (exceptional, unsustainable)
- Conversion trial → customer: ~1 in 3 or lower
- True CAC = $130 ÷ (1/3) = $390-$500 per customer
