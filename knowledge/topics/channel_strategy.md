# Channel Strategy

> Target aggregators of customers (coaches/agencies) instead of individual end-users to increase CAC payback speed

## Key Takeaway
- Coaches with followers want to know: how do I convert them to customers
- Agency model converts to stickier partner revenue

## Common Mistakes
- Going straight to fitness coaches leads to lead generation problem (how do they get customers?)
- Two-sided operation requires dedicated success managers on each side
