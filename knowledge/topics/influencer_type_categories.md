# Influencer Type Categories

> Influence correlates with positive interaction frequency, not follower count

## Framework
- A-list celebrities: lowest applause (lowest interaction)
- TikTokers/short creators: growing interaction
- YouTubers/long-formers: high interaction
- Streamers: highest interaction and influence

## Why It Matters
- Influence through frequency and depth of positive interactions
- Direct interaction (streaming) > passive consumption (celebrity)
