# Compound Work

> The gap between performers isn't marginal - it's exponential over time.

## Framework
- Gap appears 2-3x but is actually 1,000-10,000x in accumulated work
- Built over years, not weeks

## Claims
- The gap between high performers isn't 2x or 3x work - it's often 1,000x or 10,000x more work accumulated over time. — [are_you_even_trying__deYfX6flgo.txt]

## Connections
- Exponential gap between performers compounds through consistency; most underestimate reps required. _(connects to: Skill Compounding)_
- Outsized work compounds into exponential rewards; top performers put in work others avoid. _(connects to: Disproportionate Effort)_
- Gap between performers is exponential, not marginal; abilities developed on small projects compound into outsized large returns. _(connects to: Compounding Development)_
- Gap between performers is exponential; contractors on volume work multiply output for minimal cost relative to revenue. _(connects to: Work Multiplier Effect)_
