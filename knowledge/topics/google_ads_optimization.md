# Google Ads Optimization

> With proven ROAS, scale spend infinitely; optimize funnel first, then increase budget.

## Key Numbers
- Current ROAS: 13:1 at $5k/month spend
- After funnel optimization: estimated 26:1 ROAS
- Potential spend scale: $5k → $30k/month with same ROAS
- Currently upping Google ads spend from $3k to $5k/month

## Framework
- If channel returns $5 per $1, budget should scale to 'as many dollars as possible'
- Reinvest all returns back into the same channel (compounding growth)
- Fix landing page and ads before scaling spend (80/20 principle)

## Common Mistakes
- Business owners spend $1k/month on proven 10:1 channels due to psychological barriers ('I feel poorer spending $1k/day')
- Not verifying correct attribution before scaling; data must be clean
- Ignoring branded search terms, allowing competitors to bid on your name
