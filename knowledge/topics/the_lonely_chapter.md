# The Lonely Chapter

> The season where you lack both peer connection and market validation is the truest test of conviction.

## Key Takeaway
- People only root for you when you're still struggling and don't yet have what they want
- Once you've already won, support arrives but you need it least
- You must be your own first applause in the auditorium

## Implications
- Isolation from peers is a selection effect—it indicates you're following an exceptional path
- If everyone else were cheering you on, you'd be doing what everyone else does
- The harder the path, the fewer people will follow it

- The Lonely Chapter is a period where you are too different from friends but not yet successful enough to befriend people at your desired level; it's when you need support most but receive it least — [the_lonely_chapter_xyKwap1p3rU.txt]
