# Trustworthiness Maintenance

> Earn trust through reinforcement and never using information against people

## How-To / Method
- When someone discloses something vulnerable, reinforce immediately: thank them, reassure them it's safe
- Never use what they've disclosed against them in future conflicts, even if it's tempting in arguments
- If you know something sensitive, don't share it with others—loose lips sink ships and destroy your reputation as trustworthy

## Implications
- Being trustworthy gives you influence (people tell you more = more context)
- More context allows you to be more helpful to others
- People follow your advice more if they see you keeping your word
