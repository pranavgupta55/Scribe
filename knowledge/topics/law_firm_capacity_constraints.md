# Law Firm Capacity Constraints

> Law firms with A-talent focus are demand-constrained, not product-constrained. Scaling is pure hiring.

## Implications
- Niche practice (HOAs): endless market
- Endless demand: can hire 20+ more without worrying demand dries up
- Constraint: pure talent availability
- Not economics or pricing
