# Employee Training and Leverage

> Document-Demonstrate-Duplicate method for training scalable teams to perform core four activities

## Key Takeaway
- Employees let you scale past your personal capacity; time traded for management is massive ROI (40 hours work → 4 hours managing = 36 hours savings)

## Framework
- Document: write exact steps as checklist while performing; record multiple attempts
- Demonstrate: teach employee step-by-step following checklist
- Duplicate: employee performs while you observe; both iterate checklist until perfect replication

## How-To / Method
- Hire for grit over genius; teach systems; focus feedback on following directions over results
- Use 3-week trial; if below expectations after proper training, part ways
- Calculate ROI: (Total Payroll / Total Engaged Leads) × (1/Customer Conversion Rate) = CAC; must achieve 3:1 LTGP:CAC ratio

## Common Mistakes
- Comparing employees to yourself; you trained them, they're your responsibility
- Giving multiple feedback items at once; one piece at a time, practice until right, move to next
