# Niching

> Specializing in a narrow market allows 100x higher pricing for the same product while improving conversion and results.

## Strategy
- Pick one niche and commit; switching forces restart from beginning
- Narrow specificity enables 100x price increases for identical content
- Generic Time Management course: $19; B2B Outbound Sales Reps version: $1,000-2,000
- More narrow = more specific value = higher willingness to pay

## Why It Works
- Narrower avatars perceive higher relevance and transformation
- Specificity signals expertise and authority in the space
- Easier to locate and reach narrow audiences
- Allows solving niche-specific obstacles comprehensively
