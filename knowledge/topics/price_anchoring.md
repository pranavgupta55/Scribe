# Price Anchoring

> On discovery call, set a wide range to position complexity and prevent price shopping.

## Framework
- On phone: 'Could be 5K to 50K depending on scope. Like diagnosing your car over phone without seeing it—wouldn't give you a real quote.'
- Positions sales rep authority and discourages customer from shopping quotes elsewhere

## Implications
- Range anchoring prevents low-ball offers and price negotiation pressure

## Claims
- On discovery call, sales rep should give a wide price range (5K-50K) positioning custom scope complexity, not a specific quote; this establishes authority. — [helping_a_concrete_business_owner_fix_his_margins_f0AY76fE9GI.txt]
