# Trade-Off Reality

> There is no path to $100M without trading something; the question is which trade you accept

## Framework
- Regret comes from imagining upside without imagining downside
- You can't have both cookies and a six-pack; either want less or trade more

## Key Takeaway
- Dissatisfaction comes from wanting both the outcome and no cost; pick one

## Why It Works
- Forces clarity on what you actually value vs what you're romanticizing
