# High-End Pricing Strategy

> Serve the best customer at the highest price instead of trying to serve everyone cheaply.

## Framework
- No automation? Go up-market, not down-market
- High-end clients = higher margins, lower volume required, fewer operational complexities

## Claims
- If you don't have an automated way to serve the masses, go in the opposite direction: serve the best customer at the highest possible price. — [why_you_should_charge_5x_more_not_50_more_CTJRsVA89Oc.txt]
