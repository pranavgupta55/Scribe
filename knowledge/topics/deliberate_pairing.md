# Deliberate Pairing

> Pair weak brand with things customers like to create strong brand through association.

## How-To / Method
- Start with weak brand (logo with no association)
- Pair with things ideal customers like (athletes, values, experiences)
- Brand begins to mean what it's paired with to customers
- Customers buy product to associate with that meaning

## Example
- Nike + LeBron + Tiger = customers want to associate with winning/champion mentality, buy Nike to feel like winners
