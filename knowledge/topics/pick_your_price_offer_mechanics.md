# Pick-Your-Price Offer Mechanics

> Offer free base, then let customer choose payment level with tier-based bonuses at checkout.

## How-To / Method
- Market as free
- Deliver free (base version) if they want it
- At point of transaction, offer pick-your-price with three tiers (small, medium, large)
- Car wash: $0 free, $30 wax, $67 hand-buff, $99+ full interior

## Examples
- Lemonade: $0 free cup, $5 hand-squeezed + family donation, $25 photo, $99+ three months shipment
- Weight loss: $0 free 21-day program, $99 one-on-one call, $199 supplement handbook, $499 10-lb guarantee
