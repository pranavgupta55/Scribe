# Exercise Selection & Programming

> Offer two-tier workouts (hard tier 1, regressive tier 2) so newbies train alongside OGs without needing separate sessions or complex progressions.

## Framework
- Tier 1: BB back squat, BB bench press, BB row for advanced members
- Tier 2: Goblet squat, push-ups from knees, double KB row—same movement patterns, regressed load
- Both tiers in one session with partners: strong person rests/spots while weak person works
- Remove complex lifts (Olympic lifts, double unders) that need extra cueing and floor space; barely anyone needs them for body composition

## Why It Works
- Seamless integration: no separate 'newbie class' time slots
- Proven progression path: 'In 6 weeks you moved from 100% tier 2 to 60% tier 1 exercises'
- Newbies feel included, OGs feel like mentors (addresses potential tension); 25-50% of high attrition risk disappears
- Frees up floor space (no double unders) = 10-20% more capacity
