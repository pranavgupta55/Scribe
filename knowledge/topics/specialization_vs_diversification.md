# Specialization vs. Diversification

> Pick one business to go deep on instead of doing the hard thing over and over

## Key Takeaway
- Zero to $1M ten times is harder than $1M to $10M once
- Better to own one business fully than to partially own many

## Common Mistakes
- Founders are addicted to the pain of starting from zero
- They think they like starting from scratch, but actually they're control freaks
