# Intrinsic Meaning

> Meaning comes from beyond self; bigger why enables tolerance of pain and uncertainty.

## Framework
- Viktor Frankl: man with big enough why overcomes almost any how
- Self-serving goals too easily satisfied; meaning requires external purpose
- Your why must be bigger than you or you fail on obstacles bigger than you

## Examples
- Research: pain threshold tripled when told loved ones in next room would avoid shocks
- My passion: helping men provide; bigger than just self

## Key Takeaway
- Took year off with $46M exit and was miserable; had no quest
- Found meaning in helping younger version of self through writing/teaching
