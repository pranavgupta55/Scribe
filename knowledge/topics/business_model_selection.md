# Business Model Selection

> Choose between scalable (SaaS, software) and service models; each has different financial rules.

## Service Business (80% of market)
- Unscalable: limited by human capacity
- Only direction: raise prices (virtuous cycle) or lower prices (vicious cycle)
- No benefit to aggressive CAC: can't acquire customers faster than you can serve

## SaaS / Unlimited Scale (5% of market)
- Scalable: software/automation handles unlimited delivery
- Pricing is critical: balance gross margin vs growth rate
- Lower price = higher growth, lower margin; raise price = lower growth, higher margin

## Key Insight
- Service businesses default to raising prices; SaaS balances tradeoff
- Only exception to 'raise price always' is SaaS model
