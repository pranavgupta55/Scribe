# Effort & Sacrifice

> Tangible costs (time, money) and intangible costs (fear, embarrassment, hunger) required to achieve outcome.

## Examples
- Fitness: wake 2hrs early, hunger, soreness, fear, meal prep, new clothes
- Liposuction: sleep 5-10hrs, soreness 2-4 weeks, nausea (mostly eliminated)
- Pills vs gym: taking a pill far easier than daily exercise commitment
- Done-for-you services always more expensive than DIY due to zero effort

## How-To / Method
- Identify every effort/sacrifice required in current solution
- Engineer solutions to eliminate or minimize these barriers
- Communicate decreases in effort/sacrifice in marketing
- Use systems and tools to make execution effortless

- Effort & sacrifice includes tangible costs (time, money) and intangible costs (fear, embarrassment); liposuction commands 25x price over fitness due to lower effort. — [100m_offers.txt]
