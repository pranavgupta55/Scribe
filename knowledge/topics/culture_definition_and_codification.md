# Culture Definition and Codification

> Culture is the rules (if-then) that govern reinforcement; codify by chunking 100+ rules into 3 values.

## Framework
- Rule: conditional if-then statement ('If you do X, then you get rewarded/punished')
- Culture: system of rules governing reinforcement in an organization
- Chunk 100+ rules into 3 values that unpack to the rules

## Example
- At Acquisition.com: if not talking in meeting, you're muted; not paying attention = grounds for termination

## How-To / Method
- When expanding to new location, move your biggest culture champion there but ensure culture can self-reinforce without them
- Codify by observing conditions where behavior exists (what rules govern meetings? interactions? feedback?)
