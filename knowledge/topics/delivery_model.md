# Delivery Model

> Ultra-cheap model requires automation; high-touch model requires sophisticated operator clients who understand metrics.

## Framework
- Ultra-cheap: one-time setup (maps ranking, review management, local SEO) + minimal recurring work
- High-touch: strategy consulting for operators who understand business metrics and ROI

## Common Mistakes
- Trying to serve SMBs at mid-market pricing with high-touch delivery
- Expecting to fix SMB operator problems through marketing
- Not automating ultra-cheap offering sufficiently

## Claims
- Only two models work: ultra-cheap ($300-400/month) with almost zero delivery cost, OR high-touch expensive services for sophisticated business operators — [smb_clients_will_quietly_bankrupt_you_3Lvhd3LIwwY.txt]

## Connections
- Ultra-cheap models require automation; high-touch models require sophisticated clients who understand metrics; most services can't achieve low-price mass scale. _(connects to: Service Vs Automation)_
