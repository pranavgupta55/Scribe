# One-Time Setup Fees

> Made-up one-time fees increase customer commitment and dramatically reduce churn, especially for services requiring customer effort

## Key Numbers
- $100 setup + $10/month: near-zero churn (tanning business)
- $19 setup + $19/month: high churn (same industry)
- $5,000 setup + $267/month: 2+ year lifespan vs 3-4 month industry norm (weight loss coaching)

## Why It Works
- High barrier to entry = high barrier to exit (big head-long tail principle)
- Sunk cost effect: customer who paid $5k upfront won't quit at month 3
- Commitment effect: customers who invest commit to following through

## When to Use
- Services requiring customer to do their part (physical therapy, coaching, habit change)
- Any recurring service where commitment improves outcomes

## Implementation
- If customer leaves and returns, charge setup fee again
- Be clear about reason for fee (onboarding, setup, implementation, etc.)
- Can waive fee for all customers if invented as a discount mechanism
