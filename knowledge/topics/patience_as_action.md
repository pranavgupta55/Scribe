# Patience as Action

> Patience = figuring out something to do in the meantime; without defining, it's a meaningless abstraction to a child.

## How-To / Method
- Instead of 'be patient,' say 'here's what to do while you wait'
- Operationalize the behavior so it's actionable
- Example: all waiting for S&P 500 to grow = we're doing 'patience' (holding)
