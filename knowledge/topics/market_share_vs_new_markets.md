# Market Share vs New Markets

> Growing pie of existing market beats entering new markets

## Why It Works
- Oklahoma expansion: same whale present, but represents <15% potential upside, ties up founder brain
- Texas growth: 50% -> 70-80% market share, same geography, same people, compounding advantage
- Business value multiplies faster with monopolistic position than with geographic diversification

## Caveats
- Only 3-4 states total would work for this remedy (wage protection laws)
- None generate revenue close to Texas
