# Personal Standard Setting

> You are the standard setter for your company and life; you decide what's tolerable and acceptable.

## Framework
- You get what you tolerate and accept as good enough
- Standards apply to sales rates, profit margins, cash flow, fitness, speed, relationships
- Every domain has a standard you set implicitly or explicitly

## Why It Works
- High standards compound daily
- You then get upset about the fruit of your own low standards
- You decide if something is minimum viable or world-class
