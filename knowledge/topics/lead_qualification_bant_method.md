# Lead Qualification & BANT Method

> SMS-based BANT qualification after VSL opt-in; ensures only qualified buyers enter sales call with clear expectations

## Framework
- Budget: "What's your budget for this project?"
- Authority: "Does your spouse/partner need to be on the call?"
- Need: Assumed (already watching railing videos = high intent)
- Timing: "When do you need this installed? How fast?"

## Key Takeaway
- Filters out low-intent leads before expensive phone call
- Sets expectations for decision timeline and authority structure
