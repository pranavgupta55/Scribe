# Acquisition vs Organic Growth

> Acquisitions need deal flow; organic growth needs better founders

## Key Takeaway
- Deal flow for acquisitions is straightforward: outbound, trade shows, conferences
- Organic growth of acquired companies is bottlenecked by founder quality

## Framework
- Can't do both equally - highest leverage is generating demand for acquisitions
- Delegate portfolio management to team, focus on next acquisitions

## Claims
- Expanding too fast with multiple underperforming locations is harder to undo than taking extra year to nail it - expansion mistakes compound — [he_buys_businesses_but_can_t_make_them_grow_6WPNzaWuAic.txt]
