# Risk Perception and Overestimation

> People know the cost is guaranteed but don't know the gain; this uncertainty makes them unwilling to take bets.

## Key Takeaway
- Cost is fixed and known
- Upside is uncertain and unknown
- This imbalance makes trades feel scary

## Framework
- You don't know how much you'll have to pay
- You don't know how long you'll have to pay it for
- But it's faster than you think and less scary than you fear
