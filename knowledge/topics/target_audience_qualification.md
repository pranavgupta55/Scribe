# Target Audience Qualification

> Close rates spike when you only pitch to people with actual buying power; segment your messaging by audience.

## Method
- If selling $20K high-ticket offer, only talk to million-dollar+ business owners
- If selling $500/month SaaS, can talk to anyone with a business
- Qualify upfront via marketing: say 'we only cater to [specific profile]'

## Why It Works
- Talking to right audience = higher close rate
- Higher close rate = not leaving pricing power on the table
- Qualification does half the selling work
