# Lead Scoring

> Prioritize calls to leads meeting both qualification criteria: $5k+ annual vacation spend AND $5k+ monthly credit card spend.

## Framework
- Two-factor scoring: vacation spend + credit card spend
- 2-for-2: call first (highest probability)
- 1-for-2: call second (still qualified)
- 0-for-2: low priority (still worth eventual outreach)

## Why It Works
- Filters out leads without spending behavior to learn strategies
- Improves sales team talk time by focusing on highest-intent leads
- Qualification happens before dialer, not after
