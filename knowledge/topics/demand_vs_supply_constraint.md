# Demand vs Supply Constraint

> Demand-constrained business must lower CAC via creative and conversion; supply constraint would be lack of capacity.

## Framework
- Decision tree: demand-constrained → new channel, lower CAC, or spend more
- Business can handle more customers; constraint is acquisition cost
- Hitting ceiling at $100k/month spend despite good offer signals creative, not spending power

## Key Takeaway
- Three levers unlock growth: better creative, conversion optimization, better offer
- With proper creative, can scale to $100k+ per day (not just month)
