# Risk Assessment

> Replace fear with math: evaluate debt as a volatility/coverage problem, not an emotional one.

## Framework
- Debt increases business risk by amplifying volatility effects
- If you miss covenants, lender takes control
- Key metric: cash flow coverage relative to payment obligations

## Method
- Map seasonal volatility
- Calculate coverage ratio: cash flow / debt payments
- Ask: what coverage ratio keeps me safe?

## Key Takeaway
- Good lenders already do this diligence; if they approve, they modeled the risk
- 99% cash / 1% debt: risk aversion is purely personal; one person's too risky is another's comfort
