# Value Creation Through Pre-Work

> The fortune is in doing the complex work for them and delivering simple output.

## How-To / Method
- Identify the work customers don't want to do (ad testing, property research, trend analysis)
- Do that work at scale in the background
- Hand them the winning result to deploy in their local context

## Key Takeaway
- Don't give them a cookbook; pre-chew the food and spit it into their mouths
- They pay for net gain of time they get from not doing the work themselves
