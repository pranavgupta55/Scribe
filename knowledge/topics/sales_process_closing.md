# Sales Process & Closing

> Use C-L-O-S-E-R framework to systematically move prospects through belief shifts and close 85%+ of cold appointments.

## Framework
- Clarify: Ask 'What brought you in? What's your goal? Why did you sign up?' to establish the gap they want filled
- Label: Recap their current vs. desired state with specific numbers ('180 lbs at 28% body fat, need to lose 40 lbs')
- Overview: Review past attempts (big-box gym, Weight Watchers, shakes); show why each failed using a chart of fitness/nutrition/accountability gaps
- Sell vacation: Don't talk programming details; sell the end state ('You'll fit your old clothes, feel energy, keep your kids up late playing')
- Explain: Answer objections stalls ('need to think about it'), decision-makers ('need to ask spouse'), and price ('can't afford it')
- Reinforce: Send handwritten invite to next event, owner call welcoming them, free t-shirt same day to seal decision

## Common Closes
- Rocking Chair Close: 'When you leave, you won't sit and think—you'll get busy. In 4 days you'll forget why you came. Make the decision now.'
- Best Case/Worst Case: 'Best: lose weight and get refund as credit. Worst: we fail and I personally refund you. So what's the real risk?'
- Expensive Comparison: 'You spent $1,000 on heels worn 8 times (4 hours per time = $33/hour). You'll wear your body 24/7—isn't it worth more?'
- Identity Swap: 'You're shifting from unhealthy to healthy. Healthy people budget for fitness. New identity = new priorities.'
