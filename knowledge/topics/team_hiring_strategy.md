# Team Hiring Strategy

> Skip juniors; hire one top performer you're compelled to recruit, giving them your lead flow, brand, and pre-built systems

## Common Mistakes
- Hiring junior agents: 90% burn out within year due to time burden on you
- Betting on volume: multiple mediocre hires drain focus and training time
- Not selling them on the opportunity: if you don't believe it's good, they won't either

## Framework
- Hire one stud only
- Offer: learn from you, get lead flow, inherit brand, inherit systems
- Poach from competitors if they have independent success already proven
