# Organizational Structure & Hiring

> Hire lean and add roles only when workload demands it, scaling from owner-operator (0-100 EFTs) to multi-role team (400+ EFTs).

## Growth Stages
- 0-100 EFTs: Owner, 1 trainer; owner does all sales, operations, coaching
- 100-175: Add operations manager (20 hrs/week @ $1.3k/mo); owner still sells
- 175-250: Add challenge manager (20 hrs/week); operations expands to 40 hrs
- 250-325: Add retention manager (20 hrs/week); ops stays 40 hrs
- 400+: Add sales manager (only at scale and after 6 months of $20k/mo profit); all roles now specialized

## Key Takeaway
- Hire before you need the position so you have time to find the right person and train them
- Don't open second location until: 6 months of $20k/mo profit, staff running original gym without owner, $200k+ in bank
