# Content Unit Structure

> Smallest atomic unit of engaging content containing three elements that work for any platform or medium

## Framework
- Hook: get attention (5 seconds max)
- Retain: keep watching (lists, steps, stories with unresolved questions)
- Reward: deliver promised value

## How-To / Method
- Link multiple units together for longer content; no material length difference, just more repetitions
- Short form easier to start; master before attempting long form

## Claims
- Content units have three components: Hook (grab attention), Retain (keep watching), Reward (satisfy the promise); lists, steps, and stories are primary retention methods — [100m_leads.txt]
