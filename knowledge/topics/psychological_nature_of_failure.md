# Psychological Nature of Failure

> Failure is psychological unless you die; everything else is solvable through iteration.

## Framework
- Did you die? No. Did you figure it out? No. Guess what? You're not dead. Try again.
- Fear of failure is the worst case; people spend energy on what-ifs instead of action
