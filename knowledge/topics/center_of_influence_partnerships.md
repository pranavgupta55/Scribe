# Center of Influence Partnerships

> Reposition customers as the CPAs and financial advisors, not the companies themselves

## Framework
- Think of CPAs and financial groups as customers, not just referral partners
- CPAs want to increase profitability for their clients; position offer as their new service

## Implications
- Measure success by acquisition cost per center of influence
- Market messaging targets what CPAs care about: servicing their clients better

## Connections
- Real customers are CPAs and financial advisors, not companies; they refer to companies. _(connects to: Centers Of Influence)_
