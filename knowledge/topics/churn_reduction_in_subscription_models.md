# Churn Reduction in Subscription Models

> Separate one-time skill training (high value, one-time cost) from ongoing consumables (low value, ongoing fee); high churn indicates misaligned value delivery

## Framework
- One-time value: skill training, frameworks, process (high price, lasting benefit)
- Ongoing consumables: lead gen, content, support (low price, recurring use)

## Key Takeaway
- Real estate coaching churn solved by getting advisors activated (engaging with training)
- Activation and skill development drive retention, not price

## How-To / Method
- Price skill training at true value (premium)
- Price ongoing services at cost to maintain access
- Focus on activation and initial value delivery
