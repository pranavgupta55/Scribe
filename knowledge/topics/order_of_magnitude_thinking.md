# Order of Magnitude Thinking

> Scale effort in 10x/100x jumps, not doubling

## Framework
- Don't go from 5 min/week workout to 10 min
- Go from 5 min to 50 min (10x) or 500 min (100x)

## Why It Works
- Business has no context for effort sufficiency
- Orders of magnitude create actual change
