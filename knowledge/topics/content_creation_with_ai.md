- Record all calls and transcribe them
- Ask bot to extract top five most interesting situations/decisions from past week
- Creates true stories and narratives without fabrication

## Why It Works
- Proof and authenticity matter for B2B credibility
- AI can identify patterns in what's actually happened in your business
