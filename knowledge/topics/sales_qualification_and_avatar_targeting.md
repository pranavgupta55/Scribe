# Sales Qualification and Avatar Targeting

> Wrong avatar kills high-ticket sales; if only 1 in 100 prospects is wealthy, pitching 100 broke people guarantees zero conversions and wrong conclusion.

## Framework
- Assume top 1% are the only ones who will buy premium offers; if speaking to wrong avatar, you'll get zero yeses
- Wealthy people self-select out of broke person friend circles; can't test high-ticket offers on poor people

## Common Mistakes
- Pitching $10K offer to bottom 50% of wealth distribution: 0 conversions; mistakenly conclude high-ticket doesn't work
- Reality: it does work, but only with correct avatar (7-figure+ earners)
- Most salespeople don't qualify prospects; they pitch to everyone and wonder why rich people don't say yes
