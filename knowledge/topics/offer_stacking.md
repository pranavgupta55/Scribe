# Offer Stacking

> Wrap the continuity solution inside the defined-term product people actually want; don't sell continuity directly.

## Framework
- Entry offer: 3-week summer program (what they want)
- During program: educate on long-term results needed
- Exit offer: 1-year bundle continuation (what they need)

## How-To
- Run 3-week program as before; stop promoting ongoing tutoring
- Bundle 3-week + 1-year in same offer
- Guarantee: 4+ points on score or free tutoring until achieved

## Key Takeaway
- Defined-end offer is psychological entry point; continuity is psychological exit
- People feel committed during program; upsell happens when momentum is high
