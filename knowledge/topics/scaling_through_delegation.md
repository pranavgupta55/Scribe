# Scaling Through Delegation

> Systematically trade smaller time commitments up the ladder until you manage only managers

## Framework
- Trade 40 sales hours for 2 management hours per person
- Keep making that same trade: 40 management hours for 2 hours managing a manager
- Repeat until you're managing managers who run teams, not doing the work yourself
