# Custom Order Strategy & Positioning

> Custom option activates higher-intent customers and positions brand as premium; rolling out across all products tripled custom order submissions

## Key Takeaway
- Custom orders had high intent clicks on best sellers
- Expanding to all products increased leads 3x with same ad spend

## Implementation
- Add custom order button/form to all product pages
- Sticky banner across top: "Custom designs available"
- Custom flow leads to form → calendar → VSL → SMS nurture → call

## Implications
- Differentiation from competitors who don't offer custom options
- Higher perceived value and lower price sensitivity

## Claims
- Custom orders on best sellers got high intent clicks; putting custom option on all products increased qualified lead volume to 3x baseline — [building_a_1_000_000_business_for_a_stranger_in_26_minutes_HGZOxBfnF-E.txt]
