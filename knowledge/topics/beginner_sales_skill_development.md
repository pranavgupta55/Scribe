# Beginner Sales Skill Development

> Cold email teaches universal sales skills (volume, qualification, selling to skeptics) that apply to all channels; ads teach platform-specific tricks

## Why It Works
- Cold email skills: volume, persistence, cold offer, handling objections → transfer to ads, social, referrals
- Ad skills: pixel optimization, audience segmentation → don't transfer to cold email

## Claims
- Cold email teaches volume, cold selling, and cold prospect handling—these skills apply everywhere; ads require platform expertise that doesn't transfer — [why_i_tell_beginners_to_cold_email_before_running_ads_MEuEEoC-9s0.txt]
- Warm leads generated from content: close easier but only if you apply cold process to them; mistake is treating warm like warm and leaving process open — [why_i_tell_beginners_to_cold_email_before_running_ads_MEuEEoC-9s0.txt]
- Sell warm like cold, and you maximize every conversion; warm audience won't mind full presentation if you show up credible—they just want certainty before buying — [why_i_tell_beginners_to_cold_email_before_running_ads_MEuEEoC-9s0.txt]
