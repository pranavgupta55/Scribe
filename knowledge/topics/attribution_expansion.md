# Attribution Expansion

> Performance marketing window: 30–60 days; brand marketing window: 6–12 months; must expand attribution to see real returns

## Framework
- Performance: "What's my ROAS in 30 days?"
- Brand: "What's my customer lifetime value in 6–12 months?"

## Key Takeaway
- Founder thinks quant (spreadsheets); need to think psychology + long-term
- Brand compounds; direct response doesn't
