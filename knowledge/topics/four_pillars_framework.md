# Four Pillars Framework

> Four data-driven pillars increase show rates: Availability, Speed, Personalization, Volume; based on analysis of 4,000+ daily appointments and 20,000+ leads/day across portfolio.

## Framework
- Availability: number of open appointment slots and how far out leads can schedule
- Speed: how fast you respond to leads and how quickly appointments are booked
- Personalization: making communication useful and relevant to individual leads
- Volume: number of times you reach out before giving up on a lead

## Claims
- Show rates increased from 50% to 90%+ by implementing four pillars; a 20-40% increase in show rates translates to 2-3x profit improvement in year one with 20% margins — [100m_playbook_lead_nurture.txt]
