# Gross Margins & Economics

> High margins enable reinvestment, faster cash conversion, and higher EBITDA; a 50% margin business at $20M revenue outperforms a 10% margin business at $100M

## Key Numbers
- High-margin businesses: media, information, education, software (50%+ typical)
- Low-margin: grocery, farming, restaurants (10-20%)
- 5x incremental EBITDA per dollar earned with 50% vs 10% margins

## Examples
- High: podcast ads, pharmaceuticals (pennies to manufacture, dollars to sell)
- Low: commodity food products, general retail

## Implications
- Decommoditize products to increase margins
- Use margins to fund growth and employee retention
- Faster cash conversion cycle improves business viability
