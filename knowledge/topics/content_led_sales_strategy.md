# Content-Led Sales Strategy

> Content and ads are now one system; make winners in content, then pay to distribute them

## How-To / Method
- Create lots of content on all platforms
- Identify which content performs best
- Take the winner, add a CTA (call-to-action), and run it as a paid ad
