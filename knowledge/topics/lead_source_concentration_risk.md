# Lead Source Concentration Risk

> Dependency on 4 CPAs for 60-70% of revenue is a risk; scaling to 40 CPAs mitigates that risk.

## Key Takeaway
- Four CPAs = $500-750K per CPA per year = lot of leverage they have over you
- If one CPA stops sending deals, business drops meaningfully
- Current third-party partnerships creating bend-over-backwards pressure

## Implications
- Diversify lead sources to 40; then no single source can dictate terms
- Current third parties get business because you need them; scale sources, you choose partnerships
