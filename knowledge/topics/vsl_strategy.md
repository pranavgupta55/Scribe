# VSL Strategy

> Video sales letter as qualification and pre-framing layer before phone call

## Framework
- Sits between ad/organic traffic and sales call
- Qualifies interested prospects
- Builds trust and sets expectations

## Why It Works
- Organic traffic doesn't understand offer; VSL pre-frames
- Filters to hot leads who watch full video
- Sales call becomes closing, not teaching
