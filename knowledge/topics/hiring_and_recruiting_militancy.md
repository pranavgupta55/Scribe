# Hiring and Recruiting Militancy

> Recruiting is a discipline requiring multiple interview rounds, contractor vetting, and systematic training.

## Key Takeaway
- Many interview rounds required for both warehouse and sales roles
- Hiring house cleaner as first employee + founder recruiting principles = great team
- Buy salespeople (Amazon affiliates) vs build (interview many candidates)

## How-To / Method
- For sales: recruit from Amazon affiliate sellers (already do live sales, understand affiliate %)
- Use contract to lock in salespeople for X period
- For warehouse: commoditized hiring (pulse, leader, decent work = enough)
- For sales training: founder must create playbook, teach via behaviors, not hope

## Key Numbers
- House cleaner → first employee → trained to sell → 110k followers on Whatnot → $6M/year business
- 3 salespeople to 5-7 salespeople = $6M → $38M (5 hours/day to 24/7)

## Finance Requirement
- Physical products business needs finance person for forecasting as it scales
- Cashflow-based accounting (reinvest all profit) works until it hits cash crunch
- Finance person models growth rate, cash requirements, when to buy inventory

## Claims
- For a live-selling business, buying salespeople (recruiting from Amazon affiliates who already do live streams) is lower-risk than building (hiring toads and training soft skills); buying = proven model, building = longer, more hiring attempts. — [helping_e_commerce_business_owners_scale_8C_6qojTA78.txt]
- Recruiting is an operational discipline requiring militant processes: multiple interview rounds for both warehouse and sales roles; hiring house cleaner as first employee and applying founder/influencer recruiting principles leads to a great team. — [helping_e_commerce_business_owners_scale_8C_6qojTA78.txt]
- For physical product businesses, cash-based accounting (reinvesting all gross profit into more inventory) works until it doesn't; as you scale, you need a finance person to model growth rates and cash requirements to avoid cash crunches. — [helping_e_commerce_business_owners_scale_8C_6qojTA78.txt]

## Connections
- Systematic recruiting at small scale enables volume hiring at larger scale; discipline creates capacity. _(connects to: Volume Based Hiring)_
- Recruiting discipline with vetting and training justifies 20% commission on high-salary hires ($500K+) over low-value contractors. _(connects to: Recruiting Economics)_
