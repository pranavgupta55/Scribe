# Excuse & Responsibility

> Excuse = statement to avoid punishment; identify exact behavior vs. labeling someone as 'irresponsible'.

## Examples
- Don't say 'you're lazy'
- Say 'you're slow to respond' and 'you come unprepared to meetings'
- Observable behaviors, not character judgments
