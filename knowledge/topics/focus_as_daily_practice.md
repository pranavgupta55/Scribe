# Focus as Daily Practice

> Focus is like beating addiction: not a one-time victory, but daily vigilance

## Key Takeaway
- Distraction changes form daily; must be adaptable in fighting it
- Can't say "I was focused this week" and rest; must fight again next day

## Why It Works
- Acknowledges focus as ongoing practice, not character trait
