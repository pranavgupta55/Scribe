# Track Record Verification

> Two-question framework for deciding whether to trust

## How-To / Method
- Question 1: Do they have a track record of protecting what they've been given? Have they had damaging information before and not used it?
- Question 2: Does betraying you cost them more than protecting you? Would burning you be economically worse for them than staying loyal?

## Key Takeaway
- If both answers are yes, proceed. If either is no, don't make yourself punishable
