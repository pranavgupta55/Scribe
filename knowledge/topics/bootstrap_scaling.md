# Bootstrap Scaling

> Cap time at 5-10% doing high-ticket one-on-one at 10X rate; 100% margin funds aggressive 90% growth spending.

## Framework
- Dedicate small time slice to premium (5-10% of week)
- Price at 10X or more your standard rate
- All profit goes toward aggressive growth
- Remaining 90% of time builds scalable business

## Key Takeaway
- Bootstrap without debt or venture funding
- Premium tier is a strategic cash generator, not main business
