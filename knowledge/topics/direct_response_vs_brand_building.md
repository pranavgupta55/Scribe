# Direct Response vs Brand Building

> Pure media arbitrage caps at $10-12M; only brands survive dupe competition and scale to $100M+.

## Key Takeaway
- Media arbitrage: revenue up, CAC up, margins down → unsustainable at $10-12M
- Dupes flood successful products and undercut prices once they see traction
- Brand defensibility: either patent (expensive, small scale) or brand (people buy from you despite cheaper dupes)

## Framework
- Direct response arbitrage = make promise → run ads → capture sales
- Brand = make promise + associate values + deliver product that reinforces promise → virtuous cycle
- Product reinforces or destroys brand; brand loop compounds or collapses based on product delivery

## Why It Works
- Private equity buys brands, not products; a dupe-proof brand is worth $500M, an arbitrage business is worth $50M
- Brand allows 10x+ premium over knockoffs when done right
- Defensibility without legal team = brand + community trust
