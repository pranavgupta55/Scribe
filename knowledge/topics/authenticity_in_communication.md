# Authenticity in Communication

> People want to hear your true thoughts, not what they think they want; honesty strengthens relationships.

## Key Takeaway
- When someone asks your opinion, tell them what you actually think, not what you think they want to hear
- Honest disagreement strengthens relationships more than false agreement

## Example
- Turning down book endorsement with honest explanation = future trust in your yes
