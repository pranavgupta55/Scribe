# Ambition-Based Founder Screening

> Founders who scaled to $20M are likelyetter than those who sell at $3M

## Framework
- Entrepreneur who built to $20M ARR demonstrates higher ambition and capability
- Entrepreneur who caps at $3M and sells shows different ceiling
- For buy-and-hold, bigger acquisition targets with proven founders is lower-risk

## Claims
- Best founders getting to $20M ARR are likely better entrepreneurs than those who cap at $3M and sell - shows ambition and capability gap — [he_buys_businesses_but_can_t_make_them_grow_6WPNzaWuAic.txt]

## Connections
- Founders who scale to $20M likely outperform those exiting at $3M; founder dependency blocks 80-agent scale. _(connects to: Founder Sales Dependency)_
- Founders who scale to $20M less likely trapped in expertise-based sales; higher ambition drives delegation. _(connects to: Founder Driven Sales)_
- $20M-scaled founders outperform $3M exits; highest ROI comes from buying back founder time. _(connects to: Founder Optimization)_
- Founders scaling to $20M likely better than those selling at $3M; VC scaling lacks profitability vs profitable competitors. _(connects to: Venture Backed Scaling Illusion)_
