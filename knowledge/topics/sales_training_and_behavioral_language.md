# Sales Training and Behavioral Language

> Teach salespeople via concrete behaviors (raise voice, talk faster), not amorphous qualities (charisma).

## Key Takeaway
- Amorphous language ("have more charisma") = no one knows what to do
- Telephone game of interpretation kills training speed and consistency
- Concrete behaviors = replicable, teachable, measurable

## How-To / Method
- Instead of "have high energy," say: raise your voice, talk faster, shoulders back
- Instead of "be confident," say: maintain eye contact 5 seconds, smile, no filler words
- Film yourself, mark the exact moments, teach others to replicate
- Compress training time 2-3x by using observable behaviors

## Examples
- Livestream sales person: raise voice on calls, lean forward toward camera, faster pace, higher energy tone, point at product while talking
- Founder's trainee copied founder from yesterday; instead of managing, put her on camera to sell where her copy-paste behavior is asset

## Why It Works
- People can't interpret "charisma"; they can replicate "shoulders back"
- Observable behaviors create standard training curriculum
- Once you break down your elite salesperson's behavior, scale replicates it
