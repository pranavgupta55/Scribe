# Soft Handoff

> Transition clients without abandoning relationships through shared ownership model

## Framework
- New practitioner handles clinical components
- Founder retains relationship ownership
- Founder touches base 5x less frequently (message vs calls)
- Founder still visible/involved but lower touch

## Why It Works
- Clients still feel founder proximity
- Reduces risk of new practitioner leaving with whole book
- Allows gradual client confidence transfer
