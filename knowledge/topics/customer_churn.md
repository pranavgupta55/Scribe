# Customer Churn

> At $2K/month, SMB stick rate is 4-6 months average; at $400/month, stick rate is 30-40 months.

## Key Numbers
- Current: 10/month × $2K = $20K MRR → 5-month average life → $100K per cohort
- By month 10-12, churn starts exceeding new sales growth

## Path Forward
- Drop price to $300-400 and automate delivery, OR
- Raise price to $2K+ and serve only sophisticated operators with real metrics
