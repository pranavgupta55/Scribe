# Founder Replacement Economics

> Founder who reached their personal financial goal is unlikely to grow 10x more

## Key Takeaway
- Out of 24 acquisitions, kept 5 winners, gave back 19
- Winners don't plateau - they either die or they win

## Why It Works
- Founder satisfied with $20-30M life-changing money won't risk it to go for $50M+
- They're protecting families and relationships, not building empires
- Fundamentally different mindset than 3x10 ambitious founder
