# Direct Response Doom Loop

> Revenue growth + margin compression + supply chain burden = large, unprofitable operation forced to keep scaling

## Key Numbers
- $12M revenue with 7% margins = unprofitable on per-unit basis despite looking successful
- Doom loop: must keep selling to maintain payroll despite low margins

## Why It Works
- CAC rises exponentially with scale in saturated channels
- Dupes enter market and undercut on price when product becomes visible
- Supply chain liabilities (inventory, fulfillment) eat into margins

## Implications
- Must transition from media arbitrage to brand before hitting this trap
- Escaping doom loop requires either brand build or product elimination
