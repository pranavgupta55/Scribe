# Three Content Buckets

> Content falls into entertainment (watch), education (change behavior), or edutainment (both).

## Framework
- Entertainment: goal is consumption; success = views
- Education: goal is behavior change; success = application of learning
- Edutainment: attempts both; results in neither excelling

## Examples
- MrBeast videos: mostly entertainment (viewers know it's entertainment)
- Physics textbook videos: pure education (viewers know expectations)

## Claims
- Entertainment succeeds by getting people to watch; education succeeds by changing what people do; edutainment attempts both. — [the_3_types_of_content_and_why_only_one_builds_a_business_NTm8BibjUqQ.txt]
