# Promotion Velocity

> First promotion should be easy (6-inch putt); subsequent ones progressively harder, spaced 3–6 months apart

## Key Numbers
- First milestone: 25 deliveries/stars (gets to Junior+)
- Next: 100 deliveries
- Next: 200 deliveries
- Each escalates, but 3–6 month spacing keeps momentum

## Why It Works
- First win happens in first 30–45 days (critical churn window)
- Person thinks "I like my job" and stays
- Expands reinforcement cycle so always something to look forward to

## Key Takeaway
- More ways to win = better retention
