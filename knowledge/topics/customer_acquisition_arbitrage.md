# Customer Acquisition Arbitrage

> Negotiate vendor discount, market/sell at market rate, keep the spread as pure profit.

## How-To / Method
- Ask: 'If I did 100 deals, what's the wholesale price?'
- Less sophisticated owner says $50 wholesale for $100 retail
- You sell at $1,000, keep $950 margin
- If you have $5K upfront, put it all into ads rather than asking owner to spend
