# Raising Capital Path

> Take investor money to fund aggressive growth in high-risk, high-return opportunities.

## Key Numbers
- Tech platforms, social networks, marketplaces require years of losses
- Amazon lost money for 10+ years before profitability
- Far fewer venture-backed competitors than bootstrapped competitors

## Implications
- Must serve two customers: end user and VCs
- Equity dilution and potential loss of control at 3+ funding rounds
- High failure rate but rare upside potential
