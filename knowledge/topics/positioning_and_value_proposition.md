# Positioning and Value Proposition

> Don't position as cost-cutter; position as profitability multiplier

## Framework
- 'Better than what you have' + 'Costs less' = customers want both
- Avoid purely price-based messaging
- Lead with outcomes, not features

- Healthcare VA company serves OT/PT/SLP practice owners with 5+ locations who buy repeatedly (2, 5, then 20 VAs) — [the_10x_move_for_healthcare_service_businesses_Bt6lTDsX12s.txt]
