# Seasonal Marketing Angles

> Two major seasonal cycles per year enable year-round campaigns; align all channels on single theme per 2-month block.

## Key Numbers
- Corey angles: spring pollen allergies (April peak), fall/winter mildew smell (heating season)
- Messaging can run 2-month seasonal campaigns × 2 cycles/year = year-round engagement

## How-To / Method
- Pre-season (Jan): 'Get ready for allergy season'
- During-season (Apr): 'Allergy relief is here'
- Post-season (Jun): 'Did you miss allergy relief? It's still available'
- Repeat pre/during/post on second cycle (mildew/heating season)

## Examples
- Gym industry: New Year's resolutions (pre/during/post Jan) + summer body (pre/during/post May)
- HVAC: allergy angles (pollen) + mildew angles (heating season); total 4 distinct messaging blocks
