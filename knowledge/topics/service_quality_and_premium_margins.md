# Service Quality and Premium Margins

> Rich customers want fast, easy, guaranteed; pre-work and ease-of-delivery command 10X cost premium; service business margins come from removing friction for premium buyers.

## Framework
- Rich customers prioritize: (1) Speed, (2) Ease, (3) Guarantee
- Service business margin equation: do $X work for rich customer, charge 10X its cost; they pay because it saves them time/complexity

## How-To / Method
- Pre-do research and decision-making for premium tier (pre-choose options, scout location, drive to door)
- Cost of pre-work is ~5-10% of price increase; customer pays 100% for it because it saves their time

## Why It Works
- Rich person earning $100K salary sees $100 as 1% of wealth; will happily pay for speed/ease because their time is more valuable
- Virtuousness cycle: high price → high margin → hire better people → better service → better reputation → word of mouth → higher demand → price goes up more
