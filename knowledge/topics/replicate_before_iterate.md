# Replicate Before Iterate

> Copy exactly first, then improve - don't assume you know better than the source.

## Framework
- Follow instructions to the T
- Once you replicate results, apply your general knowledge to refine
- Then diverge with confidence

## Why It Works
- Prevents false ownership claims from mentors
- Proves you can execute the blueprint before changing it
