# Talent Acquisition Funnel

> Build parallel talent funnel that mirrors your customer acquisition funnel

## Framework
- Customer side: lead gen → nurture → sales → onboarding → retention
- Talent side: app gen → app nurture → interviewing → onboarding → retention

## Key Takeaway
- Track talent metrics with same discipline as customer metrics
- Spending on talent acquisition has massive ROI (10x return)
