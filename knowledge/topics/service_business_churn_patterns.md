# Service Business Churn Patterns

> SMBs at $1,500–$3K/month stick 4–6 months; staying out of this zone requires super-cheap automation or high-touch sophistication

## Key Numbers
- SMB service at $450/week ($2K/month): average stick 4–6 months, will hit $100K/month ceiling
- Review/SEO at $300–$400/month: 30–40 month stickiness, much healthier

## Why It Works
- SMBs are volatile; business downturns cause churn regardless of service quality
- CAC increases as volume grows; margin compresses from constant acquisition

## Common Mistakes
- Trying to fix SMB churn by doing more (sales motion, nurture): wastes time, SMBs will churn anyway
- Staying in the middle band ($1,500–$3K): margins compress, CAC rises, founder burns out
