# Income Growth as Primary Lever

> Growing income unlocks compounding more than cutting expenses alone.

## Key Takeaway
- Income has infinite upside; expenses have finite downside
- $1K/month extra makes $13K in future dollars (13x ROI)

## Examples
- Gig work, selling online, promoting platforms, adsense revenue
- Every extra $1K/month = $10M in 50-year compounding (with investment)

## Claims
- Increasing income is more powerful than just reducing spending because there's infinite upside above your income but finite room below zero. — [how_the_top_1_actually_think_about_money_6BQ3whjWG3M.txt]
