# Equity vs Stake Distinction

> 5% equity stake is different from 5% partnership

## Key Takeaway
- Having a stake = owning piece of company
- Being a partner = shared decision-making and joint liability

## Framework
- 5% for $2M revenue means earning equity through sales performance
- But could generate $2M in own business instead, so equity is not leverage
