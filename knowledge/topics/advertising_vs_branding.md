# Advertising vs Branding

> Advertising informs; branding is the pairing that occurs as a result.

## Framework
- Advertising = letting people know about product exists
- Branding = association that forms between product and customer preference

## Example
- Dylan Mulvaney post was great advertising (high awareness) but bad branding (negative association for core audience)
