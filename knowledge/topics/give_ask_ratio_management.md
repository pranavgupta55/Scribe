# Give-Ask Ratio Management

> Balance between providing free value and requesting customer actions to maximize audience growth and monetization

## Key Numbers
- TV: 47 min giving, 13 min asking = 3.5:1 ratio
- Facebook: 4 content, 1 ad = 4:1 ratio
- Growing platforms: 10:1 or higher (give-give-give-ask strategy)

## Framework
- Integrated asks: advertise within every content piece (maintains 3:1+ ratio)
- Intermittent asks: 10 give posts, then 1 ask post
- Optimal: give until they ask (never stop giving public, ask happens privately)

## How-To / Method
- Advertise lead magnet or core offer; use PS statements for asks
- Match ask to content relevance for higher conversion

## Claims
- The give-ask ratio should be 3.5:1 minimum for content marketing; growing platforms use 10:1 ratios (give much more, ask rarely) to maximize audience growth — [100m_leads.txt]
