# Intelligence as Speed

> Intelligence is measured by learning velocity, not innate capacity - you can change the variable.

## Framework
- Intelligence = speed of learning (rate)
- Not intelligence = total amount you can learn (capacity)

## Why It Works
- If someone is 'quick' they need fewer iterations
- You can't change iterations needed but can change time between them

## Claims
- Intelligence is speed of learning - how quickly you change behavior in the same condition; it's a rate, not a fixed amount. — [you_think_you_re_not_smart_enough_REDPMH2BMrY.txt]
