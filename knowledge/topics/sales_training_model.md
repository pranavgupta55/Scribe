# Sales Training Model

> Daily morning role-play by sales stage keeps team sharp and identifies weak spots

## Framework
- 5-part rotation: Intro, Discovery, Offer, Objections/Looping, Hot-Topic-Of-Week
- Each stage rehearsed in group with role-play, not one-on-one
- Keeps script crisp so team doesn't "fuck up something"

## How-To / Method
- Scale with group training, not individual coaching
- Separate team-side training from one-on-one coaching
