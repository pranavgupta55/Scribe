# Value Equation Framework

> Four-element model determining how valuable people perceive your offer to be

## Framework
- Dream Outcome (what they want, how they want it)
- Perceived Likelihood of Achievement (will it work, proof, guarantees, social proof)
- Time Delay (how fast they get results)
- Effort and Sacrifice (what they give up, what they keep doing, hidden costs)

## Key Takeaway
- Maximize first two elements, minimize last two; small offer improvements compound massively

## How-To / Method
- Use 11 ways to increase perceived likelihood: own story, testimonials, volume of reviews, certifications, numbers/stats, expert endorsement, new approach, celebrity, guarantee, specific pain description, live demonstration
