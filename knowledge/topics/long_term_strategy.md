# Long-Term Strategy

> Chick-fil-A grew slower than Boston Market; Boston Market IPO'd early and imploded; Chick-fil-A is now gigantic.

## Examples
- Boston Market: aggressive growth + debt → IPO early → implosion
- Chick-fil-A: conservative, slow, steady growth → survived 30+ years → dominance

## Key Takeaway
- If 20-30 year horizon is your target, slow appropriately-risked growth wins
- Debt-fueled fast growth is riskier; one bad year can trigger covenant breach and failure
