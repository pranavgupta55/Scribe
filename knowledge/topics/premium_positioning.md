# Premium Positioning

> Branding allows you to command higher prices and stay competitive

## Framework
- Same product, different brand = different price and loyalty
- People pay premium for feeling cool, not just product quality

## Examples
- Post stuff next to things people like; they think your stuff is cool
- Remove the cool thing; your stuff is still cool
- Charge premium; people buy more; stay loyal

## Claims
- Brand arbitrage is only winning if you're operating at margins that competitors can't; need 70% brand for defensibility — [why_your_10m_brand_will_never_hit_100m_x3Ft7uuSlKM.txt]
