# Business Stage Diagnosis

> Identify if business is demand or supply constrained before solving problems.

## Framework
- Demand-constrained: can absorb 2x lead volume with existing infrastructure
- Supply-constrained: doubling leads requires new resources and infrastructure
- Corey is demand-constrained; can handle 2x leads immediately after hiring two employees and adding third vehicle

## Key Numbers
- Corey at Stage 5 (Productize) with 10-19 employees
- $1.25M revenue, 38% net margin, $479k profit, $60k debt

## Common Mistakes
- Fixing problems that worsen existing bottlenecks without understanding constraint type
- Many business owners confuse their constraint and solve the wrong problem
