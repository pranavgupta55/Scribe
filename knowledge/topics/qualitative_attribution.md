# Qualitative Attribution

> Simplest, lowest-tech method: ask customers where they first heard about you

## Framework
- Ask: where did you first discover me?
- Gives directional indication without complex tech
- Works for long-tail sales where conversion takes months

## Why It Works
- Customers remember first touchpoint better than 5th
- Can inform allocation decisions without full attribution model
