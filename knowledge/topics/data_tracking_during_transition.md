# Data Tracking During Transition

> Set up tracking now before it gets harder with multiple locations

## Framework
- Current constraint: don't know why customers buy what
- Set up tracking now while have bandwidth
- Will inform menu, pricing, operations decisions

## Why It Matters
- Each decision without data has greater stakes as you scale
- Easier to establish good habits with one location
