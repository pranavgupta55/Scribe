# Bait-and-Switch Prevention

> Don't build audience on unrelated content and hope they convert; choose content that naturally leads to your offer.

## Common Mistakes
- Built 250K on relationship advice, expected life coach training conversions
- Algorithm rewarded relationship content but trained audience for wrong intent
- Fix: start teaching what you sell, not what gets engagement
