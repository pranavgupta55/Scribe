# Skills Over Outcomes

> The actual outcome of work is the person you become, not the external result.

## Framework
- Venture fails → outcome zero
- But person gained skills, experience, judgment
- Skills compound on next venture

## Claims
- Profit in all labor exists even when outcomes fail - you become better through doing, regardless of the venture's success. — [you_re_not_a_victim_of_your_circumstances_keep_going_LOSLhlb5GKM.txt]

## Connections
- Both concepts relate to outcome and result. _(connects to: Continuous Improvement Vs Outcome Fixation)_
- Expert outcomes matter less than person-you-become from process; skill development creates external results as byproduct. _(connects to: Outcome Vs Process Orientation)_
- Work outcome is the person you become; more work compounds into more skill and productivity. _(connects to: Work As Identity)_
