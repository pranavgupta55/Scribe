# Situational Ethics

> Events have no inherent good or bad nature; judgment limits emotional range and is impossible until death.

## Framework
- Can't know if anything is good or bad until complete timeline ends
- Even knowing at death won't matter because you'll be dead
- Assigning judgment limits peaks and valleys unnecessarily

## Example
- Story of boy and horse: gift is good, injury is bad, exemption from war is good—can't judge until full timeline

## How-To / Method
- Whitewash all inherited judgments from childhood
- Actively rebuild what you believe good/bad based on your goals
- Accept things as simply occurring, not as inherently good or bad
