# Suffering Fixed Cost

> All life paths require equal suffering; the choice is what you get for that suffering.

## Framework
- Business painful when shrinking, plateauing, or growing
- Flavor of pain changes but cost constant across all paths
- Poor, rich, married, single, employees, entrepreneurs all suffer

## Key Takeaway
- Can't control suffering cost; can control destination
- Secret: doing lots of things you don't want to get what you want

## Implications
- Pick destination worth suffering for
- If all paths hurt same, pick one with biggest upside
