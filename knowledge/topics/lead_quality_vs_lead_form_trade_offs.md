# Lead Quality vs Lead Form Trade-offs

> Lead forms deliver lower show-up rates (5.25%) but similar or better CPA; funnel pages improve attendance but may increase cost-per-close.

## Findings
- Couplepreneurs tested lead forms vs. funnel pages for 1 year; lead forms won on CPA to close despite lower show-up %
- Logic: lead form attracts higher-intent buyers (they'll work to opt-in); funnel page attracts curiosity-driven tire-kickers
- Industry standard: funnel pages ~25% show-up; lead forms ~5%

## Key Takeaway
- Only metric that matters is cost-per-acquisition to close, not show-up % or lead quality scores
- If $1.5K CAC on $2.5K product is acceptable, lead-form path fine; if CAC rises to $2.5K, funnel may be needed
