# Learning Budget and Experimentation

> Allocate 1-10% of income monthly to education and calculated experimentation.

## Method
- Set learning budget at percentage of income (1%, 5%, 10%)
- Force spend on education or experiments knowing many fail
- Track which experiments unlock scaling opportunities

## Examples
- 10% of $300K/month = $30K learning budget
- Increased ad spend from $400 to $1,400/day (within learning budget)
- Experiment unlocked path to $2M+ monthly revenue
