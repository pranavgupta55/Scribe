# Accessibility as Differentiation

> Founder's cell phone access is a differentiator when underdog; becomes a liability at scale once credibility is proven

## Key Takeaway
- "You can reach me; they can't" works at launch
- At scale: "I've proven value; you don't need my cell" works better and protects founder

## Examples
- Early stage: "Go to those guys, you're a number. With me, you get my cell phone."
- At scale: "I can't give you my cell phone because I'm good enough and I've proven that."
