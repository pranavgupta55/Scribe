# Sales Behavior Analysis

> Top performers execute specific behaviors that rest of team can adopt through training

## Framework
- Study top 2 people per division
- Document what they do/say differently
- Make it team standard
- Test if team performance lifts with behavior adoption

## Connections
- Top performer behaviors can be extracted and taught to entire teams. _(connects to: Best Person Duplication)_
- Top sales behaviors are teachable; training concrete actions beats coaching abstract qualities. _(connects to: Sales Training And Behavioral Language)_
- Top performer behaviors teach; daily role-play identifies and fixes weak spots. _(connects to: Sales Training Model)_
- Sales performance hinges on structured training and clear processes. _(connects to: Group Role Play Sales Training)_
- Identify top performer behaviors and train team via group role-play; daily drills sharpen skills. _(connects to: Sales Training At Scale)_
- Top performer behaviors become training; process and script adherence matter more than talent. _(connects to: Sales Training And Script Execution)_
- Codifying founder sales behaviors into system enables team adoption; top performers execute specific behaviors others can replicate through training. _(connects to: Behavior Codification)_
- Model winners by observing specific behaviors, not asking why; top performers execute teachable actions others can adopt through training. _(connects to: Modeling High Performers)_
