# Hedgehog Concept

> In any domain, excellence comes from relentless focus and continuous refinement at one thing.

## Framework
- You must know what you're optimizing for
- All decisions measured against that one metric
- Distinctiveness comes from singular focus over time

## Why It Works
- Effort compounds when directed toward one outcome
- Prevents dilution across many mediocre efforts
- Creates competitive moat over time
