# Proof as Competitive Moat

> Real-world accomplishment and real-time demonstration are the two proof types; as stakes rise, proof becomes the primary differentiator.

## Framework
- Third-party proof: exits, revenue ($250M/year), companies, headcount—mention once, understood
- Demonstration proof: showing real-time expertise in customer calls, audits, problem-solving
- Elon has both: richest man in world = ultimate third-party proof

## Why It Works
- Consumers use proof to decrease their own personal risk when deciding to act on advice
- Real-time demonstration is harder to fake than past accomplishments
- Two-spoon approach: both types feed the proof machine
