# Market Selection

> Picking the right market matters more than offer or persuasion skills; look for growing markets with pain, purchasing power, and targeting ease.

## Four Indicators
- Massive Pain: unmet needs in health, wealth, or relationships
- Purchasing Power: audience must afford your solution
- Easy to Target: prospects must be findable and reachable
- Growing: market expanding, not shrinking (avoids headwinds)

## Importance Ranking
- Starving Crowd (market) > Offer Strength > Persuasion Skills
- Great market can succeed with bad offer and weak persuasion
- Normal market needs great offer to compensate for weak persuasion
- Bad market makes nothing work regardless of offer quality
