# The Pricing Virtuous Cycle

> Higher price → higher margins → better talent → better service → better reputation → more demand → raise price; the cycle compounds.

## Framework
- Start: high price for service
- Margins allow hiring top talent
- Better talent delivers better service
- Better service = better reputation
- Better reputation = more inbound demand
- More demand than supply = raise price again
- Loop repeats at higher price level

## Why It Works
- Unlike products with manufacturing limits, services only improve with pricing
- You filter customers to those who can afford quality
- Each cycle improves every dimension of the business
