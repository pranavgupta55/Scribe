# Before/After Operationalization

> Build before/after collection into the patient journey

## How-To / Method
- Immediate photo on day 0 (baseline)
- Photo on day 30
- Photo on day 60
- Optional: offer rebates (often not needed)
- Format: quick 30-second hook + before/after (can cover eyes if needed)
- Add pain-based hook ('If struggling with XYZ, we do this all the time')
