# Existing Vehicle Optimization

> Improve the business you have before pivoting; fix margins, fix operations, then pursue new models.

## How-To / Method
- Identify COGS leakage and operational inefficiency
- Target 10% margin improvement = ~60% bottom-line increase
- Execute via M&A (acquire, apply system) or internal efficiency
- Only pursue new model (licensing) after proving team scalability

## Key Numbers
- 10% margin improvement = 67% bottom-line increase
- M&A playbook: acquire + 6 months to hit your metrics
