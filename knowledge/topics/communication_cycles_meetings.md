# Communication Cycles & Meetings

> Three non-negotiable meetings per week (daily 10-min huddle, weekly 45-min team meeting, weekly 30-min one-on-ones) keep pulse and prevent drift.

## Daily Huddle
- Duration: 10 minutes max
- Content: Yesterday's sales count, cancellations, attendance records, client wins/losses, quick announcements, motivation
- Owner presence: Yes; as business grows, ops manager can run but owner rides shotgun

## Weekly Team Meeting
- Duration: 45 minutes scripted with time limits per role
- Agenda: Trainers (9-12 min) discuss vibe/wins/losses, challenge manager (16 min) reports progress, ops manager (13 min) covers numbers/goals, owner (19 min) covers promotions/praise
- Content: Data-driven (sales, cancels, billing issues), forward-looking (goals for next week), culture-building (praise peers)

## Weekly One-on-Ones
- Duration: 30 minutes per person; all back-to-back one day
- Content: 'What's going well? What's not? How can I help you win?'
- Leadership: Owner listens 80%, asks 20%; rarely tells but asks leading questions so employee figures it out
- Outcome: Clarifies expectations, uncovers obstacles, builds trust, prevents turnover
