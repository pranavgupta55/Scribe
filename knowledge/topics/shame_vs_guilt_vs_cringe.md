# Shame vs Guilt vs Cringe

> Define three different social signals people use to judge

## Framework
- Shame: breaking someone else's rules
- Guilt: breaking your own rules
- Cringe: defensive status play (signal you're changing status)

## Why It Matters
- If someone says 'that's cringe', they're actually saying 'I'm changing status relative to you/others'
- It's their insecurity, not your problem
