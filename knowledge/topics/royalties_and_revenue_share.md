# Royalties and Revenue Share

> Called royalties because they were paid to royals; they come off the top, meaning you get paid first regardless of profit manipulation.

## How-To / Method
- Set up rev share instead of profit share to avoid competitors overspending
- Top line revenue is harder to manipulate than profit
- When X happens (revenue milestone), you pay me

## Why It Works
- You get paid without worrying about the company's cost structure
- Can apply licensing and royalty arrangements to move up the compensation pyramid

## Claims
- Revenue share is superior to profit share because competitors can manipulate profit through overspending, but top-line revenue is fixed — [the_6_levels_of_making_money_rMJIOK_FgJk.txt]
