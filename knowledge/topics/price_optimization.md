# Price Optimization

> Stop competing on price by moving from commodity ($99-150/mo unlimited) to two distinct tiers with premium features and accountability.

## Key Takeaway
- Virtuous cycle: Higher prices → higher emotional investment → better results → fewer demanding clients → more service → higher conviction in sales
- Vicious cycle: Lower prices → lower investment → worse results → more demanding clients → less service budget → salesperson loses conviction

## Framework
- Large group: $199/month ($39-49/week) for 3x/week sessions with trainer-led workouts, meal plan, accountability
- Semi-private: $600/month ($125-175/week) for 1-on-4 training, personalized programming, higher attention
- Avoid no man's land: $250-399/month with 5-15 people doesn't serve either buyer well
- Same-priced tiers across all clients (no custom deals) enforces fairness and keeps fulfillment simple

## Examples
- Large-group buyer: $199/month pocket includes normal-income person with medium pain or wealthy person with low pain
- Semi-private buyer: $600/month pocket includes normal-income person with high pain or wealthy person with any pain level
- 80/20 split: 80% buy large group, 20% can upgrade to semi-private if they want more attention—generates 3-5x revenue per customer
