# Cost-Plus vs Value-Based Pricing

> Stop showing customers the math; use value-based pricing with high price anchoring.

## Framework
- Old: man-hours × rate + materials × market = complex, shows work, blocks sales
- New: customer sees one price, doesn't see calculations, price is as high as possible without sales rep cracking smile

## Why It Works
- Customer doesn't know hours or materials; they only know price and outcome
- Complexity of calculation made sales hard; simplicity enables higher pricing

## Claims
- Cost-plus pricing (hours × rate + materials) is complex and sales-blocking; switch to value-based pricing with high prices that sales presents without customer seeing calculations. — [helping_a_concrete_business_owner_fix_his_margins_f0AY76fE9GI.txt]
