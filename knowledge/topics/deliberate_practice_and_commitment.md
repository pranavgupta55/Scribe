# Deliberate Practice and Commitment

> Sustained, full effort on one skill produces results that beat competitors because persistence is rare

## Key Takeaway
- Learning to really try builds expertise; full commitment reveals how few people actually do this

## How-To / Method
- Take all discretionary effort, money, time, and resources and put it toward one thing
- Do it every single day for an extended period
- Take feedback and don't stop
