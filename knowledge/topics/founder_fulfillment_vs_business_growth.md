# Founder Fulfillment vs. Business Growth

> Growth requires trade-offs; wanting work-life balance AND $100M revenue is wanting cookies and a six-pack—you must choose

## Key Takeaway
- Regret comes from imagining upside without downside; trade-offs are always present
- Dissatisfaction stems from wanting both (cookies + six-pack); either want less or sacrifice more

## Framework
- Roofing founder at $6M can work 2–3 hours/week (chose work-life balance) or go all-in (chose growth)
- Can't have both; pick one, accept trade-off, commit

## Caveats
- Family time cannot be made up in long-term; profit can be
- Founders with 30–40 productive years have 4–5 big runs left; pick carefully
