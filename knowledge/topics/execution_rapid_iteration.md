> Build a strong team, produce massive volume, measure what works, double down on winners; $4 million, 1,000 hours, 35,000 pieces of content over 40 months.

## Key Numbers
- $4 million in team, equipment, vendors, studios, software
- 1,000 hours of on-camera recording
- 35,000 pieces of content created
- 7.8 million new followers across all platforms
- 2 billion impressions
- 1+ million books sold

## Process
- Step 1: Make as much good stuff as you can
- Step 2: Post it everywhere you can
- Step 3: Learn as much as you can from what works
- Repeat: Find patterns in data, double down on winners, cut losers

## Critical Insight
- Six key lessons extracted from massive volume of attempts, not theory
- Real data (RPM, book sales, opt-ins) drove decisions, not comments or vanity metrics
- The cost is investment to learn what actually works for your specific audience
