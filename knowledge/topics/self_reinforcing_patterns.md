# Self-Reinforcing Patterns

> Communities reinforce undefined language through social approval.

## Framework
- Join community that values certain words
- Say those words → get approval, acceptance, belonging
- Keep saying words even though you've never defined them
- Believe the words because the social reward is real

## Claims
- People say words they've never defined but believe them because of the positive social response they get; if a community rewards certain phrases (manifestation, frequencies), people adopt those words without understanding them. — [why_i_hate_the_word_mindset_5xWG6jb6vB8.txt]
