# Brand Play Investment

> High-growth service businesses need brand plays beyond direct response

## Why It Works
- Direct response (30-day ROAS): good for short-term but hits ceiling
- Branding (12-month ROAS): 5-10x higher returns but requires big upfront investment
- Book launch example: $6-7M investment with no immediate ROI, but it builds brand moat

## Key Takeaway
- National brands must do 70% branding + 30% direct response
- Local businesses can operate on just direct response longer
