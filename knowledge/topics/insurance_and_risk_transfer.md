# Insurance and Risk Transfer

> Buying and selling risk itself is the highest compensation tier; insurance companies get paid every month nothing happens.

## Key Takeaway
- When nothing happens, you still get paid; nothing becomes profit
- Insurance predates the tax code; oldest companies in the world are insurers
- There is no delivery besides taking on the risk

## Framework
- Insurance is a reverse lottery where everyone chips in if bad things happen
- Government takes on physical risk through military and borders, collects taxes in exchange
