# Skill Selection

> Find skills people already pay money for rather than inventing what you think is cool.

## How-To / Method
- Print your credit card or bank statement
- See what you personally spend money on
- Look at business functions: ads, content, funnels, outreach
- Pick one skill that's already monetized

## Framework
- 1-1-1 rule: one product, one avatar, one channel until $1 million
- Don't overwhelm yourself learning a zillion things
