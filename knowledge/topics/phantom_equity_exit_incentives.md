# Phantom Equity & Exit Incentives

> Grant phantom profits interest (no control, no risk, no dilution) that vests annually and pays out at sale.

## How-To / Method
- Offer 4% phantom profits interest total, vesting 1% per year
- Payout only if employed 12-24 months and present at sale
- Can accelerate full 4% at sale if desired

## Key Takeaway
- Employee gets cash flow + exit upside, you keep control and risk
- Structured as option, so taxes due as income, not equity capital gains

## Why It Works
- Solves equity ask without surrendering control; trades cash flow + phantom equity for risk
