# RFP Frame Strategy

> Frame every sales conversation as an RFP-minded buyer would think, then add value frame on top

## How-To / Method
- RFP frame (bottom): Have the case study close ready that mirrors their situation
- Value frame (top): Show all the other things you can do beyond the RFP
- Combined: Win the RFP with proof, then offer additional consulting opportunities

## Key Takeaway
- RFP-minded buyers think in checklist terms initially, then shift to value once they see proof of outcomes
