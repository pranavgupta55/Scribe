# Business-Focused Content Strategy

> Narrow content focus to business topics to attract ideal business owner audiences.

## Framework
- Focus on: business models, leverage, selling, scaling, profit
- Avoid: relationships, college, food, lifestyle (attract different audiences)

## Why It Works
- People watching relationship content want more relationship content
- Business owners want business content, not lifestyle content
- Narrowing audience increases relevance and conversion
