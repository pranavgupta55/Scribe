# Victim Mindset vs. Agency

> 'Must be easy' externalization robs you of all power; real power comes from accepting responsibility

## Framework
- Victim: 'I can't achieve X because circumstances are against me' → removes agency
- Agent: 'I will do X despite circumstances' → keeps power

## Key Takeaway
- Power beats outcomes: better to have power and nothing than have everything while being life's bitch
