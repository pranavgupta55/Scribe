# Sales Framework

> Structured consultative process that discovers needs rather than pushes features

## How-To / Method
- Step 1: Why are they there?
- Step 2: What's the problem?
- Step 3: What have they tried?
- Step 4: Where did they fail?
- Step 5: Show how product differs
- Step 6: Ask permission to explain
- Step 7: Explain via experiences (outcomes), not features
- Step 8: Use analogies to explain experiences
- Step 9: Close
