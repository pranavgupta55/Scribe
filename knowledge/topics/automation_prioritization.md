# Automation Prioritization

> Focus on high-leverage, high-impact automation first: expectation setting, CAC, brand patterns, back office.

## Framework
- Priority 1: Expectation setting (customer-facing, affects conversion)
- Priority 2: CAC measurement (drives unit economics)
- Priority 3: Brand controllables (pattern detection; machines better than humans)
- Priority 4: Back office (already mature tools; implement immediately)

## Why It Works
- Tackles revenue-facing first, operational second
- Back office has low risk and high ROI; start there for quick wins
