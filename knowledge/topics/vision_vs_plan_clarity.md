# Vision vs. Plan Clarity

> You can defend a small area alone, but defending a block requires an army

## Framework
- Vision: the long-term destination (empire, 1000 salons)
- Plan: the singular focus needed right now to execute
- Trying to protect the entire block solo leads to burnout
