# Debt Management

> Debt increases business risk over time; prioritize elimination even on favorable terms.

## Framework
- Debt = compounding risk that tends to come due during seasonal/economic volatility
- Dave Ramsey approach: eliminate debt-first over Wall Street approach of leveraging growth

## Key Numbers
- Corey's $60k debt = 10% of annual profit
- Goal: eliminate in 4-5 months
- Debt payoff eliminates 5% of profit from expense but 100% of psychological headache

## Examples
- Enduring companies like Chick-fil-A operate debt-free
- Debt was likely taken to accelerate growth; eliminating it provides optionality for future growth
