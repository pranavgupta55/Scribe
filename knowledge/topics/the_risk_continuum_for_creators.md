# The Risk Continuum for Creators

> Creator vulnerability to AI scales with how easily consumption delivers complete value without ongoing behavior change.

## Framework
- Low-risk: entertainment (meme, comedy clip) = complete value in one consumption, purely time cost
- Mid-risk: B2C educator (hair, makeup, fitness) = teaches behavior change, lower stakes if it fails
- High-risk: B2B prosumer/enterprise = financial/business stakes, higher proof requirements

## Why It Works
- As stakes increase, consumers demand more proof before acting
- Entertainment needs no belief in source credibility; finance does
- AI excels at high-volume low-risk content; struggles with high-stakes personal accountability
