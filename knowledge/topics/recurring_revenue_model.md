# Recurring Revenue Model

> Teach a system once, then supply the consumable inputs monthly.

## Framework
- System (teach hair extensions, sales, fitness) = one-time payment
- Consumable inputs (hair stock, supplements, accountability) = monthly payment
- Like printer: sell once, sell ink every month

## Why It Works
- Finding new customers costs more than retaining; consumables drive retention
- Customers value convenience of pre-researched, curated input
- Business captures profit from effort (research, analysis, distillation)
