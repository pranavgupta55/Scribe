# Entertainment vs. Education Audiences

> Entertainment and education audiences are distinct; one doesn't convert to the other.

## Key Takeaway
- Entertainment people want more entertainment
- Education people want more education
- Almost zero conversion between these groups

## Implications
- Stop treating content as a funnel (entertainment → education → customer)
- Business outcomes come from education, not entertainment reach
