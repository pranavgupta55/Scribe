# Accounts Receivable Financing

> Use AR financing to bridge gap between billing and payment; it's cheaper than changing profitable model.

## Framework
- AR financing: line of credit secured by outstanding invoices
- Cost: ~1% per month (very cheap because partially secured by AR)
- Process: show bank lender your collection rate and cash flow

## How-To
- Find bank offering 90-day rotating AR credit line
- Document: revenue, AR aging, collection %, payment terms
- Draw against AR; pay back when customer pays

## Key Takeaway
- Solves $500-600K AR problem without changing business model
- Cost is 1% monthly (~12% annually); much cheaper than loss of efficiency from model change
