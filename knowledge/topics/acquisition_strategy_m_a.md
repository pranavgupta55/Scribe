# Acquisition Strategy (M&A)

> Buy smaller practices, apply your operational system; 6-month transformation gets you to your metrics.

## How-To / Method
- Find existing practice $500K-$1M top line
- 6-month timeline: internal training, systems implementation, quality assurance
- Result: practice now hits founder's metrics

## Key Takeaway
- Faster than hiring new operators; you control the playbook
