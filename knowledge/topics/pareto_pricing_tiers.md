# Pareto Pricing Tiers

> 20% of customers have majority of wealth. Price at 4 tiers with 5-10x gaps; expect 20% takeup per step up.

## Framework
- Tier 1 base: $X
- Tier 2: $X × 5-10 (20% takeup)
- Tier 3: Tier 2 × 5-10 (20% takeup)
- Tier 4: Tier 3 × 5-10 (20% takeup)
