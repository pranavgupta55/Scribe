# Free Trial Plus Penalty Model

> Give away work free, backed by card penalty if client doesn't do behaviors; converts faster than sales-heavy approach.

## How-To / Method
- Client puts card down, do all service work free
- If they don't do required behaviors, charge them
- Advantage: removes objection risk; you absorb it

## Why It Works
- Removes proof friction; you're confident enough to work free
- Generates testimonials faster than cold sales
