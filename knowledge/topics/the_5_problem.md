# The 5% Problem

> If successful, 95% of daily work is supporting your 5% passion; must accept this reality.

## Framework
- Passion only exists in vague not specific
- Brief moments of core work buried in supporting tasks
- Assuming passion doesn't change, which it will

## Examples
- Thought gym ownership = constant workout + meals with friends
- Reality: miserable managing business; went back to workout as hobby

## Implications
- Can only do passion as employee (same role) or solopreneur not scaling
- Business owner must love business ownership not the core craft
