# Free Content Creation

> Public one-to-many communication through written, video, audio, or image content to grow warm audience

## Framework
- Hook (grab attention in first 5 seconds)
- Retain (use lists, steps, or stories to keep curiosity alive)
- Reward (deliver on the promise with clear value)

## Key Numbers
- 10x more content = 10x faster growth (verified by Facebook: top 0.1% test 11x more creative)
- Content breaks even on audience growth at 3.5:1 give-ask ratio minimum

## How-To / Method
- Five topic sources: Far Past (lessons), Recent Past (stories from meetings), Present (note ideas as they happen), Trending (tie to current events), Manufactured (create experience to document)
- Headlines use recency, relevancy, celebrity, proximity, conflict, unusual, or ongoing story elements
- Format content to match platform expectations (reels, stories, feeds, etc.)
- Use lists/steps/stories to embed unresolved questions
- Make longer content by linking content units; short form easier to start

## Examples
- Alex's podcast grew from zero to top 100 US business podcast over 5 years of consistent 2x/week uploads
- $100M Offers split 1/4 each between ebook, physical, audio, video formats for 3-4x reach vs. single format

## Implications
- Make content for strangers (no inside jokes); loyal audience won't mind reminders
- Manually posting beats pre-scheduling due to feedback loop pressure to maintain quality

- Free Content Creation is enabled by the Content Unit Structure (Hook, Retain, Reward) which provides the three-component formula that works across any platform or medium. _(connects to: Content Unit Structure)_
