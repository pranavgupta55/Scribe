# Weak to Strong Brand Progression

> Weak brand (unknown logo) becomes strong brand through deliberate association with positive outcomes.

## Framework
- Weak: brand means nothing, logo is just a logo
- Pair: with things customers like
- Strong: brand starts to mean what it's paired with
- Loyalty: customers buy product to associate with that meaning

## Outcome
- Commoditized product transforms to premium product
- Generic white t-shirt becomes premium-priced branded t-shirt

- A weak brand paired with what customers like creates a strong brand; a strong brand on a commodity product (white t-shirt) transforms it to premium product — [what_branding_actually_is_and_how_to_build_one_fast_Vt4D1qgo9ic.txt]
