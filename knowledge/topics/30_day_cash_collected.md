# 30-Day Cash Collected

> Collect CAC + cost of goods sold within 30 days; enables cash-positive customer acquisition.

## Framework
- Goal: Cash collected in month 1 ≥ CAC + COGS
- Once hit, cycle repeats: customer funds next customer acquisition

## Why It Works
- No need to wait for product delivery to recoup costs
- Cash flow-positive from day one, enabling rapid scaling

## Variations
- Manufacturing: expect different COGS structure, still aim for 80%+ gross
- Services: same principle applies
