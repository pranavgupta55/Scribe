# Attribution & Data Problems

> Spending increased 90% with 100% revenue growth, but no attribution system makes optimization impossible; LTV/CAC metrics show impossibility, revealing hidden tracking failure

## Key Takeaway
- Impossibly low metrics (0.41:1 LTV:CAC) signal data problem, not business problem
- Post-purchase surveys contradict platform data; true source invisible without proper tracking

## Implications
- Cannot scale beyond threshold without knowing which channels work
- Optimization requires high-confidence attribution across all traffic sources
