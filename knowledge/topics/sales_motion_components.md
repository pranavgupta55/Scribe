# Sales Motion Components

> Complete sales funnel has multiple components: awareness, indoctrination, qualification, close

## Framework
- Organic/paid traffic
- Indoctrination process (VSL/video/webinar)
- Qualification (application or pre-call questions)
- Sales call (now just closing)
- Fast cash play (onboarding activation)

## Key Takeaway
- High-touch services need multiple components
- Treat sales motion as full funnel, not just phone call

## Connections
- Complete sales funnel has multiple components; systematization works for membership and bundled models. _(connects to: Sales Motion Systematization)_
- Consultative sales discovery needs complete funnel components—awareness, indoctrination, qualification, and close—to work at scale. _(connects to: Sales Framework)_
- VSL, qualification, and training cadence move cold close from 6% to 20%+ by adding awareness, indoctrination, and closing structure. _(connects to: Sales Motion Architecture)_
