# The 4-4-4 Time Split

> Structure your day as 4 hours promotion, 4 hours delivery, 4 hours building when not constrained by a day job.

## Framework
- First 4 hours: let people know about your stuff (nobody buys if they don't know)
- Second 4 hours: give customers what you promised them
- Third 4 hours: figure out what to do next and how to build it

## Why It Works
- Balances customer acquisition, satisfaction, and future innovation
- Ensures you promote (so you get customers), deliver (so you get testimonials), build (so you grow)
