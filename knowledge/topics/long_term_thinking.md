# Long-Term Thinking

> Long-term thinking is last remaining alpha; few people think 1-5-10 years out.

## Framework
- If under 30 with no responsibilities, work maximum hours possible
- Never have fewer responsibilities than now
- Compounding of early effort sets up entire life for decades

## Examples
- Pro athletes late career: less strength/quickness but better technique and timing
- Youth has unlimited time but no technique; age has technique but limited time

## Implications
- Dig well before thirsty
- Get head above water first (pay bills), then think long-term
