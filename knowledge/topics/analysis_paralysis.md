# Analysis Paralysis

> Don't get stuck at base of mountain deciding route; commit to one path for 18 months minimum

## Key Takeaway
- Waiting is the cost; it's easy to compare paths and second-guess
- Pick any path, commit hard, assess after 18 months

## Examples
- Founder started YouTube with 10-year time horizon, not 90-day goal
- First 3 years got 3,000 downloads/month; now over 1M/month; patience paid off

## Caveats
- The switching cost is often underestimated in moment
- People who jump between paths never fully climb any mountain
