# Affiliate Program Building

> Recruiting army of independent businesses to sell and promote your offer; highest leverage lead getter at scale

## Key Numbers
- ALAN: 1 super-affiliate → 10 agencies → 50 local businesses → 2,500 leads/month → $12,500 first month, growing exponentially each month thereafter
- Prestige Labs: $3M inventory, $1M affiliate software investment; $150K week 3 to $450K week 4+ (300+ orders/day, 400+ affiliates)

## Framework
- Six steps: find ideal affiliates → make them offer → qualify them → set payout structure → get them advertising (launch) → keep them advertising (integrate)

## How-To / Method
- Find affiliates by reverse-engineering best customers: who do they buy from, where do they go, what do they do, who employs them
- Make offer showing value (dream outcome without effort/sacrifice, fast, with proof)
- Qualify via investment (buy product or pay for certification; 10-20% of avg affiliate annual revenue)
- Pay via three-tier system: 25% CAC tier 1 (signup), 50% CAC tier 2 (activation), 100% CAC tier 3 (performance)
- Launch using whisper-tease-shout: create curiosity early → reveal details 30 days before → drive urgency last 3 days
- Integrate: 1) give away lead magnet with their sales, 2) sell lead magnet separately, 3) sell core offer directly; all three work

## Examples
- Tax prep: free LLC setup with entrepreneur training seminars; integrate into core package
- Chiropractor: free posture workshop at gyms; integrate as 1-2 free adjustments with new gym membership
- Supplements: free nutrition consult at gyms; integrate supplement sales at consults
