# Sales Scalability

> Shift from founder demonstrating expertise to salesman asking questions; make prospect the hero, not salesman.

## Framework
- Salesman's only job: ask questions in right order
- Salesman should NOT provide answers or demonstrate expertise
- Prospect discovers own solution through questions

## Why It Works
- Prospect owns the decision; doesn't rely on salesman authority
- Anyone can learn to ask questions; not everyone can replicate founder expertise
- Repeatable regardless of who is selling

## Claims
- Scalable sales is prospect-based, not salesman-based; all knowledge must be about questions in correct order, not salesman demonstrating expertise — [why_you_re_still_the_only_closer_in_your_business_fECT6E27oi8.txt]
