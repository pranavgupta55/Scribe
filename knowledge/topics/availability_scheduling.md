# Availability & Scheduling

> Availability is strongest predictor of show rate; businesses with most time slots had most schedules, shows, purchases. Need 7 days/week, extended hours, flexible slots, and multiple booking methods.

## Key Numbers
- 20-40% increase in show rates common after implementing availability tactics; can reach 200%+ improvement in some cases

## Claims
- Businesses with most available time slots had most schedules, shows, and purchases; availability is biggest single predictor of show rate, more important than speed — [100m_playbook_lead_nurture.txt]
- Leads scheduled 3 days out or closer show significantly higher than leads scheduled 5+ days out; limiting scheduling to 72-hour window is recommended — [100m_playbook_lead_nurture.txt]
