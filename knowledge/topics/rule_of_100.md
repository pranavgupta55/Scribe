# Rule of 100

> Commit to 100 consecutive days of 100 deliberate actions in one channel; volume breaks through.

## How-To / Method
- 100 days × 100 actions = 10,000 total actions in one acquisition direction
- Typical result: first customer by week 3 (but commit to full 100 days)
- Applies at all business levels: scale existing channel or new channel same way

## Key Insight
- Feast-or-famine feeling = insufficient volume, not inconsistent leads
- Companies 30x larger typically do 30x more advertising/content (e.g., 25,000 pieces/year vs 365)

## Common Mistakes
- Trying to do it 'to prove wrong'—won't work; requires genuine commitment
- Optimizing early before hitting critical volume; diminishing returns still yield returns
