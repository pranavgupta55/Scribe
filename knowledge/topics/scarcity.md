# Scarcity

> Limited supply of slots, bonuses, or exclusive access creates FOMO and enables higher pricing without changing the offer itself.

## Three Types
- Limited Supply of Seats: cap new clients at X per week/month
- Limited Supply of Bonuses: only available with this offer, never for sale
- Never Available Again: one-time release, never recreated

## Key Numbers
- Arnold's fundraiser: cut tickets $15k→$25k, raised extra $1M from 100 people
- Demand is fractal (80/20): 1/5 of prospects will pay 5x the base price
- Always sell out or let people know you sold out to build desire for next launch

## How-To / Method
- Honest scarcity: simply state real limits (capacity, time)
- Cohort-based caps: only X clients per month or quarterly cohort
- Business-wide caps: only X clients total, building waiting list
- Rotate between scarcity tactics to maintain effectiveness over time
