# Betrayal Consequences

> One betrayal obliterates trust accumulated over years

## Why It Works
- Trust is built incrementally over time through zero punishment
- But brain weights single negative event extremely heavily
- Like a tree cut from nutrients—still visible but dead inside

## Key Numbers
- Can trust someone for 20 years, one betrayal ends it: zero trust remaining

- A single betrayal undoes all accumulated trust—one punishing event can obliterate 20 years of reinforcement because the brain weights the negative heavily — [100m_ceo_explains_how_to_find_people_you_can_trust_k5-57282taI.txt]
- Trust requires zero punishment over time—you give small things, they don't betray, you give more, they don't betray. Eventually the incentive to betray becomes so high they do, destroying the entire relationship — [100m_ceo_explains_how_to_find_people_you_can_trust_k5-57282taI.txt]
