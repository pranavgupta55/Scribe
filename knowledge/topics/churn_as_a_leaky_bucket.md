# Churn as a Leaky Bucket

> MRR grows when new members exceed churn; track joins vs. cancels monthly and classify growth trajectory.

## Framework
- MRR grows = new members > churn members
- Flat growth = new members = churn members
- Decline = new members < churn members
- Track joins vs. cancels monthly to label business as growing, flat, or declining

## Connections
- Churn is the hidden growth constraint; track monthly joins vs. cancels to reveal growth trajectory. _(connects to: Churn As Growth Ceiling)_
