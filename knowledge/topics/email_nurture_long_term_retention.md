# Email Nurture & Long-Term Retention

> 2x weekly email commitment rotating before/after transformations, cool projects, and FAQ; long-term list building for repeat purchases

## Key Numbers
- Email list: 10k at $2.5M (very small list, high quality)
- Frequency: 2x per week

## Email Structure
- Email 1 (before/after): Image hook, caption, CTA link, P.S. discount or joke
- Email 2 (cool shit): New project transformations, same structure
- Email 3 (FAQ): Rotate common objections (Who installs? Shipping cost? etc.)

## Why It Works
- Keeps brand top-of-mind for next expansion/railing need
- Compounding effect: snowball builds over time as list grows and opens increase
