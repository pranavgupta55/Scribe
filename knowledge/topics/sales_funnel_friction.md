# Sales Funnel Friction

> Good friction removes bad leads and improves close efficiency; bad friction pushes everyone away

## Framework
- Bad friction: slow pages, confusing signup, high wall
- Good friction: qualification questions, application, VSL
- Good friction increases cost per contact but ROAS even more

## Examples
- Add checkbox 'want VIP ticket?' on opt-in: 16% sales increase, changed nothing else
- Pre-framing power of questions affects downstream purchasing
