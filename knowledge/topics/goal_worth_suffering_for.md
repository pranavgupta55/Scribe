# Goal Worth Suffering For

> Biggest alpha is selecting meaningful destination worth the fixed suffering cost.

## Framework
- Belief in ability to figure it out is only belief needed to win
- Entrepreneurs who stick uncommon path earn disproportionate returns
- Must be only one who believes in you sometimes

## Examples
- 50%+ high school seniors delusionally think they'll be millionaire by 25
- Most don't realize volume/suffering required

## Why It Works
- Big enough goal justifies the hard path
- Meaning pulls through uncertainty and repeated failure
