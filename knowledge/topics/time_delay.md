# Time Delay

> Duration between purchase and benefit realization; includes long-term outcomes and short-term experience milestones.

## Key Takeaway
- Short-term wins keep customers engaged on path to long-term outcome
- Fast beats free; people pay more for speed than accept free solutions
- Liposuction (instant) commands 25x price over fitness (12+ months)
- Create immediate emotional wins to reinforce purchase decision

## How-To / Method
- Get first $2,000 sale or first result within 7 days of purchase
- Create visible milestones and progress indicators early
- Provide momentum wins to keep clients committed to full journey
- Celebrate short-term achievements alongside long-term goals
