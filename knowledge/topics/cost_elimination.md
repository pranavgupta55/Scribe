# Cost Elimination

> Cut all living costs to accumulate cash flow for reinvestment.

## How-To / Method
- No eating out; only buy from discount grocery stores
- Reuse all current clothing for two years minimum
- Live in shared house splitting bedroom for $300-400/month
- Get a paid-off clunker car or pay off car completely

## Why It Works
- Creates cash flow fluff for reinvestment in skills
- You're already spending all money you make; need to decrease downside to have capital
