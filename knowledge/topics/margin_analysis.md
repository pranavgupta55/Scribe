# Margin Analysis

> Service business margins should target 30% minimum, not industry average

## Key Numbers
- Current net margin: 14%
- Industry average: ~10-15%
- Target margin: 30%+

## Common Mistakes
- Using industry averages as goals
- Growing revenue without improving unit economics

- Net margin flat year-over-year despite significant growth ($4M to $8M to $11M to $15M) — [how_to_triple_profit_without_growing_revenue_9Xmcfgf1tsc.txt]
