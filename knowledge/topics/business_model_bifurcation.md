# Business Model Bifurcation

> All client-acq agencies end at one of two poles: low-cost-high-volume or premium-whales.

## Framework
- Low-cost-high-volume: $300-400/mo, high retention, almost zero CAC, profitable day-one
- Premium-whales: upmarket, high ticket, they don't need help closing, just deal flow

## Why It Works
- Middle market (current $5K+/mo) is painful; churn factory; bad avatar
