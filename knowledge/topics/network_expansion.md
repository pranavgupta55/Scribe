# Network Expansion

> Geographic hubs and consistent networking expand luck surface area and accelerate opportunity clustering

## Framework
- Hubs: NYC (finance), Hollywood (film), DC (politics)
- Meeting people daily (coffee, gym, events) expands luck surface area
- Fastest way to change life = change people around you

## How-To / Method
- Move to hub where your target industry congregates
- Network daily: coffee shops, gyms, industry events
- Surround yourself with people already doing what you want

## Why It Works
- People in hubs have higher density of relevant opportunities and introductions
- Proximity + shared context accelerates relationship formation and collaboration
