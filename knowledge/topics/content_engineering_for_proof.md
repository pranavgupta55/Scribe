# Content Engineering for Proof

> Build content systems that extract real moments from business activity and package them as proof.

## How-To / Method
- Look at calendar, list all meetings (especially virtual), transcribe each
- Ask AI: tell me the interesting moments from these meetings
- Share real examples from the transcripts rather than made-up stories

## Why It Works
- No extra time: already doing the meetings
- Proof is baked in (real conversations, real problems, real solutions)
- Volume scales without effort multiplying
