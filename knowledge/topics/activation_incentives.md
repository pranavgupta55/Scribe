# Activation Incentives

> All effort targets activation; once a user places a fence deal, the product works and churn collapses

## How-To / Method
- Charge $199 upfront with conditional rebate
- Direct every new customer into immediate onboarding
- Assign staff to be on-call for technical help during trial
- Walk customer through first fence placement to close loop

## Why It Works
- User sees tool in action and closing a deal
- Success built in, not left to chance
- LTV:CAC improves dramatically once churn drops

## Claims
- Do NOT scale ads until fixing churn—pouring money into a funnel with high activation failure just burns cash — [i_built_an_app_and_no_one_s_using_it_-Le_gCaW_5c.txt]
- Free trial with penalty: charge $199 upfront; if user sells a fence 2-3 times in 10 days, rebate the full $199, or make second month free — [i_built_an_app_and_no_one_s_using_it_-Le_gCaW_5c.txt]
- Offer support during trial: staff on-call for tech issues, sign users up to onboarding immediately, walk them through one fence placement to ensure activation — [i_built_an_app_and_no_one_s_using_it_-Le_gCaW_5c.txt]
