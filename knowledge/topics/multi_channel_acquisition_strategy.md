# Multi-Channel Acquisition Strategy

> Local service businesses succeed via multi-channel: paid ads short-term, content long-term; focus on highest-ROI channel based on constraint

## Examples
- Chiropractor: 50% referral, 20% paid ads, 20% organic Google (probably SEO)
- WAAS: 100% cold calling outbound; double down on inbound (paid ads), not product innovation

## Key Takeaway
- If demand-constrained, scale highest-performing channel with data (paid ads work faster)
- If supply-constrained, don't add channels; fix capacity first, then marketing

## How-To / Method
- Tier 1: paid ads (immediate, data-driven, scales fast)
- Tier 2: content + thought leadership (long-term, expands radius, premium pricing)
