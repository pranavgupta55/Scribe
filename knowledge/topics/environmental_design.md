# Environmental Design

> Change surroundings to increase likelihood of desired behavior; reference group matters more than time spent.

## Framework
- Harvard study: number one correlate to wealth is reference group (who you compare to), not who you spend time with
- Want to get fit? Get around fitter people (deprivation effect motivates)
- Want to get rich? Get around people who make more money

## How-To / Method
- Identify desired behavior
- Identify conditions that increase likelihood of that behavior
- Stack the deck in your favor by changing environment

## Claims
- Getting around fitter people deprives you of fitness and motivates you; getting around wealthier people creates motivation to increase income — [a_lifetime_of_personal_development_in_18_minutes_PsxOnxIU274.txt]
