# Focus Over Resources

> Do one thing that 10xs company instead of 100 things

## Framework
- Question: if only one thing is true in 12 months and would 10x company, what is it?
- Answer: 3-5 big creators driving audience to platform
- Therefore: remove everything not that one thing

## Why It Matters
- Clarifies priorities
- Keeps team focused on highest leverage
