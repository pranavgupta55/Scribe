# Licensing Model

> Instead of agency model, licensed creative to gym owners who already knew how to run ads

## Key Takeaway
- Gym owners didn't need Gym Launch to run ads (already had skill)
- Only needed fresh creative monthly; Gym Launch just fed the black box

## Why It Works
- Scaling ads to local market is easy: drop a pin, target female demographic, run
- Gym owners ran to pre-tested pages (Gym Launch owned them)
- No need for 100 account reps; scalable through product, not people

## Framework
- Provide the skill teach, then license the output (creative)
- Customer runs the tactic, you provide the asset
