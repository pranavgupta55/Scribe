# Audience Awareness Pyramid

> Larger audiences require proportionally better creative; ads scale by improving quality, not by just increasing spend.

## Framework
- Most Aware (warmest, smallest): customer knows product, needs deal
- Product-Aware: knows product, unsure if right
- Solution-Aware: wants result, doesn't know product can deliver
- Problem-Aware: senses problem, unaware of solutions
- Completely Unaware (coldest, largest): unaware of problem itself

## Why It Works
- Ad quality required increases as audience awareness decreases
- Colder traffic requires stronger creative to convert
- Old Spice example: strong creative converted 70% of market despite audience not initially seeking it

- Most ads hit a wall when targeting expands from warm audiences to cold audiences; better creative quality, not market saturation, is the limiting factor — [100m_playbook_goated_ads.txt]
