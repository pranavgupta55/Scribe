# Time Value of Money in Wealth Building

> Early money compounds 2-3x more than late money due to time horizon.

## Key Numbers
- 10-year difference: 33x (start at 28) vs. 80x (start at 18) compounding
- $1K/month at 18 = $10M at 67; same at 28 = $3.3M
- Age gap advantage: 2.4x multiplier despite potentially lower early income

## Implications
- Don't flex small earnings when young - time is irreplaceable asset
- Low early earnings still worth more than later earnings
