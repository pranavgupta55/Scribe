# UGC Content Loop

> Shift from 1-2 internal ads to 20-30 community-generated videos per week, testing to find winners.

## How-To / Method
- Ask customers to post 60-second trip montages to unlock extra checklist/training
- Collect and re-post videos as organic content first
- When video wins, add 5-second CTA and run as paid ad (double dip)
- Blast 20-30 UGC videos weekly; identify winners, juice them with variants

## Why It Works
- Product is highly visual; customers already want to share trips
- Real user content converts better than polished brand content
- Decentralizes content production burden away from team

## Examples
- Travel pages with 1-2M followers post trip montages getting gazillions of views
- Real people in first-class seats > professional brand ads
