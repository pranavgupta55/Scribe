# Lead Generation and Sales Strategy

> Single-channel focus beats multi-channel chaos; separate inbound leads (high-cost) from outbound (high-effort); choose channel matching team skill.

## Framework
- Do not mix inbound and outbound sales—sales reps will choose easy inbound (fed leads) over hard outbound (door-knocking)
- Purchased leads at $7,500 CAC against $6,500 average profit do not work unless offer is differentiated or lead quality improves
- One sales channel focused beats multiple unfocused channels; all channels (email, social, LinkedIn, in-person networking) can work if one is optimized

## How-To / Method
- Pick sales channel matching team's highest skill overlap (e.g., if networking in-person is strength, scale from 3-4 events per week to 3-4 per day)
- For roofing companies with historical door-knocking skill, separate door-knockers from inbound-lead closers; only top performers ('savages') graduate to inbound
