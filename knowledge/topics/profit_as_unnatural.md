# Profit as Unnatural

> Profit is unnatural because people naturally spend money when it's available; maintaining profit requires active resistance.

## Key Takeaway
- People spend money they have in their bank account
- Success has constant pressure of normalcy fighting it
- Someone must hold the line: we make more without hiring more

## Framework
- Get more customers and service them better without adding headcount
- Do it faster and with less resources
- This requires someone constantly fighting normal human tendency to spend
