# Influence Stack

## Key Takeaway
- Influence = high likelihood of compliance with requests
- Requests can be soft (subscribe, watch) or hard (buy, attend event)

- Example: trust fund kid has status (money)
- Kid with money + 10 proven stock picks = status + power = much higher influence
