# Conscious vs Unconscious Trades

> Worst trades are the unconscious ones where you lose on both sides.

## Key Takeaway
- Sacrifice your 20s AND don't build skills for your 30s = getting nothing
- Either sacrifice for skills or don't sacrifice for experiences, but pick one
- Conscious trade means getting value for the cost

## Framework
- Always conscious of what you're trading
- Always conscious what you're trading it for
- Question: do you want their life—not whether they approve

- Worst trades are the ones you don't consciously make; you sacrifice your 20s without building skills for your 30s, getting neither fun nor foundation — [how_to_change_your_life_MZPVPCIeUpg.txt]
