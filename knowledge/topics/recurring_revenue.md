# Recurring Revenue

> GC recurring work is better than residential repaints; 20-to-1 LTV to CAC justifies capital intensity.

## Key Numbers
- Residential repaint: transactional, high CAC, lower LTV
- GC work: recurring by same general contractor, very high LTV to CAC ratio
- GC LTV to CAC: 20:1 (exceptional)

## Key Takeaway
- Don't abandon 20:1 machine because of cash timing issue
- Use financing to solve timing; keep the profitable model
