# Passive vs. Active Income Management

> Keep passive investments passive (REITs, funds); active money returns more than passive, so focus energy on main business

## Key Takeaway
- Real estate funds + REITs generate hands-off returns; putting in $200K earns better than market
- But time spent making real estate active = opportunity cost in main business (higher-return)

## Examples
- Roofing founder (ADHD, collects gold/silver, buys buildings): fine if passive, not fine if active
- Don't fold secondary business into main (e.g., roofer building custom gazebos on real estate)

## Implications
- Active energy should flow to the business with highest ROI and leverage
- Passive income is lifestyle asset, not strategic focus; don't let it consume founder bandwidth
