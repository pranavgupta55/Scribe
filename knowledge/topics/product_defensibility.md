# Product Defensibility

> Legal defensibility (patents) expensive and weak at small scale; brand is more cost-effective long-term moat

## Key Takeaway
- No patent protection = vulnerable to dupes at scale
- Legal enforcement too expensive until you're large enough to justify legal team

## Framework
- Small scale: build brand instead of fighting legal battles
- Large scale: strong brand makes legal easier (PE will back patent enforcement)

## Caveats
- If product has true patent defensibility, pursue legal route at scale
- If not, must build brand moat
