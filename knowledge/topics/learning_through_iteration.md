# Learning Through Iteration

> Real learning requires behavior change through feedback loops, not just content consumption.

## Key Takeaway
- If listening to content doesn't change your behavior, you're not learning
- Malcolm Gladwell's 10,000 hours is actually 10,000 iterations
- Iterations assume you fail and get feedback

## Framework
- Do lots of volume to generate feedback
- Analyze top 10% outcomes to see what they do differently
- Identify the most important differentiating details
- Try variations one at a time to isolate what works
