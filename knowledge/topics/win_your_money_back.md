# Win Your Money Back

> Customers put money down and get it back (or as credit) if they hit goals or complete actions

## How-To / Method
- Set trackable criteria: results-based (lose Z lbs), actions-based (attend all sessions), or both
- Make criteria easy to track and aligned with customer goals (existing phone functions, photos, attendance)
- Three follow-up meetings during program create upsell opportunities; roll credit forward across longer/more expensive offers

## Key Numbers
- ~10% refund rate expected; only use offer if comfortable with this
- Credit should be rolled into 4x+ more expensive upsells (e.g. $600 credit toward $2,400+ year membership)
- Generated $1B+ in sales industry-wide

## Why It Works
- Removes customer risk; they only pay if they fail
- Advertises business (required criteria often include referrals, social posts, testimonials)
- Customers who win often stay as paying customers when next offer is ready
