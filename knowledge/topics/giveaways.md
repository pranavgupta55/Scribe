# Giveaways

> Advertise grand prize to get leads; after winner announced, offer everyone else the prize at discount

## How-To / Method
- Pick grand prize (what you want everyone to buy)
- Assign believable monetary value as price anchor
- Get eligibility questions and qualifying actions during entry
- Run 3-7 days with daily countdown updates showing benefits and social proof
- Announce winner, contact everyone else privately with 7-day claim deadline
- Explain cost-to-value using discount (e.g. $5k value for $1.8k = 64% value-to-cost gain vs 10% discount)

## Key Numbers
- Grand prizes valued $5k-$12k typically
- Promotional offers at 10-30% discount off retail
- Give away 2 prizes if referrer bonuses available (if referred person wins, referrer also wins)

## Implications
- If giveaway doesn't work, grand prize wasn't grand enough or poorly targeted
- Scarcity by time (7 days) or entry count critical
- Requires legal counsel on sweepstakes rules

## Claims
- Giveaways with grand prizes advertised at $5,000-$12,000 value followed by discounted promotional offers convert more leads when limited to 7-day timeframes or capped entry numbers. — [100m_money_models.txt]
