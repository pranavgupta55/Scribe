# Leverage Through Risk

> You are compensated in proportion to the risk you take on, specifically the perceived risk in the market's eyes.

## Key Takeaway
- People who do this well have risk be very real for others but not for them
- Market overcompensates for perceived risk
- You can shift terms by offering to take on risk

## Framework
- In any deal, the overarching question is: who has the risk?
- The more you shift risk in your favor, the more you get paid
