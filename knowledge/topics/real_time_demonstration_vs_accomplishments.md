# Real-Time Demonstration vs. Accomplishments

> Past accomplishments are static signal; real-time demonstration is dynamic proof that's harder to fake.

## How-To / Method
- Instead of saying 'I sold a company,' show yourself advising a real customer on a real problem
- Instead of 'I scaled to $10M,' take calls from customers, walk them through audits, document
- Real-time = harder for AI to replicate; past accomplishments = AI can mimic the claim

## Examples
- B2B prosumer (Erica, Vivian Tu, Dave Ramsey): all show credentials + ongoing knowledge
- Hair extension business: demonstrate product live; don't just say 'I sell extensions'
