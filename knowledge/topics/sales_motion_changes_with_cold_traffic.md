# Sales Motion Changes With Cold Traffic

> Warm organic traffic (high trust) converts at different rate than cold paid traffic; funnel redesign required when shifting acquisition sources

## Key Takeaway
- Warm: challenge format, 5-day launch events convert at high rates
- Cold: requires different motion (one-on-one calls, VSL, nurture sequences)

## Implications
- Current funnel tuned for warm audience will break under cold traffic
- Must rebuild sales process for different buyer temperature and trust level
