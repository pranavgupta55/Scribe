# Ownership Equity Incentive Structure

> Increasing owner equity incentivizes expansion discipline

## Framework
- Location 1: 33% owner (bought in with dad 67%)
- Location 2: 50-50
- Location 3+: 100% owner

## Why It Works
- Owner benefits increase with each location
- Incentivizes opening multiple locations
- But still requires GM to handle day-to-day
