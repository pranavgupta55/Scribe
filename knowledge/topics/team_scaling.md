# Team Scaling

> Scale from 1-2 salespeople to 6 salespeople with parallel dialer to work 300 daily prospects.

## How-To / Method
- Calculate daily prospects across channels (~300)
- Assign $300/day revenue KPI per salesperson
- 6 people needed to cover volume
- Implement parallel dialer to maximize efficiency

## Key Takeaway
- Team scaling unlocks backend conversion without frontend spend ceiling
- Bigger team makes dialer more efficient (5% pickup rate distributes across more reps)
