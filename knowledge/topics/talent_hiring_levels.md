# Talent Hiring Levels

> Hiring talent is a ladder; each level shifts your perception of what's possible

## Key Numbers
- First $50k employee: revelation that they can read/write
- First $100k employee: game-changer
- First $250k, $500k, $1M: each a new level of capability

## Framework
- "The best talent is always in the future, not behind you"
- You're now graduating into the "who game" at scale

## Why It Works
- Better people do more, faster, with less direction
- Allows founder to focus on vision, not execution
