# Conversion Rate Benchmarks

> Use channel-specific conversion targets to benchmark performance and identify optimization opportunities.

## Key Numbers
- In-person service (meta/local leads): 10% conversion
- Cold webinar leads (broad): 2-3% webinar-to-sales
- Cold webinar (niched): 5% webinar-to-sales
- In-home sales (proper process): 35% prospect-to-close
- Web page: 1-2% (2-4% on trusted platforms like Amazon, 25% for trusted author pages)

## Framework
- Above benchmark = raise price
- Below benchmark = fix sales process/avatar before lowering price
- Platform trust multiplier: Amazon ~12x typical web page conversion
