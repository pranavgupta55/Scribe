# B2B High-Ticket Creators

> B2B creators protected if they provide real proof of expertise; stakes are too high for audiences to trust AI.

## Examples
- Erica Taught Me: attorney background = credible on disputes/refunds
- Vivian Tu: came from finance/Wall Street = credible on investing
- Dave Ramsey: huge business success = credible on financial advice

## Caveats
- Without third-party credibility (exits, revenue, experience), even great content loses out
- Two people giving identical advice; one with 10 sales teams built, one with zero = the experienced one wins by a lot

- B2B prosumer creators (savings, investing, money) require more visible proof; Erica Taught Me is credible as an attorney; Vivian Tu has Wall Street credibility; Dave Ramsey has big business success behind his advice — [the_new_way_of_making_content_in_the_age_of_ai_XsWSvz-aewA.txt]
