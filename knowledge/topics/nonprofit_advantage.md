# Nonprofit Advantage

> Nonprofit model is key credibility differentiator vs for-profit brokers

## Key Takeaway
- For-profit brokers get 5–10% commission to convince clients to overpay
- Nonprofit gets 20% cut on savings generated; aligned with client success

## Why It Works
- Removes perverse incentive (selling more expensive, bad plans)
- Aligns founder with customer outcome

- Health benefits nonprofit at $3M; founder worked at Berkshire Hathaway and P&G; health insurance industry is broken — [sounds_like_a_scam_pitching_200k_in_savings_to_ceos_Ht9u-qEXTQY.txt]
