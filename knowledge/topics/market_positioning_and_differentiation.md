# Market Positioning and Differentiation

> Compete on offer (quality, speed, reliability, guarantee) not price; premium pricing works only if offer is defensible and perception is aligned with price.

## Key Takeaway
- If competitors sell commodity (indistinguishable), you must sell different—otherwise price competition destroys margins
- Customer perception: if you're 20% above market price, offer must be measurably different (faster, more reliable, guaranteed)

## Examples
- Electrical contractor beating six heating/cooling companies for 15 years but has no marketing leverage; they have all the leverage because contractor is dependent
