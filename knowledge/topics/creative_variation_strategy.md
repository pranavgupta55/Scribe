# Creative Variation Strategy

> Test hundreds of variations weekly to identify winning patterns, then double down on what works rather than constantly inventing new ideas.

## How-To / Method
- Create many variations in beginning phase when winners unknown
- Test different segments with different hooks and creative angles
- Identify winning ads and reuse same hooks for new variations
- Double down by making more variations of winners, not abandoning them

## Why It Works
- New customers enter market daily; existing ad will be first-time view for many
- Prevents audience fatigue by constant rotation of 600+ ads monthly
- Confusion effect: competitors can't identify which ads actually perform

## Key Numbers
- Old Spice ads converted 70% of total market through creative strength alone
- Process scales from 5 ads/month to 600 ads/month
