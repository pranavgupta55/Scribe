# Time vs. Money Earning

> Everyone earns per hour: annual income ÷ 2,000 hours = hourly rate; investors/entrepreneurs do too, just don't denote it explicitly.

## Framework
- Hourly rate = total annual income ÷ 2,000 working hours
- Applies to salaries, projects, investments, all income types

## Examples
- Warren Buffett: analysis + research + 1,000 rejected deals = time on winning deal
- You're not escaping time; you're just not measuring it
