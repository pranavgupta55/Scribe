# Large-Group Training Revenue

> Monetize large-group tier via three conversion attempts spaced 3-6 weeks apart, each positioned as a gift or reward rather than a hard sell.

## Framework
- Day 1 bribe: 'Sign up for membership today and get the entire $600 program free'—some instantly commit, feel like heroes
- Week 3 abandon hope sale: Halfway weigh-in; recap progress, ask about long-term goals, position EFT as continuation—closes 90%+
- Week 6 last chance: Credit weight loss at $25/pound; spread credit over 6-14 months to back into desired $49/week rate; offer maintenance phase

## Examples
- If client lost 13 lbs: $325 credit spread over 6 months = $54/month discount → $250/mo regular rate − $54 = $196/mo ($49/week)
- If client lost 10 lbs: $250 credit over 5 months = $50/month discount → same $196/mo outcome
- Variations in duration make the math work but all roads lead to $49/week—client feels individually negotiated rate
