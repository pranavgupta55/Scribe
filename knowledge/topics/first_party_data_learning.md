# First-Party Data Learning

> Analyze your own sales calls, content, and customer interactions to extract patterns.

## How-To / Method
- Collect lots of sales call recordings not just one
- Study top 10% of calls, content, and customer service interactions
- Find the eyes, feet, whatever the critical details are
- Repeat analysis 100 times then try making next 100 look like top 10

## Why It Works
- First-party data beats all theory
- Real world feedback teaches faster than courses
- People will call you talented when really you just did 10K iterations
