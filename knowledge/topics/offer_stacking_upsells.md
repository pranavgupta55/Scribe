# Offer Stacking & Upsells

> Anchor with $100K offer, then downgrade by removing one specific feature to create $35K mid-tier.

## Framework
- Commit fully to selling $100K; must feel like a real offer, not a throwaway
- When they decline $100K, remove one specific feature (e.g., DJ's origin story)
- Frame downgrade as 90% similar, one-third the price

## Key Numbers
- Current close rate ~20% on $10-30K; expect 30%+ on $35K tier after anchor
