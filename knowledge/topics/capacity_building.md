# Capacity Building

> Being prepared with capacity enables you to recognize and capitalize on opportunities others cannot

## Framework
- Opportunities present to everyone; only those with capacity can recognize AND act
- Capacity = time, money, skills, audience, network, readiness

## Key Takeaway
- Good Samaritan study shows capacity (being early vs late) had 6x impact on behavior vs self-perception

## Implications
- Busy/late people can't recognize opportunities even if morally motivated
- Capacity comes first; opportunity second
