# Premium Pricing Power

> Strong brand enables 10x+ price increase and 6x better advertising response.

## Key Numbers
- Unbranded white t-shirt: $5
- Nike branded white t-shirt: $60 (12x price)
- Generic response: 0.5% click-through
- Nike response: 3% click-through (6x better)
- Both same identical t-shirt

## Why It Works
- Customers pay for association, not product attributes
- Strong brand creates customer willingness to pay premium
