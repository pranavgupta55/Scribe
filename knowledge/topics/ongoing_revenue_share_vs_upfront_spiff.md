# Ongoing Revenue Share vs Upfront Spiff

> Ongoing share (10-15%) more psychologically powerful for repeat referrals than one-time spiff.

## Framework
- Upfront $6-10K: fast initial activation, but one-time incentive
- Ongoing 10-15% on revenue: builds partnership mentality, repeat referrals

## Key Takeaway
- Best affiliates want long-term relationship, not one-off deal
