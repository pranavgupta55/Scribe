## Framework
- 100 Million Scaling Roadmap: stages 1-8 from 0 employees to 500+
- Stage 3 (Stabilized: 1-4 employees) diagnosis: slow buyer velocity, wasted leads, maxed time, unclear priorities, lost-feeling customers

- $480K revenue, $206K profit (43% margins) baseline
- 42 current clients, only 4 churned in 12 months (payment issues only)
- Target: 150 clients → $4.5M–$5M annual revenue (10-11x growth)
