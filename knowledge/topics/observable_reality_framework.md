# Observable Reality Framework

> Define all concepts in observable, measurable behavior; avoid binaries; reality requires many variables and nuance.

## Framework
- Reality exists in observable universe, not abstractions
- Everyone can agree on what they see
- Reality = spectrum, not binary (honest/dishonest, authentic/inauthentic)

## Why It Works
- B.F. Skinner: 'if many variables exist, many variables must be studied'
- Specificity enables precision
- Avoids vague accusations and blame
