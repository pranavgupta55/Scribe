# Capacity Maximization

> Use five micro-levers to serve more clients in the same space without opening a new facility or hiring proportional staff.

## Framework
- Membership levels: Switch from unlimited to 3x/week with Mon/Wed/Fri or Tues/Thurs/Sat options—doubles capacity overnight because attendance was 85% average
- Attendance spread: Ask new signups which session time they want *before* offering their preference; lead with least-booked time to balance load
- Session duration: 45-minute classes back-to-back (instead of 60 min + 15 min break) lets you split packed times; 33% more sessions with same payroll
- Physical space: Partner clients (one rests while one works), blow out office walls, move sales to corner cubicle; frees 4+ slots per session
- Exercise selection: Replace double unders and Olympic lifts with simpler moves (goblet squats, push-ups, KB rows); frees floor space and reduces trainer supervision needed

## Why It Works
- Constraints force innovation: limited membership feels premium, not restrictive
- Partnership = double revenue per square foot with zero added overhead
- Shorter sessions feel 'efficient' not 'short'; clients appreciate time savings
