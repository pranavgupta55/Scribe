# Winning Hooks From Existing Content

> Reuse and repurpose proven hooks from your best-performing assets before creating new ones.

## How-To / Method
- Mine hooks from previous winning ads (easiest, most reliable)
- Extract hooks from high-performing free content (longs, shorts, tweets, emails)
- Save and repurpose hooks from competitors' ads that are broad enough to apply
- Find hooks from others' free content with high view counts in your space

## Key Takeaway
- Winning hooks in one industry often transfer to others
- One good hook can provide significant runway for scale
- Reuse proven winners 80% of the time before expanding to new creative
