# Cohort Analysis & Time-Based Churn

> Churn drops dramatically with tenure; focus on hitting day 90 and month 6 milestones rather than optimizing average monthly churn.

## Key Numbers
- Months 1-3: 20%+ churn (highest)
- Day 90: drops to ~10% or less (50% reduction)
- Month 6: drops to ~2% (5x reduction from months 1-3)

## How-To / Method
- Strategy 1: get people past first month with activation, onboarding, expectation-setting
- Strategy 2: deliver outcome/behavior change by day 90
- Strategy 3: facilitate real connections by month 6

## Caveats
- If business <90 days old, churn is always inflated; don't overhaul based on month 1 numbers
- Don't panic if month 1 churn looks bad unless nearly everyone is leaving
