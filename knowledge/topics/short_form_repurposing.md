# Short-Form Repurposing

> Short-form agency or editor clips moments from long-form without extra writing needed

## Why It Works
- Schedule clippable moments during long-form recording
- Editor makes shorts from natural breakpoints
- Less work than creating separate short content

## Examples
- Brutally Honest Advice: consistently 1M+ views from tweet-based material

## Connections
- Shorts and longs serve different audiences; both necessary but separately optimized strategies. _(connects to: Short Form Vs Long Form Content)_
- Short-form clips funnel audiences to long-form content, which then funnels to books and products. _(connects to: Short Form To Long Form Conversion)_
