# Member Lifecycle Management

> Guide members through four milestones (Connect → Refer → Testify → Ascend) using specific touch points and systems at each stage.

## Framework
- Connect: First 2-4 weeks with gym; personal greeting, swag, OG mentor introduction, daily reach-outs to prevent dropout
- Refer: Member feels connected and vouches for gym; triggered by testimonial request or handwritten card incentive
- Testify: Member leaves Google/Yelp/Facebook review; incentivize with free swag if all three are posted
- Ascend: Member upgrades from large-group to semi-private or adds supplements; marketed via internal plays and recommendations

## Touch Points Per Stage
- Connect: Welcome email, handwritten card, swag on day 1, gym event invite, daily text check-ins (first 5 weeks)
- Refer: Handwritten card with referral incentive, trainer pointing out 'who should be here with you?'
- Testify: Incentive email/text, make-it-easy buttons on phone at end of workout
- Ascend: Internal play invites, one-on-one consultation about 'next level' options, limited-time semi-private discounts
