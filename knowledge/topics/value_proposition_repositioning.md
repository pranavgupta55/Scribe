# Value Proposition Repositioning

> "Save $200k" sounds like scam; "We're nonprofit so we give you the 20% industry profit margin" sounds credible

## Old Frame
- "We'll save you $200k; here's how we do it"; sounds too good to be true

## New Frame
- "Industry average profit margin is 20%; we take 0% and pass that to you"
- More believable because it's anchored to industry reality

## Why It Works
- Nonprofit structure is the credibility engine
- Explains how without sounding like a gimmick
