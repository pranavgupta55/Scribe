# Creative Production

> More winners come from more creative tests, not from optimizing existing ads; volume of ideas beats perfection of execution.

## Examples
- Money Models: 2,000 ads = higher probability of hitting big goal
- Each platform requires different format; TikTok ads ≠ Instagram ads ≠ X ads

## Method
- Create 25-50 ads daily as baseline
- Test across platforms simultaneously
- Track which win; scale those
