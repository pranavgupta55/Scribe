# Identity Shift to Trainer

> Move from selling $500K/month individually to building 80-agent infrastructure

## Framework
- Sales god: How good am I at closing? (ego-driven)
- Sales training god: How good can I make anyone at closing? (system-driven)
- Second identity is what allows $24M business to exist

## Key Takeaway
- No one cares what founder makes. They care that you built infrastructure of 80 agents knocking $30M/year

## Timeline
- Hard-block 25% of time ($1M/year foregone) to reinvest in hiring/training
- Each additional agent you help close 1 more deal = >$1M back in ROI
