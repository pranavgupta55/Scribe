# Grand Opening Strategy

> Grand opening is the only time aggressive marketing is justified

## Framework
- Only open when location can reach full capacity in 6 months on word-of-mouth alone
- Playbook includes overstaffing + ad budget across channels + promotional giveaways

## Caveats
- Premium concepts still do promotions, just exclusive and invite-only
- Grand opening is short burst of marketing, not ongoing strategy
