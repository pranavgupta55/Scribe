# Time and Cash Flow Constraints

> Three primary pain points: slow cash conversion cycle, maxed time on delivery, and unlimited Slack access burning 40% of time.

## Key Problems
- Challenge funnel: spend $19,440 per challenge, take months to recoup acquisition costs
- Slack access: available 24/7 with 24-48 hr response time, consuming ~40% of available time
- Neither problem is a selling point post-purchase; customers expect them but don't cite them as reasons to stay

## Cash Flow Solution: Layaway
- Require $9K down (first quarter) before service delivery begins; offer 3-payment down sell ($3K × 3)
- Fast action bonus: prepay 9K = start immediately + qualify for next in-person event (within 4 months)
- Most buyers will prepay first quarter to lock in event attendance; pulls cash forward 3 months

## Time Solution: Eliminate Slack
- Cut Slack entirely; replace with 2-4 'emergency lifeline' calls per year for genuine 911 moments
- Reframe: daily access trains clients to outsource decisions; quarterly strategy teaches self-reliance
- Frees up 40% time = 168 hours/year per coach
