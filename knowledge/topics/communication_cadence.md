# Communication Cadence

> One big weekly touchpoint (call or post) plus best-of is ideal; people can keep up and it's enough.

## Key Numbers
- Weekly = works (like TV shows; Netflix binge is exception, not norm)
- More than weekly = people revert to ONE best thing anyway

## How-To / Method
- Default: one weekly call + weekly best-of post
- That's all you need to keep community engaged
