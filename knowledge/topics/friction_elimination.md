# Friction Elimination

> Map every action customer must take; systematically delete friction; higher cost to reduce friction = justifies 10x price.

## How-To / Method
- List all actions customer must take toward outcome
- Delete friction point by point
- If needs vendor help, pay them more to prioritize
- More friction reduction = higher price justified

## Why It Works
- Friction reduction costs money (service delivery, vendor priority)
- High-ticket tier funds this cost
- Creates competitive moat (you pay vendors better, they prioritize you)
