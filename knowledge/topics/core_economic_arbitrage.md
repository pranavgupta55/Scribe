# Core Economic Arbitrage

> Business = efficient allocation of resources to get customers and extract value; arbitrage is better efficiency than competitors.

## Framework
- Cost of getting customers (acquisition)
- Value extracted from customers (monetization)
- Gap between them = business health
- Superior throughput on output side = business success
