# Suffering As Fixed Cost

> All life paths have equal suffering; secret is doing things you don't want to get what you want.

## Framework
- Poor suffer, rich suffer, single suffer, married suffer, employees suffer, entrepreneurs suffer
- Flavor of suffering changes but cost is fixed across paths
- Business growing/shrinking/plateauing all hurt

## Key Takeaway
- If three life paths cost same suffering, pick one with biggest upside
- The fix isn't avoiding suffering; it's aiming bigger for same cost

## Why It Works
- You'll tolerate difficulty for meaningful goal, not for bag of chips
- Trade-off calculation: why suffering matters more than how much
