# Early-Stage Motivation and Passion Protection

> Shift focus from external wins to identity construction to survive the low-reward early period.

## Method
- Reframe the metric: 13 views = same as pitching weight loss to 13 people in a room, which was acceptable before
- Focus on who you are becoming as the asset, not just the outcome numbers
- Build milestones into your narrative; celebrate small wins internal to your growth

## Why It Works
- Outcome metrics are too sparse early to sustain motivation
- Identity-based motivation is always available and intrinsic
- Positive reinforcement on character development provides daily wins
