# Wedding Cake Profit Model

> Stack three revenue tiers (large group, semi-private, supplements) with two monetization streams each (icing = front-end cash, cake = recurring) to reach $100k+/month.

## Framework
- Tier 1 icing: 30 × $500 = $15k/month from 6-week challenges
- Tier 1 cake: 243 recurring members × $167 = $40.6k/month (from 30 new sign-ups, 65% conversion, 8% attrition)
- Tier 2 icing: 4 × $2,500 = $10k/month from 12-week semi-private programs
- Tier 2 cake: 32 recurring × $550 = $17.6k/month
- Tier 3 icing: 29 × $200 = $5.8k/month supplement front-end (no conversion, flows to cake)
- Tier 3 cake: 126 subscribers × $200 = $25.2k/month (23% attrition on recurring)

## Key Takeaway
- Icing keeps business afloat during slow seasons; cake provides stable floor
- Stacking buying curves: allow wealthy clients to spend 3-10x more on your service by offering semi-private tier
- Stacking spending wallets: tap fitness, supplements, meal prep, events—each has separate budget in prospect's mind
