# Operations and Margin Compression

> As SMB service businesses grow, margins compress from rising CAC and churn; fix with AI workflows, not more sales

## Key Numbers
- WAAS at $20M revenue: 3.6% EBITDA margin (too many people), should be 7%+
- Reducing headcount 50% with AI workflows: keeps customer delivery same, doubles margin

## Why It Works
- AI workflows amplify people, don't replace them; 2–3x leverage per employee
- Freed margin funds CAC absorbing paid ads expansion without profitability hit

## Caveats
- Founders must be data-first before AI-first; no data = no AI layer effective
- Don't reduce headcount before AI workflows in place; timing matters
