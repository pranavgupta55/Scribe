# Sales Operations & Training

> Trained 300+ phone reps with role-play drills; ensured every rep could concisely pitch both offers and brand alignment.

## How-To / Method
- Train day 1: explain each offer, model citizen behaviors
- Train day 2: role play; reps play customer (creative), staff plays sales
- Goal: every rep comfortable conveying value in brand-aligned way
- Emphasis: concisely explain value, help customer make purchase decision

## Examples
- Two-tier: single book ($100) vs. bundle ($5,998 with workshop/tools)
- Reps could explain: 'you'll feel just how chunky this is' (tactile, real)
