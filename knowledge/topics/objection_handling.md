# Objection Handling

> Use childlike curiosity and genuine interest instead of combative reframes

## Examples
- Objection: 'Husband won't approve'
- Instead of debate: 'Why wouldn't he? Tell me more. Does he want what's best for you? Know you're struggling? Would he prefer you continue struggling?'

## Why It Works
- Person doesn't feel attacked/defended against
- They come to own conclusions
- Removes confrontation fear that blocks sales
