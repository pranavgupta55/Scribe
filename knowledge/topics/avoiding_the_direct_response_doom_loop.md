# Avoiding the Direct Response Doom Loop

> The doom loop: revenue ↑ → margins ↓ → force perpetual selling → run large nonprofit with no buffer.

## Key Numbers
- Typical collapse point: $12M revenue, 7% margins
- Signs: cash crunches, supply chain burden, team-dependent, unsellable business

## Common Mistakes
- Scaling 4-5 product portfolio simultaneously; ends up as 4 mini-businesses cannibalizing same customer base
- Continuing to scale losing SKUs instead of focusing on the clear winner

## How-To / Method
- At $12M + 7% margins, identify your one clear-winning product (carrying all the losers)
- Kill the losers; reinvest 100% into brand-building the winner
- Shift from media arbitrage to brand + affiliate recruitment (influencers who believe in product)
