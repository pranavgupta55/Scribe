# Hedonic Adaptation

> Subjective well-being doesn't change drastically; we're wired to find problems so solving one doesn't make us permanently happier.

## Key Takeaway
- People think solving problem will make them happy; it won't
- Our brains evolved to find threats and problems
- We're kept stressed by constant problem-seeking

## Framework
- Unhappiness and anxiety move us forward through innovation
- We think when we solve this we'll be happy
- We actually just find next problem
