# Attribution Tracking and Input-Output Equations

> Define core revenue drivers or neither you nor employees will know how to scale

## Why It Works
- Founders lacking attribution don't know if $1 paid becomes $5 or $20 returned
- Employees can't execute on unknown priorities; founder clarity cascades down

## How-To / Method
- Implement pixel/tracking on paid ads (Meta, Google) to measure cost-per-lead
- Tie lead source to revenue outcome (referral, content, paid) with CRM data

## Claims
- Without attribution tracking, businesses cannot define the input-output equation; employees don't know what core actions increase revenue if the founder doesn't know it. — [watch_this_if_you_have_a_service_business_jqo0lVveh98.txt]
