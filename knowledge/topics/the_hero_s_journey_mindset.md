# The Hero's Journey Mindset

> Nobody tells stories about heroes with no hardships; reframe yourself as the hero navigating dragons.

## Framework
- Joe Rogan's rule: imagine you're in a movie and you're the hero—what would that guy do?
- The speaker's razor: whatever will be the most epic story is what you'll most likely do
- Apply it to major decisions: sell company, marry, live frugally, fly around doing turnarounds

## Examples
- Documenting journey from beginning to billionaire (wanting to see 1997 Amazon content)
- Making 5-view videos knowing they'll be watched later when you've made it
- Living the long saga rather than taking shortcuts for immediate victory
